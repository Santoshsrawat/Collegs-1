Top Medical Colleges in Uttar Pradesh 

Team getmyuniversity 05 Apr 2023 2554 views

India is renowned for hosting some of the most prestigious universities in the world, which have nurtured countless eminent scholars, scientists, and doctors. Many Indian states have some of the top medical colleges that have world-class infrastructure, top faculties, and a good reputation. They have all those essentials that can enhance and give a major boost to your medical career.

While pursuing MBBS in India can be an exceptional choice. When we talk about the best state for medical colleges, we can not miss the Top medical colleges in Uttar Pradesh in 2023. Uttar Pradesh is a state of immense significance in the history and culture of India. With a population of over 240 million people, and the fourth largest in terms of area. This state is one of the best states known for its medical colleges, also the importance and reputation of education in Uttar Pradesh have always been the highest.

While it is important to note that there are still some institutions that require further improvements in their infrastructure to provide students with better education. Whereas, top government medical colleges in Uttar Pradesh Require some changes, and private UP medical colleges too. These colleges may lack adequate facilities, equipment, or resources to provide students with the necessary practical training and exposure to medical practice. 

Therefore, it is crucial for aspiring medical students to thoroughly research the institutions they plan to attend and ensure that they offer quality education and proper infrastructure to meet their academic and professional goals.

One of the benefits of pursuing medical education in Uttar Pradesh is that students who scored below 200 marks as well as those who scored more than 500 marks in their entrance exams can easily secure admission to the state's top medical colleges. This provides a unique opportunity for students who may have faced difficulty securing admission in other states due to their scores.

We have compiled detailed reviews of all these colleges, providing you with a comprehensive analytical view that can help you make an informed decision about your educational pursuits. From fees to infrastructure and student facilities, these colleges are well-equipped to offer an immersive learning experience that enables you to focus solely on your studies.

Why Choose Top Medical Colleges In Uttar Pradesh?

Quality Education: Many of these colleges have well-equipped labs, hospitals, and experienced faculty members who provide comprehensive training to the students. But, there are still some colleges that require improvement in the area of Infrastructure, medical training, and clinical exposure.

Clinical Exposure: Clinical exposure is invaluable for medical students. It helps them acclimatize to clinical environments, develop professionally, interact confidently with patients, and develop self-reflection. Clinical exposure is a must for medical students but somehow its lack in the Uttar Pradesh walk-in patient is low compared to the southern state. 

Affordable Fees: Medical education can be quite expensive, but many colleges in Uttar Pradesh offer education at a relatively lower cost compared to other states in India. This makes medical education accessible to a larger section of society.

Good Job Opportunities: Uttar Pradesh has a large number of hospitals and healthcare institutions, which provide ample job opportunities to medical professionals. Many of these hospitals are well-known for their quality healthcare services and are considered top employers in the medical field.

Overall, the combination of quality education, affordable fees, and good job opportunities make Uttar Pradesh an attractive destination for medical education in India. We are going to solve the queries that many of the students have, such as which are the top 10 best medical colleges in Uttar Pradesh.

1. Shri Ram Murti Smarak College- Bareilly

Shri Ram Murti Smarak Trust is a prominent public charitable trust that was established in 1990 to commemorate and honor the memory of the revered freedom fighter, the Late Ram Murti Ji. 

In the year 2008, the Shri Ram Murti Smarak Trust laid the foundation of a self-financed medical institute, which is currently managed and run by the trust in Bareilly, Uttar Pradesh. 

The college has consistently demonstrated its commitment to merit and quality by achieving a respectable position among all parallel private and government medical institutes in the region.

Key Objective

  • Good existing scientific literature 
  • Vast topic to enhance students' critical thinking

This college has a dedicated curriculum that thoroughly focuses on the overall development of medical students. 

Furthermore, the college has been selected as a participant in the TEQIP program of the National Project Implementation Unit. World Bank to finance India's project after seeing the potential of becoming the Top medical college in Uttar Pradesh.

SRMS College is dedicated to promoting education and healthcare services in the region, with a strong focus on empowering the underprivileged sections of society.

2. ERA’s Lucknow Medical College - Lucknow

Established in 1997, Era's Lucknow Medical College is a privately-owned medical college located in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, India. The college was previously affiliated with Dr. Ram Manohar Lohia Avadh University until 2016, after which it became affiliated with Era University.

ELMC also has a well-established hospital on campus, which provides medical services to the community and serves as a training ground for students to gain practical experience in clinical settings. ERA’s college has made required changes in its infrastructure to make its name among the Top medical colleges in Uttar Pradesh. 

This college has state-of-the-art equipment and facilities, and a team of highly skilled medical professionals who provide round-the-clock medical care to patients. That is why it is considered to be one of the best medical colleges in UP.

Key Objective

  • Best Science literature 
  • Good and renowned Journals
  • Best future direction in the research field

ELMC is one of the top 4.3% of medical colleges in India for advanced medical research. Era's Journal of Medical Research (EJMR), a multidisciplinary journal, is published every two years by Era's Research cell, empowered by international and national research collaborations. 

More than 1622 research articles have been published in national and international journals since 2009. With ample research and data supporting its proficiency in providing top-notch clinical education, this college stands out as an excellent choice for students seeking a strong foundation in the medical field.

3. Subharti Medical College- Meerut

Subharti Medical College is a private medical institution located in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, India. It was established in 2001 and is affiliated with Swami Vivekanand Subharti University. 

Key Objective

  • Quality Medical Education
  • Compassionate healthcare professional 
  • Overall development of students

This college is recognized by the Medical Council of India and offers undergraduate and postgraduate medical programs in various specialties. It is equipped with modern infrastructure, including well-equipped laboratories, a library with a vast collection of medical literature, and a hospital on campus that make it the best example of a Top medical college in Uttar Pradesh. 

The college has a team of highly qualified and experienced faculty members who are dedicated to providing quality education and training to students.

4. School of Medical Science Sharda- Greater Noida

As we all know how prestigious Sharda University is and the level of education they provide is really exceptional. With a faculty of renowned scholars and access to state-of-the-art facilities and resources, Sharda University might be a top choice for students seeking a transformative educational experience.

Key Objective

  • Subject that infuses ethical responsibility in future doctors
  • Lifelong learning to build super-minded doctor
  • Perfectly designed curriculum

As a medical school, the School of Medical Sciences and Research (SMSR) is primarily in charge of providing top-notch medical education to MBBS and postgraduate students. When considering the top medical colleges in Uttar Pradesh, this particular college is the first one that comes to the minds of all individuals seeking admission to an MBBS college.

SMSR is prioritized to create a truly global model for medical education and assist doctors with advanced and comprehensive medical care capabilities, who are skilled at working in clinical conditions, mainly aim in providing solid practical skills, and can play active roles in the healthcare industry.

5. Hind Institute of Medical Science- Barabanki

Hind Institute of Medical Sciences (HIMS) is a private medical college and hospital located in Barabanki, Uttar Pradesh, India. It was established in the year 2008 and is affiliated with Dr. Ram Manohar Lohia Avadh University, Faizabad. 

The college is recognized by the Medical Council of India (MCI) and offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in medical science. The MCI is the body of the Indian Government that ensures smooth admission to the best MBBS colleges in Uttar Pradesh. 

The college also has well-equipped laboratories, museums, and a hospital with state-of-the-art facilities for practical training and clinical exposure.

Hind Institute of Medical Sciences aims to provide quality medical education and healthcare services to the community and to produce competent and compassionate healthcare professionals who can become valuable doctors.

6. Teerthanker Mahaveer- Moradabad

Teerthanker Mahaveer University (TMU) is a private university located in Moradabad, Uttar Pradesh, India. It was established in the year 2008 by the Teerthanker Mahaveer Education and Research Foundation. 

The university is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various fields of study including engineering, management, law, education, pharmacy, nursing, journalism, and healthcare.

The campus of TMU is spread over an area of 140 acres and is equipped with modern infrastructure and facilities. Every medical student seeking admission in Top medical college in Uttar Pradesh, can give this college a try as it has one of the best infra. The university has state-of-the-art classrooms, laboratories, libraries, auditoriums, sports facilities, and hostels for students. The campus is Wi-Fi enabled and has a vibrant and diverse student community from across the country.

The university has a strong placement record with several leading companies visiting the campus for recruitment. TMU aims to provide holistic education and prepare students to become responsible and ethical professionals who can contribute to the welfare of society.

7. Rohilkhand Medical College - Bareilly

Rohilkhand Medical College & Hospital is located in the heart of Bareilly, a city in the northern Indian state of Uttar Pradesh. The college is situated at a distance of approximately 255 kilometers from Delhi, the capital city of India. It takes around 4-5 hours by road to travel from Delhi to Bareilly.

The college is situated on a vast expanse of 42 acres, covered with abundant greenery. The campus offers comfortable residential accommodations for faculty members and separate hostel facilities for male and female students, all of which are located within the campus premises. 

A well-built residential complex for faculty and a separate hostel for boys & girls are available within the campus. The campus gives students a 24/7 water supply, electricity supply, and all other essential amenities. All these facilities are enough to make it the Top medical college in Uttar Pradesh and provide exceptional facilities to students. 

8. Muzzafarnagar Medical College

Muzaffarnagar Medical College is a private medical college located in Muzaffarnagar, Uttar Pradesh, India. The college was established in 2006 and is affiliated with Chaudhary Charan Singh University, Meerut. The college offers undergraduate (MBBS) and postgraduate (MD/MS) programs in various specialties.

Muzaffarnagar Medical College is recognized by the Medical Council of India (MCI) and is also approved by the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Government of India. The reason for its approval is the college's latest ongoing efforts to reach the stage of Top medical college in Uttar Pradesh. The college is known for its quality education, experienced faculty, and excellent clinical training facilities.

Key Objective

  • Recognize by the Medical Council of India
  • Quality Education
  • Excellent clinical training facilities

The college has a 750 bedded multi-specialty hospital with state-of-the-art infrastructure and facilities. It also has well-equipped laboratories, classrooms, lecture halls, and a library with a vast collection of books, journals, and online resources.

9. Heritage Institute of Medical Science

Heritage Institute of Medical Sciences (HIMS) is a private medical college located in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, India. It was established in the year 2015 and is affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), Lucknow. The college is recognized by the Medical Council of India (MCI) and offers undergraduate (MBBS) and postgraduate (MD/MS) programs in various specialties.

Key Speciality 

  • Packages that are all-inclusive and crystal clear at an affordable price.
  • Famous surgeons specialize in various areas such as cardiothoracic, neurosurgery, plastic surgery, and general surgery.
  • Top- Grade Clinical Exposure

Heritage Institute of Medical Sciences is known for its excellent faculty, quality education, and excellent clinical training facilities. The college provides a conducive learning environment that enables students to develop their knowledge, and skills, from one of the best MBBS colleges in Uttar Pradesh and competencies in the field of medicine.

10. Rama Medical College - Kanpur 

Rama Medical College - Hospital Research Centre was established in 2008 at Kanpur (U.P.) by Rama Educational Society, Kanpur. Dr. B. S. Kushah served as its first chairman. The Rama Medical College-Hospital & Research Center, located in Kanpur, is considered one of the top medical schools in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

Aside from clean air and water, Rama medical college also has a 24-hour transportation system like buses and trains, and it's 21 kilometers from KANPUR airport. On its campus of over 70 acres, the Medical College has more than 25 acres of land.

Key Speciality

  • Proper Infrastructure to support the best clinical exposure
  • Extremely beautiful campus
  • Top Quality Facilities

One of the major advantages of Rama Medical College in Kanpur is its well-equipped infrastructure, including the latest tools, equipment, essential books, and a comprehensive syllabus that enable students to gain practical experience and exposure to the clinical aspects of medicine. This makes Rama Medical College a Top medical college in Uttar Pradesh and a great choice for those seeking a top-notch MBBS college.
