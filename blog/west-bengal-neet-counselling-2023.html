Introduction

Department of Health and Family Welfare, Kolkata, conducts MBBS counselling for West Bengal. Admission is determined by a student's NEET score. Therefore, passing NEET 2023 is crucial. There tend to be two rounds of counselling in West Bengal NEET MBBS counselling. If seats are still available, a third round known as the mop-up is also held.

In order to participate in West Bengal NEET Counselling 2023, you must fill out the West Bengal MBBS application form 2023. Read the entire blog to obtain the necessary understanding of WB NEET MBBS Counselling 2023 and learn how the process is carried out.

West Bengal NEET Counselling 2023 Registration Process

Step 1: West Bengal NEET Counselling 2023 Registration

Every medical aspirant is first required to register on the official website, https://wbmcc.nic.in/

and complete the necessary payment. After then, students can log in and review the seating chart to make their selections.

Step 2: Fill the West Bengal NEET Counselling 2023 form

After creating id and password, student is required to fill the West Bengal NEET Counselling form and take a print out of the same and pay the fees accordingly.

The authorities will publish the seat vacancy details for each round of counseling. A merit list will also be published based on the NEET score and other eligibility criteria. 

Step 3: Upload

When applying for West Bengal NEET 2023, some of the documents that will be required to submit are as follows :-

  • NEET Admit Card
  • Rank Card
  • 12th marksheet
  • Signature of student and parents
  • Thumb impression of student

Step 4: Download the verification slip

When all the documents are successfully submitted and payment is made then, with the verification slip downloaded a medical student can begin the choice filling process.

Step 5: Choice filling and locking

Students can start filling out the choices once the results of the mock test are announced. Since there can be no additions once the selections have been made, it is a crucial step that calls for wise decision-making. Rearranging or deleting is permitted, though.

Step 6: Reporting

After the declaration of result, make the payment and report to the respective institute.

Salient Features of West Bengal NEET Counselling 2023

  • The process of document verification would start before the seat allocation in the WEST BENGAL NEET MBBS counselling.
  • It is mandatory for the candidates to lock their selections.
  • New registration will be made available for each counselling session.
  • Candidates who don't show up for admission after receiving a seat in the first round of counselling will be eliminated.
  • The relevant colleges will not be the location of the document verification for NRI-allocated seats; instead, it would take place at Swasthya Bhavan.
  • West Bengal's private medical schools reserve the right to request a bond and bank guarantee at the time of reporting.

Eligibility Criteria for West Bengal UG NEET Counselling 2023

The following requirements must be met in order to be eligible to apply for West Bengal NEET Counselling 2023:-

  • Nationality

A medical aspirant must be a citizen of India in order to apply for West Bengal NEET Counselling. Also, NRI aspirants with Indian passports can also apply.

  •  Age Limit

For West Bengal NEET Counselling 2023, the minimum age requirement is 17 years old (as of December 31).

  • Academic Qualification

A student should have cleared 10+2 exam with a minimum of 50% marks in Physics, Chemistry, Biology and English as their core subjects from a recognized board. However, for candidates belonging to SC/ST categories, the minimum percentage required in 40% and 45% for physically disable aspirants.

  • Domicile

Candidates must have a residence or domicile certificate stating that they have lived in West Bengal for at least 10 years in order to apply for WBMCC NEET Counselling 2023. For applicants who have passed the higher secondary examination in or earlier and have lived in West Bengal for the previous ten years, a certificate of residence is required. For applicants who have not lived in West Bengal for a continuous period of ten years or more but whose parents are permanent residents of West Bengal with a home address in West Bengal and have lived in West Bengal for a continuous period of ten years or more, a residence/domicile certificate is required.

  • Non- Domicile

Non-domicile candidates can only participate for management quota seats in Private Medical Colleges in West Bengal.

What was the last year West Bengal NEET Counselling Cut off 2022?

For NEET 2022, the general category cutoff score was 50%, the PH category cutoff was 45%, and the reserved category cutoff was 40%. NEET Cut-off 2023 will be made public by the NTA following the 7th May 2023 NEET exam.

Is WB NEET 2023 Counselling Offline for MBBS?

WB NEET 2023 Counselling process is conducted in three rounds. Where, the first two rounds are conducted online and the third round i.e. West Bengal NEET mop up round is through offline mode.

Is West Bengal an open state for MBBS NEET Counselling?

Yes, West Bengal is an open state for MBBS NEET Counselling. Other State students are only eligible for management quota seats in Private medical college in West Bengal.

When WBMCC counselling will start?

West Bengal NEET UG Counselling 2023 starts after the declaration of NEET entrance exam result.

Is there any chance of medical seats with NEET score of 450 in West Bengal?

A student can easily get into a good medical college with 450 marks, which is a sufficient and good score.
