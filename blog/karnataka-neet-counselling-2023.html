Karnataka NEET Counselling 2023 Admission Process 

After completing the registration process on the KEA website, the student must then complete the following steps to begin the Karnataka NEET Counselling Process:-

STEP 1: Register on the official website of KEA i.e. kea.kar.nic.in and then login using ID and password.

STEP 2: Fill the Karnataka NEET Counselling 2023 application form and don’t forget to take a print out of online application by paying the fees

STEP 3: Upload the required set of documents such as NEET Admit Card, rank card, 12th marksheet, signature of parents and student as well as thumb impression of student along with his or her photograph

STEP 4: After uploading all the documents, secret key will be given which a student will be required to download document verification slip.

STEP 5: With the downloaded document verification slip, a medical aspirant can then start the process of choice filling online.

STEP 6: After declaration of mock test, choices made can be either reshuffled or deleted but cannot be added.

STEP 7: And when the final result is declared, student can then proceed to pay fee.

STEP 8: The last step is Reporting. After the Karnataka NEET first round counselling results, student should report to the Karnataka Examination Authority before going to the college.

Important Part of Karnataka NEET Counselling 2023

Choice Filling

The most important part, choice filling, must be done carefully because the method used in Karnataka is considerably different. As, a student while filling the choices, can either rearrange or eliminate their college preference, but cannot add any further names. This makes the process somewhat difficult for students.

Thus, an expert with experience in this field can assist a medical student in making the right decision. With ten years of expertise helping medical students get admitted, team Get My University can help, one that you won't regret and for which you'll be grateful after you're admitted.

Choice after Allotment of Seat
Choice 1: If the student is happy with their allotment, they can choose option 1 and proceed by paying the required fees, providing the required documentation, downloading the admission order, and then reporting to the college. One must keep in mind that the student who received an allocation would then lose their eligibility for subsequent counselling sessions.
Choice 2: Choose Choice 2 if you were given the allotment but are still looking for other alternatives. Students may keep their round one seat and search for a better one in round two. Therefore, even if the student chooses Option 2, they will still be protected because they have the seat that was already assigned to them in first round.
Choice 3: The student will have to give up the seat they were given in Round 2 if they are still unsatisfied and want to take part in the subsequent round.  Additionally, the already-completed decision cannot be modified.
Choice 4: You can opt for this choice, if you have been allotted seat somewhere else and do not wants to participate in a further round of counselling.

 

NOTE :

  • Availability of Free Exit in Round 1 and Round 2
  • There is no requirement of security deposit in Karnataka NEET Counselling 2023 application

Eligibility Criteria for Karnataka NEET Counselling 2023

The following eligibility requirements must be met by potential medical students: -

  • Medical aspirant must be a citizen of India
  • Students should have scored minimum of 50% percent (40% percent for SC/ST/ OBC) in 12th class with main subjects physics, chemistry, and biology.
  • For Karnataka medical counselling registration, every medical student is required to register on the official website of Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA).
  • Medical aspirant must have qualified NEET entrance exam.

Why MBBS from Karnataka?

Not just for MBBS but also for PG degrees (MD, MS, MCH)  Karnataka is a demanding destination.

The reason for this is the reasonable cost, enough seating, and excellent clinical exposure. In a nutshell, Karnataka satisfies all of a student's wishes and results in overall grooming of the medical aspirant.

Karnataka, the sixth-largest state in terms of area in India, is home to a variety of medical colleges where students can find top-notch amenities, a large number of patients, and most importantly, reputable medical institutions.

And the best part is that if a student prefers to pursue MBBS from Karnataka then they get reservation of Internal quota while pursuing PG as well.

Is NEET exam compulsory to participate in Karnataka NEET Counselling 2023?

Yes, passing the NEET exam is a must to take part in the Karnataka NEET Counselling 2023.

Which college is the best option available at a low NEET UG score?

For medical aspirants looking for a college open with a low score, Siddaganga Medical College is the ideal choice.

What are the top 5 Private Medical Colleges in Karnataka?

Top 5 Private medical Colleges in Karnataka include Kasturba Medical College, St. John's Medical College, MS Ramaiah Medical College, Kempegowda Institute of Medical Science and J.J.M Medical College.

What is the safe score for NEET 2023 Karnataka?

For General category students, NEET cut off score ranges between 440-450. Overall, for general category minimum of 50% and for other category it is 40th.

Is Karnataka an open state for NEET Counselling?

Yes, Karnataka is an open state and offers non domicile students also an opportunity to study in top colleges of Karnataka.

Majority of medical colleges in Karnataka offers an affordable budget that ranges only between Rs. 50-60 Lakhs.
