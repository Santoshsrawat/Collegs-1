Why Manipal University Karnataka?

A globally oriented institution, Manipal Academy of Higher Education has partnerships in place with more than 250 top universities all over the world. Faculty and students have access to top-notch institutions, experiences, and resources due to their connections with institutions, government agencies, and organizations in study, research, and service.

Over the years, other institutions have been added, and in June 1993, the Indian government officially recognised this group of institutions as a Deemed to be University. As a result of its outstanding record in academics, infrastructure, and research achievements as well as its status in national and worldwide rankings, the Ministry of Education, Government of India, designated it as an Institution of Eminence Deemed to be University (IoE) in October 2020.

Understanding the Direct Admission Process

Direct admission is a streamlined process that allows students to secure a seat in the Manipal Academy of Higher Education. This option is beneficial for students who have excelled in their previous academic endeavors or possess exceptional talent in a specific field. 

Direct admission in Manipal Academy of Higher Education offers a quicker and more straightforward pathway to pursuing your desired course, eliminating the stress and uncertainty associated with entrance exams. By choosing direct admission in MAHE through management quota, you can focus on enhancing your skills and preparing for the academic challenges that you may face ahead in future.

Manipal University Campuses in India 2023

In 1957, the initial year of Manipal, there was only program offered that was civil engineering. But gradually with time, from 1967 onwards it started to expand and increased two more courses namely Mechanical and Electrical.

As Manipal University grew, as the college added 23 more engineering courses between 1961 and 2023. The most well-liked of all of them is computer science and IT. Manipal University has a total of 8 campuses, including those in Antigua, Dubai, Malaysia, Jaipur, and Sikkim. Other campuses include those in Bangalore, Mangalore, Malaysia, and Dubai. There are eight campuses total, however the four most well-known campuses offering engineering program are Karnataka, Jaipur, Sikkim, and Bangalore.

Eligibility Criteria for Direct Admission in Manipal Academy of Higher Education

1.    Age – Aspirant planning to pursue B.Tech and interested in BTech Direct Admission in Manipal Academy of Higher Education, then he or she must be at least 16 years old.

2.    Academic Qualification - The academic qualification for admission in B.Tech course is candidate should obtain at least 50% in higher secondary board exam. Physics, chemistry and maths should be as main subjects in 12th board.

3.    MET - Manipal University Entrance test is mandatory to be qualified by the candidate.

About Manipal Entrance Test (MET)

MET stands for Manipal Entrance Test (MET) which is mandatory to clear to get admission in Manipal Academy of Higher Education into various UG and PG programmes.
MET Exam is a university level exam which has a duration of 2 hours 18 mins. It is conducted twice in a year, once in between 28th to 30 April and second attempt in the month of many between 28-28th May.

Manipal University Scholarships.

National Scholarship Portal (NSP), Govt of India provides a common electronic portal for implementing various scholarships schemes launched by Union Government, State Government and Union Territories across the country.

State Scholarship Portal (SSP), Govt of Karnataka is a state portal that covers all the scholarship schemes offered by the Karnataka state government, to provide financial aid for the education of the students of the underprivileged sections. SSP portal is a single application portal for all the pre-matric and post-matric scholarships provided by the different departments of Karnataka State.
