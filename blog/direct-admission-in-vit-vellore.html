Direct Admission in VIT Vellore 2023

Direct admission in VIT Vellore is a way for deserving students to secure a seat in their desired engineering program. This option is available to students who have excelled in their class 12 board examinations or equivalent. The direct admission process in VIT Vellore is highly competitive, as there are a limited number of seats available. Therefore, it is essential to understand the eligibility criteria and the application process to increase your chances of securing a spot in VIT Vellore.

Eligibility Criteria for Direct Admission in VIT Vellore

To be eligible for getting direct admission in VIT Vellore, candidates must meet the following criteria:

1. The candidate belonging to general category must have cleared their class 12 board examination or equivalent with a minimum aggregate percentage of 60% in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics/Biology. 

*Candidate belongs to SC/ST hailing from Jammu & Kashmir/ Ladakh and the Northeastern states of Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland, Sikkim and Tripura, scoring to 50% in Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics/Biology is required.

2. The candidate must have studied Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics/Biology as compulsory subjects in their class 12 board examination.

3. Applicant whose date of birth falls on or after 1st July 2001 are eligible to apply for direct admission in VIT Vellore.

4. Applicant should be a Resident/Non-Resident Indian National/ROI. However, NRI applicants can directly apply under “NRI category” through application form. Similarly for foreign applicants who studied or studying abroad can apply directly through international application form.

VIT Vellore Selection Criteria

•    On the basis of rank a candidate secures after giving CBT (Computer Based Test) VITEEE 2023, selection is done.
•    With a rank, student can participate in online counselling and can select the campus, program and category of fees. However, allotment will be made on the basis of rank obtained and the options available at that point of time.
•    Students having rank upto 1 Lakh are eligible for counselling to Vellore and Chennai campus.
•    After making the payment of VIT Vellore Admission fees, VIT will check the eligibility criteria.
•    Every student is required to present documents on the day of coming to campus or as and when asked by the office of Admissions.

NOTE – Provisional Admission offered during counselling will be made provided to a student only when the eligibility criteria is fulfilled. 

VIT Vellore Admission Process

The application process for direct admission in VIT Vellore is straightforward. Here are the steps you need to follow:

1.    Submission of VITEEE Application through web by applicants at viteee.ac.in
2.    Application Verification & Scrutiny
3.    General link for test slot booking to the candidates
4.    Test Slot, center booking and admit card generation 
5.    Appear for VITEEE 2023
6.    VITEEE 2023 Result Announcement
7.    Announcement of Online Counselling based on VITEEE 2023 Rank
8.    Selection of Program
9.    Provisional Admission Letter to B.Tech Programme based on merit
10.    Document verification

After submitting the application form, keep a copy of the confirmation page and any other relevant documents for future reference. It is also advisable to regularly check the official website for updates and notifications regarding the admission process.

About VITEEE

For direct admission in VIT through management quota, the Vellore Institute of Technology Engineering Entrance Examination (VITEEE) is held. The VITEEE will be held at specific locations throughout India and abroad between April 15 and April 21, 2023 (tentative). The test will last for 2 hours and 30 minutes. For the VITEEE, candidates can only appear once for VITEEE. There will be multiple choice questions, with one mark awarded for the correct response and zero for the incorrect answer. There will be a total of 125 questions, 40 of which will be in the math/biology portion. The remaining 35 questions will be in physics, 35 in chemistry, 10 in aptitude, and 5 in English.

The question paper will be in English only. The cost towards application and subsequent counselling is non-refundable. The detailed syllabus is available at www.viteee.vit.ac.in.

Culture and Fests at VIT Vellore

Since its initial iteration in 2002, Riviera, a cultural and athletic festival, has been held at the VIT Vellore campus. The festival is primarily run by the institute's students and includes a variety of noteworthy events, including sporting contests, literary readings, gaming tournaments, music and dance competitions, parades celebrating culture, and stage performances.
