Top Medical Colleges in Bihar

Team getmyuniversity 06 Apr 2023 1529 views

Are you considering Bihar as an option to pursue an MBBS degree, but feeling uncertain about what the state has to offer?  Most people have misconceptions about the locality and education quality of Bihar. The initial step for admission to Bihar MBBS College is that you have to appear for the NEET-UG 2023 entrance exam to secure a seat in the Top government MBBS college in Bihar. Bihar has a mix of some good colleges and there are some colleges that really need improvement. They require improvement in terms of facilities and education quality, on the other hand, there are also hidden institutions that offer excellent academic study opportunities.

The quality of education and facilities may depend on one institution to another, but overall, Bihar is a good place to pursue an MBBS degree. A detailed analysis of Get My University about Top Medical Colleges in Bihar will surely help you to find a top-notch college that fits your academic and personal goals.

Bihar is an open state, where students from any state can participate in their counseling process for getting admission into private medical colleges. Bihar has 8 medical colleges offering admission in MBBS colleges. 

There are 2 colleges, which deserve to be called the top medical colleges in Bihar

  1. Katihar Medical College (KMC)
  2. Mata Gujri University 

Narayan medical college, Sasaram should be on your list too, when selecting the right medical college for yourself. This college has made remarkable strides in recent years, showing significant improvements in both its academic programs and patient inflow. With its focus on providing quality medical education and state-of-the-art facilities, the college has emerged as a top medical college in Bihar.

Also, the overall cutoff in these colleges has been relatively low and not that much hard to achieve.  These 3 colleges have also shown good signs of improvement and have a good patient flow that helps students to learn more practically. 

For many aspiring medical students, securing an MBBS seat in the neighboring states of Bihar can often be a tough task. However, in Bihar, the scenario is quite different - students can easily find MBBS seats even with lower scores during the last phase of counseling.

Here are the reason that can help you in selecting the Top medical colleges in Bihar

1. Great opportunity for students with low scores- Bihar has a good number of colleges that have several good reputed medical colleges. This presents a great opportunity for students who may not have secured a seat in their home state or any other state. You have the opportunity to pursue an MBBS in some of the Top medical colleges in Bihar. 

2. Moderate Cost- Compared to other states in India, pursuing an MBBS degree in Bihar is relatively affordable. The cost of education and living expenses in Bihar is generally lower than in many other states, making it a great option for students who want to pursue a quality medical education on a tight budget. The average cost of pursuing MBBS in Bihar could cost around 60 lakh minimum and 90 lakh maximum. Well, this is a moderate budget if you want to study from any of the Top medical colleges in Bihar. 

3. Clinical Exposure-  Bihar offers excellent clinical exposure to medical students, thanks to its diverse patient population and the presence of several reputed medical institutions. Students pursuing an MBBS degree in Bihar get the opportunity to work with a variety of patients, from rural populations to urban centers, exposing them to a wide range of medical conditions and healthcare challenges. 

4. Inclusive approach to medical education- Bihar's medical institutions have an inclusive approach to medical education, ensuring that every student gets the best MBBS education, whether they are paying huge amounts or a moderate cost. That makes it a great option for students.

5. Fascinating cultural heritage- Bihar's rich cultural heritage and history make it a truly captivating place to live and study, offering students an opportunity to immerse themselves in the region's unique traditions. Bihar offers a treasure trove of experiences that students can explore in their free time, making their college years not just academically fulfilling, but personally enriching as well.  

6. Easy to evolve- Students from the northern region of India will find it easy to collaborate and connect with the people and culture of Bihar, creating a welcoming and inclusive environment that fosters personal growth and learning.  We have seen that some of the students find it hard to evolve.

Quick details about the best medical colleges in Bihar

 

1. Katihar Medical College

The College is affiliated with the Bhupendra Narayan University and approved by the National Medical Commission. The Medical College aims to provide high-quality education and an excellent atmosphere for the students in medical education. Katihar Medical College is committed to providing the highest quality of education and training to its students that make it, Top medical College in Bihar.  This college facility includes a state-of-the-art library that is well-stocked with a vast collection of medical books, journals, and other reference materials.

The library is one of the most impressive facilities on campus, with a wide range of resources available to students. Library covers an area of 17,500 square feet, houses over 16000 books and has subsca ription to 51 Indian and 57 foreign journals. 

Katihar Medical College provides an inclusive and accessible learning environment for students who may not have scored high on the NEET exam, with admission opportunities available even for those who scored under 200.

2. Mata Gujri University

Mata Gujri Memorial Medical College Kishanganj is a good college. Commonly referred to as MGMMC Kishanganj, it was established in 1990 and has since been offering an exceptional education in the field of medicine. The college has emerged as one of the Top medical colleges in Bihar, renowned for its academic excellence, infrastructure, and top-quality faculty.

The college is affiliated with the B. N Mandal University and approved by the National Medical Commission. It is located in Kishanganj and has been focusing on making a good quality doctor out of every student. The infrastructure of Mata Gujri University is exceptional and committed to the overall development of the students.

3. Narayan Medical College

Narayan Medical College (NMC) is a private medical college located in Sasaram, Bihar, India. The college was established in 2008 and is affiliated with Aryabhatta Knowledge University, Patna. NMC offers undergraduate courses in medicine (MBBS) and postgraduate courses in various specialized courses. 

The campus of Narayan Medical College is spread over 52 acres of land and includes academic blocks, administrative blocks, lecture halls, laboratories, a library, an auditorium, sports facilities, hostels for students, and residential quarters for faculty and staff.

The college has a team of experienced and dedicated faculty members who use modern teaching methods and technology to impart knowledge to students. All these reasons are enough to tell why NMC is on our list of Top medical colleges in Bihar.

Conclusion

As we have already mentioned, Bihar has a mix of some good colleges and there are some colleges that really need improvement. Well, it’s not that means you don't even consider Bihar or not put it in your list of desired states for MBBS study. 

Here, are numerous Top medical colleges in Bihar. That is doing great in making Bihar one of the best states for Admission to MBBS college. 

Although there is room for improvement in terms of facilities and education quality, it is a fact that many medical colleges in Bihar are dedicated to providing their students with the best possible education. In addition, there are other colleges in Bihar that are committed to ensuring that their students receive excellent academic training.

The Procedure for admission to Bihar MBBS colleges is straightforward and accessible online. However, it is important to note that there is a requirement for document verification, which must be done in person at the respective college where the seat has been allotted to the candidate.
