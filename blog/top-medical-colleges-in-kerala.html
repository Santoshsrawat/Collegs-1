Top Medical Colleges In Kerala 

Team getmyuniversity 06 Apr 2023 1733 views

In today's day and age, attending a reputable medical college is a crucial factor for a successful medical education. Therefore, selecting a medical college that aligns with your budget and academic goals is imperative to ensure a rewarding and fulfilling learning experience. 

Each year, numerous Indian students aspire to pursue a career in medicine and pursue an MBBS degree. However, many of them lack clarity on the admission requirements for a top MBBS college in India and for which state they should go for their study.

This article thoroughly examines the top medical colleges in Kerala, providing readers with reliable and precise information, gathered through extensive research and on-site visits to each college campus.

Kerala is considered one of the best places to study MBBS due to its excellent medical education system, experienced faculty, well-equipped infrastructure, research opportunities, and emphasis on practical learning, as well as its serene environment and vibrant cultural diversity. Kerala has a total number of 19 private colleges that have 2500 MBBS seats.

From 2019 admission, they have started taking non-Keralite students to pursue MBBS, Number of students from the Northern part is gradually increasing. Currently, any student, regardless of their residing state, is eligible to seek admission in Kerala based on their academic performance, unlike in the past when such opportunities were limited. Also, 15% of the seats are available for open admission. 

Facilities and Qualities Top Private Medical Colleges in Kerala Provides -

  • Quality Education: Kerala is known for its high-quality education system, and its medical colleges are no exception. The state has some of the top medical colleges in the country, offering world-class education and training to aspiring doctors.
  • Pioneering academic excellence:  It is already a known and proven fact that how good southern states are for their academic excellence and Kerala is on top of them with one of the best academic excellence across the region. 
  • Affordability: Compared to other states in India, the cost of pursuing MBBS in Kerala is relatively affordable. The tuition fees are reasonable, and there are several scholarships and financial aid programs available to students.
  • Availability of Seats: Kerala has a large number of medical colleges, both government and private, which means there are plenty of seats available for MBBS aspirants. This reduces competition and makes it easier for students to secure admission.
  • State of art facilities: Kerala's medical colleges are equipped with state-of-the-art facilities that give you proper exposure to medical science. 80% of the colleges in Kerala have a good infrastructure that has enough equipment to give you the best clinical facilities to learn more and grow more. 
  • Connectivity: One of the best advantages of studying in Kerala is its exceptional connectivity to airports. The state boasts of having excellent transportation facilities that make it easy for students to commute to and from college.

Whether you are a local student or coming from another part of the country, getting to your college in Kerala is a breeze. With well-connected airports in major cities like Thiruvananthapuram, Kochi, and Kozhikode, you can reach your college in no time.

  • Exposure to Diversity: Kerala is a culturally diverse state, and studying MBBS in Kerala provides an opportunity for students to interact with people from different backgrounds and learn about their customs and traditions.
  • Experienced Faculty:  The medical colleges in Kerala have highly qualified and experienced faculty, consisting of doctors, professors, and researchers with years of expertise. They bring with them a wealth of knowledge and experience helping their students to become successful doctors and healthcare professionals.
  • Clinical Exposure: Kerala's medical institutes offer a very high level of clinical exposure, and almost 80% of the colleges have an exemplary walk-in ratio of patients. 

The features of Kerala and its colleges provide ample evidence to support the assertion that there exist top medical colleges in Kerala that rank among the best in the country.

Top Medical College In Kerala For Bright MBBS Future

1. Jubilee Mission Medical College and Research Institute - Thrissur

Jubilee Mission Medical College and Research Institute, located in Thrissur, Kerala, is a highly acclaimed medical institution known for its excellence in medical education, research, and patient care.

The college, established in 2003, offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in medicine, nursing, and allied health sciences. The courses are designed to provide students with a strong foundation in medical knowledge and clinical skills, preparing them for successful careers in healthcare.

Seats in Medical College is recognized by the Medical Council of India (MCI) and has 

  • 100 seats available for the MBBS program. Additionally, the college offers 16 postgraduate programs with a total of 35 seats and 2 super specialty programs (DM) with a total of 3 seats.

The best MBBS college in Kerala provides a supportive environment for growth and enrichment, offering ample opportunities to students.

2. Amala Institute of Medical Sciences - Thrissur 

Amala Institute of Medical Sciences is a well-known medical institution located in Thrissur, Kerala. It was founded in 1978 to provide quality medical education, research, and healthcare services to the community.

The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various medical disciplines. 

The hospital associated with the college is a renowned healthcare institution that provides exceptional patient care and serves as a valuable resource for clinical training and research. Just because of its excellent facilities and exposure, this college has made it one of the top private medical colleges in Kerala.

Seat:

  • MBBS - 100 seats

Postgraduate programs - Total 69 seats (MD/MS - 50 seats, MSc - 15 seats, PG Diploma - 4 seats)

Super specialty programs - Total 9 seats (DM - 3 seats, MCh - 6 seats)

3. Malankara Orthodox Syrian Church Medical College - Kolenchery 

The faculty at Malankara Orthodox Syrian Church Medical College comprises highly experienced and dedicated professionals who provide quality education to the students. They use innovative teaching techniques and technologies to ensure that students receive the best possible education.

Details in Quick : 

  • Established in 2001 
  • Total Number of seats is: 100
  • Seats in PG course: 19

MBBS Fee structure: 

  • Government Seat : INR 34 Lacs
  • Management Seat : INR 50 Lacs
  • NRI Seat : INR 1.1 Cr.

Malankara Orthodox Syrian Church Medical College is committed to making healthcare accessible to all, irrespective of their financial background. The college offers scholarships and financial aid programs to deserving students to ensure that everyone has an opportunity to receive a quality education in medicine. 

4. Pushpagiri Institute Of Medical Science- Thiruvalla

Pushpagiri Institute of Medical Science is a medical college located in Thiruvalla, a town in the state of Kerala, India. The college was established in 2002 by the Pushpagiri Medical Society, a registered charitable organisation. The institution offers undergraduate and postgraduate medical courses and is affiliated with Kerala University of Health Sciences (KUHS). 

Seats :

  • Maximum intake capacity is 100 seats.

MBBS Fee Structure :

  • Rs. 694380/ Year for MBBS

The college has a state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a teaching hospital with advanced medical facilities, providing a conducive environment for students to help them in growing their careers. 

5. Believers Church Medical College, Hospital- Thiruvalla 

Believers Church Medical College and Hospital is located in Thiruvalla, India. It is a medical college that offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various fields of medicine, including MBBS, MD, MS, and PG Diploma courses. 

The Medical College, which was founded in 2016, is located on a 25-acre (10-hectare) campus that can be accessed by both rail and road. It is affiliated with a multi-specialty hospital that has a capacity of 743 beds.

Course -

  • 5.5 year duration for MBBS course

Fee -

  • INR 2,868,750 with the intake of 110 candidates across various courses.

The college is affiliated with the Kerala University of Health Sciences and is recognized by the Medical Council of India. It also provides tertiary care facilities and medical services to the local community and neighboring areas.

6. MES Medical College- Malappuram 

MES Medical College is a medical institution located in Malappuram district of Kerala, India. It was established in 2002 and is affiliated with the Kerala University of Health Sciences. 

Course Details 

  • MBBS course duration is for 5.5 years

Fee

  • INR 31.27 L

The college is equipped with modern facilities, including state-of-the-art classrooms, laboratories, and a library with a vast collection of medical books and journals. 

7. Sree Gokulam Medical College

Sree Gokulam Gopalan, an esteemed philanthropist, and visionary entrepreneur, established SGMC College in 2004 with a noble mission to recognize the significance of the medical field and nurture the development of outstanding doctors as an invaluable asset to society.

SGMC College has consistently produced a multitude of exceptional doctors in each academic year since its establishment, thanks to its exceptional faculty and state-of-the-art infrastructure that have proved to be invaluable assets in providing comprehensive nurturing to its students.

Total strength of the seat is 150.

Course

  • Duration of MBBS is 4.5 year + 1 year compulsory medical internship

Fee

  • GEN.  (85%) 12,65,000/-
  • NRI (15%) $.46,000/-USD

8. KMCT Medical College, Kozhikode

KMCT Medical College is a prestigious institution located in Kozhikode, Kerala, India. The college has been providing top-notch medical education to students since its inception and is renowned for its exceptional faculty, modern facilities, and commitment to producing outstanding medical professionals. 

Total available seat in KMCT college is 150 for MBBS.

Fee

  • Rs. 2,43,900/- For MBBS

Course

  • MD/MS and MBBS

The total duration of MBBS in KMCT college is 5.5 years.

With a focus on holistic development and hands-on training, KMCT Medical College prepares students to meet the healthcare needs of society with skill, compassion, and integrity.

9. DR. Somervell Memorial CSI Medical College - Karakonam

The Karakonam Hospital is known as the renowned state's first private hospital in the state of Kerala commenced in the year 1975. Dr. Somervell Memorial CSI Medical College in Karakonam is a highly esteemed medical institution known for its excellent academic standards and state-of-the-art facilities. 

Fee

  • 12.71 lakh ( 1st Year Fee )

Course

  • 5 years for MBBS

With highly qualified faculty, modern infrastructure, and a student-centered approach to education, Dr. Somervell Memorial CSI Medical College offers students an unparalleled learning experience and prepares them for successful careers in the medical field.

10. Malabar Medical College and Research Centre, Kozhikhode  

In order to give students wishing to become doctors the required medical knowledge, MALABAR College's foundation was set up in 2010.

This college is spread across 32 acres. The MMC Hospital caters to a large portion of the North Malabar region, encompassing the districts of Calicut, Kannur, and Wayanad, by offering quality healthcare services to the local community.

The total number of courses are 12, which has 2 UG and 10 PG courses.
