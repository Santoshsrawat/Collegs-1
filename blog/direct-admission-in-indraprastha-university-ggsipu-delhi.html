    Here is everything you need to know about Direct Admission in Indraprastha University (GGSIPU Delhi).

    Direct Admission in Indraprastha University (GGSIPU Delhi) is possible in different educational courses, most popularly in B.Tech, B.Com, BBA LLB, JMC, BBA & MBA. To get admission to the best IP University Colleges (GGSIPU) in Engineering program, a candidate has to appear for JEE MAIN and should have cleared Class 12th form CBSE with Science, Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics as main subjects.

    Let’s dive in and explore the complete admission process through the management/NRI quota in IP University (GGSIPU Delhi).

    What is Direct Admission in Indraprastha University (GGSIPU Delhi) ?

    According to GGSIPU’s latest brochure for 2023-24, there are 10% of the total seats are available for students to participate through Direct Admission in Indraprastha University (GGSIPU Delhi). 

    For candidates who for some reason were unable to obtain admission through the counselling process, Indraprastha University offers a pathway called direct admission through management quota in IP University. For students to enter GGSIPU right away, it is a tremendous opportunity but it's crucial to remember that not every program offers direct admission in Indraprastha University (GGSIPU Delhi). Also, another important thing is that management quota admission in IPU is at a higher cost than regular fees, due to a very limited amount of seats.

    For other courses i.e. BBA, BCA, BJMC, B.A, B.Sc, BHMCT and M. Tech every student must take the IPU CET, IP University's own exam, to be admitted to GGSIPU.

    To participate in direct admission in Indraprastha University (GGSIPU Delhi), you must know which all courses are offered in IP University. So, here is the list of courses mentioned below-

    Direct Admission in Indraprastha University (GGSIPU Delhi): Eligibility Criteria

    To be eligible for direct admission in Indraprastha University (GGSIPU Delhi) here are some of the necessary conditions to be fulfilled, namely-

    • Every candidate must have passed 10+2 qualifying examination from a central or state board or any equivalent exam.
    • Candidate should have secured a minimum of 80% in Physics, Chemistry and Biology, Maths and English.
    • JEE MAIN is the main entrance exam which should be appeared by all students.

    Direct Admission in Indraprastha University (GGSIPU Delhi) for B.Tech : Application Process

    The following are some of the steps that every student is expected to follow to obtain direct admission in Indraprastha University (GGSIPU Delhi)

    Step 1 - IPU B.Tech Registration

    Candidates must first complete the registration procedure by providing information such as their name, mobile number, email address, program group, and program name. Credentials are distributed to the registered email address and contact number when registration is successful.

    Step 2 - Fill out the IPU B.Tech Application form

    Candidates must enter their credentials to connect to their accounts and select "Fill Application Form". The necessary information, including personal information, qualification information, and program information, had to be entered to fill out application for getting Direct Admission in Indraprastha University (GGSIPU Delhi)

    Step 3 - Uploading of documents

    Candidates must upload their photo and signature in the required format in the next step.

    Step 4 - Payment of application fee

    Candidates must select the "Preview" button to review all the supplied information after uploading their documents. On the submit button, click. Candidates who are not in the exempted categories will automatically be led to the online payment gateway, where they can pay with a debit card, credit card, or Internet banking after their application has been submitted successfully. Before a printout of the generated payment acknowledgment sheet could be obtained, a payment of Rs. 1200 had to be made.

    Our FAQ's

    How many seats are there in the management quota?

    The number of seats under the NRI/management quota/PIO quota is around 10% of total seats in private colleges.

    What is the counselling fee for GGSIPU?

    Yes, candidates must pay a counselling fee in order to take part in the IPU CET counselling for Direct Admission in IPU for B.Tech. The cost of counselling is INR 1000.

    Can I get direct admission in GGSIPU Indraprastha University?

    Direct admission to GGSIPU won't be a problem for you if you receive the right advice and complete support from a reputable career counselling expert like Team Get My University.

    What is the difference between management quota and university quota?

    We are all aware of the university quota, which stipulates that applicants must pass an entrance exam. Direct admission through management or NRI quota generally refers to a set number of seats set aside for applicants who for some reason weren't able to meet the eligibility requirements.

    Can I get admission in IPU without entrance?

    Every college has its own requirements and its own entrance exam. If we talk in general, then admission in GGSIPU is through the Common Entrance Test (CET) conducted by the university. However, if there are vacant seats, students may get direct admission in IPU for B.Tech.
