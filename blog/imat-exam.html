IMAT Exam 2023 Fee, Syllabus And Eligibility

Team getmyuniversity 11 May 2023 13693 views

The Italian Ministry of Higher Education and Research hired Cambridge Assessment Admission Testing, the global leader in the creation of university admission tests, to produce a test specifically for admission to English-taught medical degrees in Italy, it is known as the IMAT Exam 2023.

In a broad sense, it is just an entrance exam that applicants must pass to be admitted to Italian institutions to study MBBS i.e. Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery. IMAT is conducted in Italy in a manner similar to that of the NEET test in India.

You must study the entire article by GMU and get all the necessary information to learn more about the IMAT exam 2023, including the application fee, process, eligibility requirements, format, and syllabus.

How to apply for IMAT Exam 2023?

Step 1: Pre-enrollment

Only non-EU students must pre-enroll. As, EU students are exempt from this requirement. To enroll in any Italian medical institution, EU students only need to visit the consulate that is closest to them to obtain the "Declaration of Value" paper.

Step 2: IMAT Registration

The next step is IMAT Registration, which follows pre-enrollment. Online applications that must be completed by students in order to take the IMAT exam are made available in July. Apply as soon as the applications are released if you don't want to miss your chance to get accepted into one of the best medical schools,

Step 3: Giving IMAT Exam

In the month of September, the IMAT exam is conducted. So according to your selected location, you have to reach on time. And accordingly, after identification seats are allotted and given instructions on the conduct of the exam.

Step 4: IMAT Rankings and University Enrollment

The IMAT exam is given in the month of September. You must therefore arrive in plenty of time for the place you have chosen. After an identity check, seats are assigned and instructions are given on how to complete the exam.

NOTE:- You can apply only to 1 medical university!

IMAT Exam Eligibility Criteria

As per the IMAT exam eligibility criteria, a student will only be considered eligible to take the IMAT exam if they are at least 17 years old and have completed the 12th grade from a recognised board with at least 60 marks in PCB (Physics, Chemistry, and Biology).

Format of IMAT

For students who want to study in Italian Universities, IMAT is a must. IMAT Online is a written pen and paper exam having 100 minutes exam time. In the exam there are in total of 60 questions which are multiple choice questions with 5 options among which one is to be selected.

For every right answer, 1.5 points are awarded. While for the wrong answer, -0.4 is allotted and no marks for no answer. To write in the exam, the calculator is not provided and the exam is divided into 4 sections.

IMAT Exam Syllabus

IMAT Syllabus consists of 4 sections, namely:-

  • Section 1: Logical Reasoning and General Knowledge

  • Section 2: Biology

  • Section 3: Chemistry

  • Section 4: Physics and Maths

For admission to the top Italian colleges, what is a good IMAT score?

Medical students with scores between 0 to 15 or 16 to 35 are reportedly ineligible for admission to prestigious medical schools. Therefore, passing IMAT with scores between 36 and 46 will be sufficient to get you into the best medical school in Italy. Additionally, a score of 50 or higher will be more than enough to get you into the college of your choice.

Nonetheless, what qualifies as a decent score is subjective and is determined by variables including the amount of competition, the number of seats available, and whether you are an EU or non-EU candidate.

It should be emphasized that while EU students can score around 50, it is crucial for non-EU students to obtain 50 or more, preferably at least 55.

How to Prepare for IMAT?

A student needs to purchase some reference materials or use last year's sample papers to practice daily. Additionally, one's own abilities and knowledge are crucial for passing IMAT, which is why it is regarded as difficult. As a result, be careful to plan ahead, put a lot of effort, and practice frequently. This is what will make sure that your ambition to become a doctor in the future is strong.

Conclusion

The IMAT 2023 score will likely increase as a result of several adjustments and difficulties. As it gets more tough and will need more effort due to the high level of competition already present in all medical colleges in Italy. Utilising top-notch study materials and taking as many practical exams as you can, are therefore essential.

Have faith and prepare yourself well to study MBBS in Italy.

Best of luck to you.

2. Who is eligible for IMAT?

All medical candidates, both Italian and foreign, may apply for the IMAT exam after completing their 10+2 from any recognised board.

5. Does the IMAT have negative marking?

In the IMAT, 0.4 points are deducted for each incorrect response. The student, however, receives 1.5 points for each accurate response.

4. When does IMAT take place?

Every year in September, the English-medium IMAT Exam is held, with results being released in 15 to 20 days.

3. Is IMAT easy for Indian students?

IMAT is actually not so simple because it takes a lot of work to perform. Additionally, a person's knowledge and abilities count as well as their performance helps to determine whether they will be admitted to the college of their choice.
