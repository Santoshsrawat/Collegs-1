Introduction

Maharashtra is a nearly closed state where only Maharashtra residents will be permitted to participate in Maharashtra NEET Counselling in 2023. But if students from other states want to participate in Maharashtra, they may only do so through institutional quotas. Institutional quota fees must be similar to NRI quota costs.

To obtain the complete information about Maharashtra NEET Counselling 2023, go through the entire blog informing everything about Maharashtra UG NEET Counselling 2023 including important dates, admission process, documents required, seat matrix etc.

Eligibility Criteria for Maharashtra NEET Counselling 2023

To be eligible to participate in Maharashtra NEET Counselling 2023, students are advised to check the conditions.

  • Candidate must be a citizen of India
  • Qualifying NEET exam is compulsory for every medical aspirant
  • Student must have passed Class 10 or equivalent from an institute located in the state of Maharashtra           
  • Candidates who have qualified 10th class outside of the state of Maharashtra but passed their 12th class in the state of Maharashtra should produce a domicile certificate.
  • Candidates should pass the 10+2 level.

Maharashtra NEET UG Counselling Admission Process

Step 1: Register and fill out the Maharashtra MBBS 2023 Application form

A candidate who wishes to participate in Maharashtra MBBS Counselling 2023 must register on the official website of the State CET Cell. After getting registered, medical aspirants shall fill out the Maharashtra NEET 2023 Application form.

Step 2:  Choice filling and locking

When the competent authority releases the provisional merit list, student can check then accordingly participate in Maharashtra NEET Counselling 2023. Carefully complete the college preference form because it cannot be modified once submitted. The GMU team can be of assistance to students who are unsure of which college to choose. 

Step 3: Reporting to College

As soon as the authority releases the seat allotment list, candidates can then on the basis of the choices filled shall report to the college before the last date. After the seat allotment list is made public, medical applicants must report. The assigned seat will be forfeited if a student doesn't report.

Step 4: Uploading and verification of documents

Reporting to the college is said to be an important step in order to successfully complete the verification of the document process. A student is advised to carry the original documents along with an attested copy for verification to the institute. All the documents are submitted online when submitting the Maharashtra NEET Counselling 2023. However, it is mandatory for the candidate to be physically present at the college for verification of documents.

Documents required for Maharashtra NEET Counselling 2023

The documents that will be required during the verification of documents and reporting round are as follows:-

  • NEET 2023 Admit Card
  • NEET 2023 Scorecard
  • Copy of NEET application form 2023’
  • Nationality certificate
  • Domicile certificate
  • Class 10th marksheet
  • Class 12th certificate
  • Medical fitness certificate
  • Caste certificate
  • Transfer certificate
  • Aadhaar Card

Why MBBS from Maharashtra?

Multiple scholarship options are provided by the Maharashtra government to its students, which is advantageous for Maharashtra students. The following are additional advantages that MBBS students in Maharashtra may take advantage of:

  • Highest quality education
  • Several medical colleges
  • Excellent work opportunities
  • Good population density allowing large patient influx
  • Due to large population, the demand for doctors is also high

Know About Status Retention Form

Status retention form is a form which is required to be submitted by a candidate who has been allotted a seat and so being satisfied with the allotted seat, is no longer interested to participate in further rounds of counselling.

Our FAQ's

What is the official website for Maharashtra NEET counselling 2023?

The State CET Cell's official website is where applicants who wish to participate in the Maharashtra MBBS counselling 2023 must register. https://cetcell.mahacet.org/

Who is eligible for Maharashtra state quota in NEET?

Every medical aspirant who has cleared NEET 2023 exam are eligible to participate in top medical colleges in Maharashtra through 15% All India Quota or 85% State Quota.

On which mode Maharashtra MBBS counselling will be conducted?

Maharashtra NEET MBBS Counselling 2023 is conducted through online mode. So, students must take a note of it when the online application form gets released.

How can I register for MBBS Counselling in Maharashtra?

Go through the entire blog to know the complete process as to how a medical aspirant can register for MBBS Counselling in Maharashtra. For any help, you are free to contact team Get My University.

How many rounds are there in NEET Counselling in Maharashtra?

Four rounds of counselling will be held by the MCC: AIQ Round 1, AIQ Round 2, AIQ Mop-up round, and stray vacancy round. All India Quota would be eligible based on their NEET 2023 results.
