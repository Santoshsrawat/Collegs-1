Introduction

Are you interested to get a medical education at a reasonable cost while studying MBBS in India? Then, here is the blog informing you all about the medical colleges available at low cost.

In general, only government medical colleges provide MBBS at a reasonable price. However, there are some excellent private medical institutes also where you can study for a reasonable price.

So, let’s discuss it all!

Cost of MBBS in India

There are three states that offer MBBS at a lower cost than other states when it comes to economic fee structures.   All three of these states offer top-notch education, vibrant environments, and a pleasant ambiance, making them ideal locations to pursue a medical profession.

Let's start off by talking about MBBS in Kerala. The total cost of MBBS in Kerala ranges between 40 to 45 lakhs. The next state is Karnataka, where you can finish your MBBS programme in a total cost of 50–60 lakhs. The third state is Chhattisgarh, the required money to study MBBS in Chhattisgarh is between 54 and 55 lakhs.

Let's look at the yearly expenditure that a medical student will incur to pursue an MBBS in Kerala, Karnataka, or Chhattisgarh.

If you want to study MBBS in Kerala, you should be aware that this state is one where the MBBS admission form is generally released prior to the announcement of the NEET exam results. Additionally, the admission forms are rarely opened again for pursuing MBBS in Kerala.

Thankfully, the Kerala NEET Counselling forms are scheduled to be available after the NEET exam results have been released. Therefore, if you still haven't filled out the form, then fill it as soon as it is out.

Kerala is an open state having 363 open states available, making it possible for students from throughout India to study MBBS at a reasonable cost.

2.    Karnataka

Karnataka is the second state known to provide MBBS at an affordable cost in India.  It is one of the most preferred state to pursue MBBS. The reason behind Karnataka being so much preferred by students is due to the good quality education available at an inexpensive budget.

In India, Karnataka is the second state that is reputed to offer affordable MBBS at an inexpensive cost in India. Students favor Karnataka so much because Karnataka holds the maximum number of MBBS seats and have the highest number of deemed colleges. As, mostly the colleges are located in Bangalore, which is a metropolitical city having good connectivity and thus making it accessible for students to study MBBS.

Karnataka is the second state in India that offers an economical MBBS programme. The reason why Karnataka is so popular is that it has the most deemed colleges and the highest MBBS seats available. Since the majority of the colleges are in Bangalore, a major city with good facility of transportation, it is convenient for students to pursue an MBBS in Karnataka.

Without question, choosing to study MBBS in Karnataka is a wise move. The Karnataka NEET Counselling application form is made available following the announcement of the NEET exam results at the time of Round 1. Therefore, if you are interested in Karnataka, fill out the application as soon as possible; otherwise, you will ultimately be unable to participate in the Karnataka NEET Counselling 2023. 

Additionally, because Karnataka is an open state, students from other states are welcome to engage and experience medical education in Karnataka. There are 765 available open states in total. The best thing about studying in Karnataka is that there are no special requirements like bank guarantee or security deposit. Therefore, there won't be any complexity.

3.    Chhattisgarh

Chhattisgarh ranks third among states where MBBS may be studied for a reasonable cost. Many MBBS seats were vacant in Chhattisgarh as a result of numerous students leaving their seats, as has been observed. However, there is no harm studying medicine in Chhattisgarh since there are reputable medical colleges that accept students with lower NEET rank. 

Similar to the procedure in Karnataka, the NEET application form submission period begins once the NEET exam results are announced. So, if you're interested in enrolling in Chhattisgarh, you should be aware that there are 96 open seats for MBBS students there.

The state of Chhattisgarh has two additional requirements that students may encounter since they call for the submission of a security deposit and a one-year bank guarantee.
