Introduction

Rajasthan MBBS admission is conducted by NEET UG Medical and Dental Admission and Counselling board. To get admission in top medical colleges of Rajasthan, qualifying NEET entrance exam is a must.

According to historical data, Rajasthan's literacy rate has increased. As it acts as a hub for a variety of cultures and mindsets, allowing students to expand their horizons and social norms. These facts can be taken into account when pursuing an MBBS in Rajasthan because prospective students would have access to some good, developed course structures and formats with many of the top medical science institutes located in Rajasthan.

To participate in Rajasthan NEET Counselling 2023, you will need to know the eligibility requirements, the paperwork needed for the MBBS registration process, and the seat allocations for public and private medical colleges in Rajasthan. Therefore, this article will notify you of all the details of Rajasthan NEET Counselling.

Rajasthan NEET Counselling Admission Process

Step 1: Register on the official website of i.e. www.rajneetug2022.com  (website will vary every year) 

and then login using ID and password.

Step 2: Fill the Rajasthan NEET Counselling 2023 application form and don’t forget to take a print out of online application by paying the fees.

Step 3: Upload the required set of documents such as NEET Admit Card, rank card, 12th marksheet, signature of parents and student as well as thumb impression of student along with the photograph.

Step 4: After uploading all the documents, a secret key will be given to a student in order to download the document verification slip.

Step 5: With the downloaded document verification slip, a medical aspirant can then start the process of choice filling online.

Step 6: When the final result is declared, then the student can proceed to pay the fee

Step 7: The last step is Reporting. After the Rajasthan NEET first round counselling result, students should report to the respective authority of Rajasthan before going to the college.

Note:-
Refundable: If you exit the counselling process after the first round or join the college you were allotted in the second round, or if you joined in the first round but did not upgrade in the second round, you can resign during the second round of counselling to avoid losing your deposit.
Non-refundable: If you do not join the college you were allotted in the second round, your deposit will not be refunded.

Salient Features of Rajasthan NEET Counselling 

  1. Free exit is only allowed in Round 1.
  2. If you did not report then there is no free exit in Round 2.
  3. In the second round of the counselling process, non-upgraded candidates are permitted to exercise a free exit option if they choose to resign on the spot.
  4. If a candidate is allotted a seat but does not take admission, the security deposit will not be refunded.
  5. After the procedure of allotment of seats is done then, document verification will be started.
  6. The process of locking choices will occur automatically.
  7. Eligible candidates (those who have not been allotted any seat or those who have been allotted a seat and have taken admission) will receive a refund of their security deposit after the completion of all rounds of the Rajasthan NEET  counselling 2023 process.
  8. The application form will open before every round of counselling.

Eligibility Criteria for Rajasthan UG NEET Counselling 2023

  1. Medical student must qualify NEET entrance exam.
  2. The student must be an Indian citizen to claim a seat under Govt./Private/Management
  3. Foreign Nationals are eligible to apply for seats under NRI/Other Quota
  4. Non-Domicile can apply for seats in Private Medical Colleges
  5. Student must have cleared 12th class with minimum of 50% in Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  6. Domicile candidates are eligible for all seats
  7. Non-Domicile candidates can not participate from Round 1, instead they are allowed to participate from Round 2.

Criteria for Domicile Claim

  1. The candidate's natural father/mother must have lived in Rajasthan continuously for the last 10 years, and the candidate must have studied for at least five years in a recognized educational institution in Rajasthan during this period.
  2. Bonafide residents of Rajasthan who are studying in other states are also eligible.
  3. The candidate must be the son/daughter of a serving or retired employee of the Government of Rajasthan or an officer of All Indian Services who has served for more than three years with the state government.
  4. The candidate must be the son/daughter of a serving or retired permanent employee of a government undertaking, corporation, improvement trust, municipal body, council, panchayat samiti, or cooperative body constituted by the Government of Rajasthan by an act of law who has served for at least three years on the last date of submission of the online application form.
  5. The candidate must be the son/daughter of a serving/retired permanent employee of any of the State University or Secondary Board of Education (Rajasthan) who has worked in Rajasthan for three years preceding the last date of submission of the online application form.
  6. The candidate must be the natural son/daughter of a serving/retired permanent employee of the Indian Defense, and the employee must be of Rajasthan origin, irrespective of his place of posting.
  7. The candidate must be the natural son/daughter of a serving permanent employee of the Indian Defense and the employee must be posted in Rajasthan at the time of the last date of submission of the online application form for admission.
  8. The candidate must be the son/daughter of a serving/retired person of the Paramilitary Forces of India and an employee of Rajasthan state, irrespective of his place of posting.
  9. The candidate must be the son/daughter of a serving person of the Paramilitary Forces of India and the employee must be posted in Rajasthan at the time of the last date of submission of the online application form for admission.
  10. The candidate must be the son/daughter of a Judge of Rajasthan High Court, subject to the condition that he/she submits an undertaking/affidavit stating that such a benefit is not being claimed or availed of by them in any other state.
  11. Candidates who do not fall under the above clauses I to X will be considered for allotment against NRI quota seats and will also be considered for allotment against vacant seats available after exhausting the state merit list.

Eligibility for NRI Candidates

The student must have at least one parent who is an NRI and lives overseas, or a first-degree relative (such as a true brother or sister), in order to qualify for admission under the NRI quota. Alternatively, if the applicant is a ward of an NRI guardian, they may also be considered for admission under specific circumstances, providing that the guardian is the candidate's actual uncle or aunt, grandparent, or first-degree cousin.

A certificate from the Indian embassy or ministry of external affairs, or a notarized undertaking signed by the sponsor and witnessed by the Notary Public of another country, must be given as proof of NRI status. Additionally, the sponsor is required to file an affidavit guaranteeing that they will look after the student and pay for their complete tuition.

Why MBBS from Rajasthan?

With a total area of around 342,239 square kilometres, Rajasthan is the largest and sixth most populous state in India. It is strong evidence that proves all Rajasthani medical colleges would receive adequate clinical exposure.

Additionally, the state is well-known for its top-notch medical education, first-rate facilities, and diversified patient population, making it a prime location for those aspiring to careers in medicine.

Our FAQ's

In which mode is Rajasthan MBBS 2023 counselling conducted?

The first round will be conducted online and second rounds onwards of Rajasthan NEET Counselling 2023 take place in offline mode.

Is Rajasthan an open state for NEET UG Counselling 2023?

Rajasthan is an open state, thus students from other states are welcome to register for counselling.

Is it mandatory to qualify NEET exam in order to participate in Rajasthan NEET Counselling?

Yes, qualifying NEET exam is must and the prime obligation which must be fulfilled in order to participate in Rajasthan NEET Counselling 2023.

How can I register for NEET Counselling in Rajasthan?

From Rajasthan mbbs counselling official website i.e. http://rajneetug2022.com/ a medical aspirant can apply to participate in Rajasthan NEET Counselling 2023.

What is self-financed seat in Government Medical College and who is eligible to take admission in Rajasthan NEET Counselling 2023?

Self-financed seats are the paid seat or management seat in the Government Medical Colleges in Rajasthan. There is no subsidy given on these seats by the Government. Fee will be very high as compared to normal medical seats in Government college, but cut off will be relatively low as compared to Government seat. Only Rajasthan State domicile candidates are eligible to claim such seats.
