Introduction to Direct Admission in SCMHRD and Its MBA Program

One of India's top business colleges, Symbiosis Centre for Management and Human Resource Development (SCMHRD), is well known for its MBA programme. For students seeking to pursue a rewarding career in management, SCMHRD has long been a top choice due to its dedication to academic achievement and holistic development. It is the ideal college for MBA education because of its demanding curriculum, knowledgeable faculty, and industry-focused approach.

Direct Admission in SCMHRD for MBA Eligibility Criteria

SCMHRD MBA Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for direct admission in SCMHRD for MBA, the conditions to be fulfilled by every MBA aspirant are as follows-

  • The candidate should have graduated from a reputable university or other institution of national importance with at least 50% marks or an equivalent grade (45% or an equivalent grade for members of the Scheduled Castes and Tribes).
  • Students in their last year of graduation may also apply, although admission is contingent upon achieving a minimum of 50% (45% for SC/ST) in their final examination. 
  • The concession in marks at graduation for SC/ST candidates does not apply to International candidates.

SCMHRD MBA Programme Intake : 180

SCMHRD MBA (IDM) Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for direct admission in SCMHRD for MBA (IDM), the conditions to be fulfilled by every MBA aspirant are-

A minimum of 50% marks or the equivalent grade, or 45% for candidates from Scheduled Castes (SC) or Scheduled Tribes (ST), in any Engineering Stream (B.E. or B.Tech), Bachelor of Architecture (B.Arch), or Bachelor of Planning (B.Plan.) from any recognised university or institution of national importance.

OR

With a minimum of 12 months of work experience in the industries of infrastructure, infrastructure consulting, energy, real estate, logistics, supply chain, and any other infrastructure-related industry. Bachelor degree from non-engineering branches from any recognised University/ Institution of National Importance with a minimum of 50% marks or equivalent grade.

An equivalency certificate from the Association of Indian Universities (AIU) is required of any candidate who has completed the qualifying requirements at any foreign university.

SCMHRD MBA in Business Analytics Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for direct admission in SCMHRD for MBA, the essential conditions which every MBA Aspirant is expected to fulfill are-

The candidate must have graduated from a university or other institution of national importance with at least 50% honours or an equivalent grade (45% for members of Scheduled Castes (SC) or Scheduled Tribes (ST) or an equivalent grade) and at least two years of full-time job experience. A candidate who has completed qualifying qualification from any Foreign University must obtain an equivalence certificate from Association of Indian Universities (AIU).

The concession in marks at graduation for SC/ST candidates does not apply to International candidates.

SCMHRD MBA (Executive) Eligibility Criteria

Graduation from any accredited university or institution of national importance with a minimum grade point average of 50% (45% for members of the Scheduled Caste or Scheduled Tribes) AND a minimum of one year of full-time work experience in a registered firm, company, industry, academic institution, government agency, or autonomous organisation.

How to Apply for Direct Admission in SCMHRD for MBA ?

In order to apply for Direct Admission in SCMHRD for MBA, a student must carefully proceed.

Step 1 - Register for SNAP

Step 2 - Choose MBA Programme from the list of programmes given in SNAP form

Step 3 - Clear SNAP Entrance Exam

Applying for Direct Admission in Symbiosis Centre for Management and Human Resource Development  for MBA requires you to clear Symbiosis National Aptitude Test (SNAP) as it is an obligatory exam that must be cleared by every MBA Aspirant.

Step 4 - Visit the official website of SCMHRD and Fill the Application form

After clearing SNAP entrance exam, students shall visit the official website of SCMHRD and fill out the online application form. Additionally, submit the personal details as aked in the form. 

Step 5 - Payment of Fees

Once the application form is complete and all necessary documents have been uploaded, students will be required to pay the application fee as mentioned on the website. It is advisable to keep a record of the payment confirmation for future reference.

With this, you have completed the process of Direct Admission in SCMHRD for MBA.
