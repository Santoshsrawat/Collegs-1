Introduction

In the state of Uttarakhand, there aren't many medical colleges. There are eight medical colleges in total, three of which are private and five of which are public. Even though Uttarakhand has fewer medical colleges, it is nevertheless known for its high standards of education, which attracts students from other states to come and study MBBS here.

Uttarakhand NEET UG Counselling Admission Process

Step 1: Uttarakhand NEET Counselling 2023 Registration

Each medical applicant must first register on the official website and make the requisite UK NEET 2023 payment. It should be noted that both the counselling fee and the security deposit are obligatory payments and that only the security deposit is refundable.

After then, students can log in and review the seating chart to make their selections.

Step 2: Fill the Uttarakhand NEET Counselling form

After creating id and password, student is required to fill the Uttarakhand NEET Counselling 2023 form and take a print out of the same and pay the fees accordingly.

The authorities will publish the seat vacancy details for each round of counseling. A merit list will also be published based on the NEET scores and other eligibility criteria. 

Step 3: Upload

When applying for Uttarakhand NEET Counselling, some of the documents that will be required to submit are as follows :-

  • NEET Admit Card
  • Rank Card
  • 12th marksheet
  • Signature of student and parents
  • Thumb impression of student

Step 3: Choice filling and locking

Students can start filling MBBS colleges of their choice once the results of the mock test are announced. Since there can be no additions once the selections have been made, it is a crucial step that calls for wise decision-making. Rearranging or deleting is permitted, though.

Step 4: Reporting

After the declaration of final result, make the payment and then student will be required to report to respective institution and then finally report to the college allotted. And at the time of reporting to the college, the student must have all the necessary documents.

Salient Features of Uttarakhand NEET Counselling 2023

  • Medical aspirants can willingly take an exit in the first round of Uttarakhand NEET Counselling 2023 itself without any penalty being imposed.
  • It is necessary for a medical aspirant to report after getting the seat. Where the candidate does not report after seat allotment, then when initiating Round 2, student will not be allowed to take an exit.
  • In case the student does not participate after allotment of seat in the second round, then in that case there will be no refund of security deposit.
  • Verification of documents is an important step which must be completed at the time of conducting online Uttarakhand NEET UG Counselling 2023.
  • Where the student does not get a seat or if the student got a seat but do not wish to take admission then, student will be eligible to get a refund of the security amount paid at the time of counselling. After all the rounds of counselling are finished, the refund amount is processed.

Eligibility Criteria for Uttarakhand NEET Counselling 2023

The following prerequisites must be met in order to register for NEET UG. counselling process

  • A medical student must get a minimum of 50% overall (or 40% for students from the reserved categories) in physics, chemistry, and biology in 12th grade.
  • Students must be at least 17 years old as of December 31, 2023, however applicants from the reserved category have a 5-year age relaxation.
  • Student must be an Indian citizen to claim seat under Government, Private, Management.
  • Foreign Nationals are also eligible to apply for seats under NRI/Other Quota.
  • Qualifying for NEET entrance exam is a must without which a student cannot participate in Uttarakhand NEET Counselling.
  • Other State students are also eligible to apply for management quota seat in Private Medical and Dental Colleges.
  • Other State Students who have done 10th and 10+2 form Uttarakhand can claim Government seat under UR category.
  • Domicile candidates are eligible for all seats.

Criteria for Non- Domicile Claim

Candidates who do not have a domicile in Uttarakhand but have completed their 10th and 12th grades are eligible for government seats to study medicine in 2023. These applicants will not be considered for any reservations.

Why MBBS from Uttarakhand?

The best medical college in the country is located in Uttarakhand, where you may pursue MBBS program while spending less money because it is also cost-effective.

The majority of the colleges in Uttarakhand are dispersed around hill station cities like Mussoorie and Dehradun, so in addition to the excellent academics offered in medical colleges of Uttarakhand, they also have excellent infrastructure that students will undoubtedly enjoy.

Overall, for students who prefer being surrounded by hills and greenery, Uttarakhand is the perfect area to pursue MBBS. As Uttarakhand is known to be a well-known destination for medical program, there are few institutions but all are performing well. Although the cost is on the higher side, but a student gets an opportunity to study at a lower NEET score and have an advantage of pursuing it by enjoying the beautiful and peaceful surrounding.

How will the Uttarakhand NEET Counselling 2023 be conducted?

There will be two rounds of UK NEET counselling in 2023. If any seats remain empty after the second round, a third round, often known as a mop-up round, will be held. Please read the entire post to get more about the important dates for Uttarakhand NEET 2023 counselling.

What is the official website for UK NEET Counselling 2023?

Hnbumuexams.com is the designated website for Uttarakhand NEET counselling 2023. Therefore, candidates who are interested can register themselves and submit an application for Uttarakhand NEET Counselling 2023.

What is the state quota for NEET in Uttarakhand?

Hemwati Nandan Bahuguna Uttarakhand Medical Education University (HNBUMU) will provide a category-by-category list of the Uttarakhand NEET-UG cut-off for 2022. The state counselling authority will announce the Uttarakhand NEET cutoff for 85% of State-quota seats in 2022.
