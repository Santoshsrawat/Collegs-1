Is direct admission in BITS Pilani the right choice for you?

Securing a direct admission in BITS Pilani can be a life-changing opportunity for students who aspire to excel in their chosen fields. It provides access to world-class education, state-of-the-art facilities, and a diverse and vibrant campus life. 

BITS was founded in 1964 and is currently regarded as one of the most elite and popular technical colleges in the world. BITS Pilani has grown over its glorious 58 years as an independent institution, opening four additional campuses in Goa (est. 2004), Hyderabad (est. 2008), Dubai (est. 2000), and Mumbai (est. 2021).

Thus, choosing to pursue your B.Tech. at BITS Pilani is undoubtedly a smart choice you can make to advance your career.

Understanding the Direct Admission Process in BITS Pilani

Admission in BITS Pilani is possible either through clearing BITSAT entrance exam or BITS Pilani direct admission through management quota. So, if you want to get admission then you have to sit for BITSAT 2023. It is basically a computer based online test for admission to Integrated First Degree programmes of BITS Pilani.

1.    BITSAT Exam

It is necessary to pass the BITSAT entrance exam by registering for BITSAT 2023 and taking an online examination in order to be admitted to BITS Pilani.

Eligibility criteria for direct admissions in BITS Pilani

Securing a direct admission in BITS Pilani requires meeting certain eligibility criteria. 

•    To be eligible, you must have completed your 12th grade or its equivalent examination from a recognized board, with a minimum aggregate of 75% in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. Additionally, you must have scored at least 60% marks individually in each of these subjects. 

It's important to note that meeting these criteria is just the first step towards your dream of studying at BITS Pilani.

The institution also considers the top 1% of the students from each board in the country, regardless of their score. This is known as the Board Merit Scheme (BMS) and can significantly enhance your chances of securing a direct admission. However, it's important to note that the BMS is applicable only to Indian nationals.

•    In addition to the academic requirements, BITS Pilani also takes into account your performance in the BITS Admission Test (BITSAT), which is a computer-based entrance exam conducted by the institution. 

Application process for direct admissions in BITS

The application process for direct admissions in Birla Institute of Technology and Science Pilani is straightforward and can be completed online. 

Step 1 - To start, you need to visit the official BITS Pilani website

Step 2 - Fill out the application form for participating in BITS Pilani direct admission 2023. Make sure to provide accurate and up-to-date information, as any discrepancies may lead to the rejection of your application.

Step 3 – With the application, you are also required to upload scanned copies of your photograph and signature.

NOTE - It's important to ensure that these documents meet the specified requirements in terms of size, format, and resolution. Any mistake may result in your application being deemed incomplete.

Step 4 - Once you have submitted your application, you will need to pay the application fee. The fee can be paid online using various payment methods.

Step 5 - After the successful payment, you will receive a confirmation email with your application number and other relevant details. It's crucial to keep this email safe, as it will be required for future reference.
