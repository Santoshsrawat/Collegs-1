Before discussing, the Top 10 Private Medical Colleges in Maharashtra, here are some things you should know about Maharashtra -

  • In Maharashtra, students from other states are only eligible for admission to medical colleges under the Management and NRI quotas, as the state is not open for general admission.
  • Medical colleges in Maharashtra generally provide a relatively lower fee structure, typically ranging from 7 to 11 lakhs. However, it's important to note that fee structures can vary and are subject to change. The mentioned fee range is tentative. 
  • In Maharashtra, there are category-wise fee structures available for students belonging to the OBC (Other Backward Classes) and SC/ST (Scheduled Castes/Scheduled Tribes) categories. 

It is crucial to be aware of these important factors before deciding to pursue MBBS in Maharashtra. 

Now, let's begin our discussion on the list of private medical colleges in Maharashtra. Our list of MBBS colleges in Maharashtra is based on several factors that we have taken into consideration. Such as -

  • Academics

  • Patient in-flow

Top 10 Private Medical College In Maharashtra For MBBS Are Listed Below:-

 

1. K.J Somaiya Medical College, Mumbai

At the top of our list of private medical colleges in Maharashtra is K J Somaiya Medical College, situated in the heart of Mumbai, which is a trust-run medical college operated by Somaiya Medical Trust. This medical college was established in the year 1991 and is affiliated with the Maharashtra University of Health Science.  

Over the years, K J Somaiya Medical College has gained prominence and earned a reputation for excellence, making it one of the best private medical colleges in Maharashtra. Its commitment to providing quality medical education is the best. 

K J Somaiya Medical College has adopted the Competency-Based Medical Education (CBME) curriculum starting in the academic year 2019. This curriculum aligns with the regulations for Graduate Medical Education and focuses on developing and assessing the competencies required for medical professionals.

Hospital:- This medical college's hospital has 425 beds and is equipped with the latest technology. K J Somaiya Hospital and Research Centre serve as the teaching hospital for K J Somaiya Medical College. In addition to its role in medical education, the hospital offers state-of-the-art healthcare facilities to the community. It is committed to providing high-quality medical services at affordable costs, catering to all sections of society.

Library:- The library at K J Somaiya Medical College is a spacious and well-equipped facility, capable of accommodating up to 200 people at a time. With a total area of 1172 square meters.

Courses:- K J Somaiya Medical College offers a diverse range of courses in both undergraduate (UG) and postgraduate (PG) programs, also MD and MS programs. The MBBS seats in KJ Somaiya Medical College are 100 which was increased to 50 in the academic year of 2021-22.

 

2. Dr. VasantRao Pawar Medical College Hospital and Research Center, Nasik

A vision to make health accessible to the lower classes of society led to the establishment of this hospital and educational institution in 1914. 

This medical college's unwavering commitment to the upliftment of marginalized communities has played a pivotal role in establishing its reputation as one of the best among the list top 10 private medical colleges in Maharashtra.

This college was established by Rajshree Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj with the aim of uplifting the poor and underprivileged sections of society. The noble vision behind the establishment of this college was to provide educational opportunities and empower individuals from marginalized backgrounds. 

As part of its commitment, there is no tuition fee for the students who belong to the SC/ST community. However, there may still be expenses related to food and accommodation that students need to bear.

Whereas, the medical college was established in 1990, with an annual intake capacity of 100. But now the total number of seats in Dr. VasantRao Pawar Medical College is 178 for PG and UG both.

Overview:- This medical institute is the best medical college in Maharashtra, not because of its facilities and infrastructure. Due to the strong commitment to upliftment and a firm vision of making education and healthcare accessible to all, this college aims to support students who may face financial constraints or societal pressures hindering their educational pursuits.

Right now, this medical college has 342 branches and cater to the educational need of 1,66,996 students. 

Courses:- The total number of seats for the MBBS in Dr. VasantRao Pawar Medical College Hospital and Research Center, Nasik is 120 seats, and for PG courses it is 58.

Fee:- Dr. VasantRao Pawar Medical College MBBS fees are divided as per the category of students -

  • For General category students, it is - Rs. 7,77,125
  • For OBC’c category students it is - Rs. 4,46,184
  • For SC/St students it is Rs. 39,125.

Hospital:- Over the years, the hospital affiliated with Dr. Vasant Rao Pawar Medical College has flourished and expanded into a modern, well-equipped super specialty hospital. The total number of beds available in Dr. VasantRao Pawar Medical College boasts a capacity of 1000 beds, offering a wide range of medical services and treatments. 

If we talk about the walk-in patient then it is also remarkable in the Hospital of Dr. Vasant Rao Pawar Medical College.

 

3. Terna Medical College, Navi Mumbai 

Established in 1991, Terna Medical College is affiliated with Maharashtra Universtity of Health, Nasik, and is recognized by the National Medical Commission. The remarkable track record of producing highly skilled healthcare professionals has significantly contributed to the college's reputation as one of the best private medical colleges in Maharashtra. 

The medical courses at Terna Medical College provide students with a comprehensive understanding of medicine and develop essential knowledge and skills for their careers.

Infrastructure:- The infrastructure at Terna Medical College is designed to provide students with all the essential facilities necessary for focused and effective study. Also, the college campus is equipped with well-designed classrooms, modern laboratories, and a well-stocked library.

Terna Medical College provides hostel facilities for both boys and girls. 

  • For Boys - 189 rooms.
  • For Girls - 186 rooms.

Fee:- Terna Medical College Navi Mumbai fees for the MBBS are different for students belonging to the different categories -

  • NRI Quota - Rs. 39, 02,200
  • Open seats - Rs. 11,02,200
  • OBC - Rs. 7,83,818
  • SC/ST - Rs. 4,02,000

Whereas, Terna Medical College Navi Mumbai fees for P.G is -

  • State Quota 50%  - Rs. 443500
  • Institutional quota or Management Quota 35% - Rs. 443500
  • NRI Quota 15 % - Rs. 443500

Moreover, the hostel fee and deposit included in this P.G. courses at Terna Medical College are Rs. 75000/- and Rs. 15000/- per year respectively.

Hospital:- There is a vast hospital and 750 teaching-bed facilities at Terna Medical College, which provides tremendous patient flow for the students. Such as -

  • General Medicine - 14924 (patient Flow)
  • Pediatrics - 3544 (Patient Flow)
  • General Surgery - 5650 

Each department has more than 1000 patients and there are more than 20 departments in total.

 

4. Dr. Ulhas Patil Medical College and Hospital, Jalgaon

Now, it’s time for the fourth college on our list of private medical colleges in Maharashtra and it is Maharashtra of Medical Sciences and Research, Latur. This medical college is recognized by the Medical Council of India and is affiliated with the Maharashtra Universtity of Health Science (MUHS).

Overview:- Dr. Ulhas Patil Medical College was founded in 2008 by the Godavari Foundation. This is one of the flagship institutions started by the Godavari Foundation, and it is spread over an area of 33 acres. 

Infrastructure:- Dr. Ulhas Patil Medical College and Hospital in Jalgaon is widely acknowledged and esteemed for its remarkable infrastructure, making it one of the top ten private medical colleges in Maharashtra. 

Facilities at this medical college include -

  • There are 4 lecture halls with a capacity of 120 students.
  • Smart and well-stocked Library with more than 10000 books.
  • Demonstration room, Museum, and advanced laboratory.

Also, there is a hostel facility at Dr. Ulhas Patil Medical College and Hospital, Jalgaon for both Boys and Girls.

How Many Seats Are For MBBS in Dr. Ulhas Patil Medical College and Hospital, Jalgaon?

  • There are a total of 200 MBBS seats in Dr. Ulhas Patil Medical College and Hospital, Jalgaon.

The distribution of MBBS seats at Dr. Ulhas Patil Medical College and Hospital, Jalgaon, according to the different categories -

  • For SC/ST -  13 seats and 7 seats.
  • OBC - 19 seats

Here is another college included in our list of the Top 10 private medical colleges in Maharashtra, along with comprehensive information that you may find useful.

 

5. Maharashtra Institute of Medical Science and Research, Latur 

Overview:- There is a lot of confusion among students about, whether IS MIMER private or Government. It is a private medical college that was founded in a small portion of Rajarshi Shahu College in 1990,  However, in 1992, it relocated to its current premises on Ambajogai Road, which spans an impressive 95 acres of land. 

Hospital:- The present premises comprise the college building, and the Yeshwantrao Chavan Rural Hospital building, and presently it has a 610-bed hospital with 10 major operation theatres.  

Moreover, the college is associated with a rural hospital named after the esteemed leader Shri Yeshwantrao Chavan. The hospital, with a capacity of 750 beds, began operating alongside the college in 1992.

Maharashtra Institute of Medical Science and Research (MIMSR), has rapidly gained prominence and recognition within a short period of time. It has earned its place among the top private medical colleges in Maharashtra, which proved its credibility to provide the best education and affordable health.

Infrastructure:- Every department at Maharashtra Institute of Medical Science and Research is equipped with well-furnished classrooms, clinic rooms, seminar halls, practical halls, laboratories, departmental libraries, and office rooms, as well as a well-equipped Dissection Hall and Autopsy room. 

The college and hospital at MIMER cover a total constructed area of 4,95,000 square feet. This extensive space accommodates 20 departments.

Hostel:- The hostel at MIMER is situated in a large and spacious area, providing separate accommodations for both male and female students. 

  • There are 220 students capacity at each hostel for boys and girls, separately.

Library:- The library is a famous spot in this medical college because of its environment and the vast number of books available in the college. The number of books in this medical college is 15803 and 137 journals. 

 

6. Ashwini Rural Medical College, Hospital and Research Centre, Kumbhari, Solapur

Sixth, on our List of Private medical colleges in Maharashtra is Ashwini Rural Medical College, Solapur. 

Overview:- This medical college was established in the year 2012-2013 with an annual intake of 100 MBBS seats. The hospital affiliated with the college is conveniently located on campus. Moreover, ARMC is affiliated with the Maharashtra Universtity of Health Science (MUHS) and follows the MCI norms in its curriculum and in infrastructure.

Infrastructure:- The college and hospital are located within a spacious campus, covering a significant area of 86,976.86 square meters. This campus is home to 15 buildings, which collectively house all the required facilities. These include well-equipped laboratories, lecture halls, and a teaching hospital with 520 beds, conforming to the norms set by the Medical Council of India (MCI).

The quality of education and the availability of facilities at this medical college are exceptional, which has led to its recognition as one of the best private medical colleges in Maharashtra.

Hospital:- Situated on a sprawling 27-acre land, the medical college encompasses a pollution-free and serene environment. It houses a 520-bedded hospital along with residential quarters for the staff.

 

7. Prakash Institute of Medical Science and Research, Sangli  

Overview:- Prakash Institute of Medical Science and Research, Sangli was established in the year 2014, it is a newly inaugurated medical college and now has been started to count among the Top Private Medical Colleges in Maharashtra because of its facilities and standard of education.

The annual intake for MBBS at Prakash Institute of Medical Science and Research, Sangli is 150.  In addition, PIMS (the medical college) is affiliated with the esteemed Maharashtra University of Health Sciences, Nashik. 

Hospital:- This medical college offers a wide range of departments to cater to various medical needs. On an average day, it attends to an impressive number of patients, approximately 658.

Library:- The library of the college is situated on the first floor of the building, occupying an area of 2,450 square meters. 

Also, the library boasts an extensive collection of 11,917 books, which includes 5,479 textbooks, 2,465 titles, 6,438 reference books, 107 other books, 517 e-books, and access to 1,260 e-journals.

 

8. Smt. Kashibai Navale Medical College and General Hospital, Pune

Overview:- Smt. kashibai navale medical college is a full-fledged medical college that is located in Pune, Maharashtra was established in the year 2007. This medical college’s steadfast dedication to providing affordable education and delivering exceptional healthcare services has earned it a well-deserved spot on the list of the best private medical colleges in Maharashtra. 

Also, this medical college has gained a strong reputation for its academic excellence and exceptional patient services, extending its influence beyond the city of Pune to the entirety of Maharashtra. 

Hospital:- Initially, this medical college had 300 beds. However, over time, it has expanded significantly and currently boasts a total of 1,094 beds. Among these beds, the total number of teaching beds in Smt. Kashibai Navale Medical College and General Hospital, Pune are 740 beds. The initial intake for MBBS was 100, but now Smt. Kashibai Navale Medical College, Pune has 150 MBBS seats.

Beds Information of Smt. Kashibai Navale Medical College and General Hospital, Pune -

  • 80 ICU BEDS
  • 40 Casualty Beds
  • 20 Beds for Oral
  • 64 Daycare beds

With a daily average bed occupancy of 84%, and number shows that the hospital is consistently serving a significant number of patients. This increased patient volume indicates that students at the medical college have ample opportunities for medical exposure and practical learning.

Fee:- The most common question among the students is What is the fee of SMT Kashibai Navale Medical College? 

Well, the fee of SMT Kashibai Navale Medical College is Rs. 11,96,428 for the General Category students.

 

9. Dr. Vithalrao Vikhe Patil Foundation's Medical College and Hospital, Ahmednagar

This is a trust-run medical college that is run by the Dr. Vithalrao Vikhe Patil Foundation and this hospital has emerged as one of the best among the Top 10 Private Medical Colleges in Maharashtra.

Overview:- Due to the severe shortage of medical facilities in the region, Dr. Vithalrao Vikhe Patil Foundation recognized and established a medical college at Vilad Ghat, Ahmednagar. This decision was made in 2004 with the aim of addressing the lack of quality healthcare services in the community.

Hospital:- The hospital affiliated with this medical college spans an expansive area of 25 acres. The establishment of the entire medical college, including the 500-bed teaching hospital, took five years to complete. The hospital is constructed in accordance with the guidelines and norms set by the Medical Council of India (MCI).

In addition to the 500-bed teaching hospital, Dr. Vikhe Patil Memorial Hospital, affiliated with the medical college, further enhances the healthcare infrastructure. The hospital itself has over 960 beds.

Courses:- The courses available in this medical college are -

  • UG 
  • PG
  • Super-speciality

The number of seats for MBBS available in the Dr. Vithalrao Vikhe Patil Foundation's Medical College is 200. 

Location

  • By air, the nearest airport to Dr. Vithalrao Vikhe Patil Foundation's Medical College and Hospital in Ahmednagar is Shirdi Airport, which is approximately 74 km away.
  • By railway, the nearest railway station is Ahmednagar Railway Station which is 14 km away from the campus.

Last, on our list of the Top 10 Private Medical Colleges in Maharashtra is B.K.L Walawalkar Rural Medical College, Ratnagiri. Here is the introduction to this renowned medical college.

 

10. B.K.L Walawalkar Rural Medical College, Ratnagiri

The establishment of B.K.L Walawalkar Rural Medical College, Ratnagiri was led by the trust named Shri Vithalrao Joshi Charities. Established in 2015, it is affiliated with the Maharashtra University of Health Science. 

Hospital:- In order to provide quality medical care to the most difficult-to-reach areas, the Shree Vitthalrao Joshi Charities Trust took the initiative to establish the B.K.L. Walawalkar Hospital in Ratnagiri district in 1996.

The Hospital at B.K.L Walawalkar Rural Medical College, Ratnagiri has a total of 500 beds. Being situated in a remote area of the Konkan region of Maharashtra, the institution faces unique geographical challenges, but it still has a large patient flow and patients also come from the Maharashtra main city.

Seats:- The total seat distribution in B.K.L Walawalkar Rural Medical College, Ratnagiri is -

  • NEET Merit Seats - 128
  • NRI Seats - 22

Fee:- The total fee for the PG in M.D is -

  •  Rs. 13,62,700 for state quota students 
  • Rs . 4,842,70 for Institutional quota students.
  • Rs. 6,002,700 for NRI Quota Student
