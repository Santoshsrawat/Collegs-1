Eligibility Criteria for Kerala NEET Counselling 2023

  • Educational Qualification

Students should have Physics, Chemistry and Biology as compulsory subjects with a minimum score of 50% marks (40% for reserve category candidates) in 12th class

  • Age Limit

All applicants for the MBBS Kerala NEET Counselling in 2023 must be 17 years of age or older as of December 31, 2023.

  • Qualify NEET

Only after qualifying NEET i.e. National Entrance cum Eligibility Test exam, any medical student will be eligible to apply for Kerala NEET Counselling 2023

  • Nationality

The universities of Kerala allow Indian nationals, non-resident Indians (NRIs), Overseas Citizens of India (OCIs), Person of Indian Origin (PIOs) and Foreign Nationals to apply for MBBS or other medical courses.

Kerala NEET UG Counselling 2023 Procedure     

Step 1: Kerala NEET Counselling 2023 Registration

Every medical aspirant is first required to register on the KEAM website, www.cee.kerala.gov.in, and complete the necessary payments of Rs. 500. After then, students can log in and review the seating chart to make their selections.

Step 2: Fill the Kerala NEET Counselling form

After creating id and password, student is required to fill the Kerala NEET Counselling form and take a print out of the same and pay the fees accordingly.

The authorities will publish the seat vacancy details for each round of counseling. A merit list will also be published based on the NEET scores and other eligibility criteria.

Step 3: Upload

When applying for Kerala NEET Counselling, some of the documents that will be required to submit are as  follows :-

  • NEET Admit Card
  • Rank Card
  • 12th marksheet
  • Signature of student and parents
  • Thumb impression of student

Step 4: Choice filling

Students can start filling out the choices once the results of the mock test are announced. Since there can be no additions once the selections have been made, it is a crucial step that calls for wise decision-making. Rearranging or deleting is permitted, though.

Step 5: Reporting

After the declaration of final result, make the payment and then student will be required to report to KEAM and then finally report to the college allotted.
 

NOTE : It is important to note that the registration process will be open only once before the first round of counseling, and candidates must ensure that they complete all the necessary steps within the given deadlines.

Choice After Allotment of Seat

  • Option 1 - In the event that you are satisfied with your allotment, you may report to the college to which you have been assigned. Kerala Domicile     
  • Option 2 - You can opt for up-gradation in the next round if you are satisfied, but wish to retain your seat in the future.
  • Option 3 - You can skip reporting if you are not satisfied and do not want to participate in the next round.

 

Salient Features of Kerala NEET Counselling 2023

  • There is no requirement for a security deposit.
  • The registration process is available only before the first round.
  • Physical document verification will be conducted during the reporting process.
  • Students from other states can apply for admission through the management quota in private medical colleges.
  • The fee for private medical colleges is still being finalized and is subject to revision.
  • After being allotted a seat in the first round, candidates can withdraw from the process.
  • Candidates are allowed to shuffle their choices in the second round.

Types Of Seats in Kerala Medical Colleges

  1. Government – In Government Medical College, students holding Kerala’s Domicile can apply.
  2. Self-financed Private – These seats are non-subsidized seats in Self Financing Medical Colleges. (Only Kerala Domicile can apply)
  3. Management – Students from all over India can apply for Management quota seats in Private institutions.

Why MBBS from Kerala?

Kerala, the cleanest state in India, is a wonderful place that is highly valued by people. It is the only place on earth with such vibrant and have natural and exotic beauty like no other place.

Speaking from a study standpoint as well, Kerala is in the lead.

It provides the most affordable medical programs without sacrificing academic quality.  The majority of the colleges provide students with excellent academics and training, as well as a large patient intake to ensure that each student receives the greatest possible experience of training.

The NEET 2023 exam is scheduled to be held on May 07, 2023, and it serves as the gateway for aspiring medical students to secure admission into top medical colleges across India.

Our FAQ's

Who is eligible for KEAM 2023?

For KEAM 2023, every citizen of India is eligible to apply including Person of Indian Origin/Overseas Citizen of India.

Is CEE and KEAM same in Kerala?

KEAM i.e. Kerala Engineering, Architecture and Medical Admission Exam, is basically a state-level entrance examination which is conducted by the Commission of Entrance Examination (CEE).

What is the registration site for KEAM 2023?

The official registration site for KEAM 2023 is www.cee.kerala.gov.in which the students will be required to visit to fill the application form of KEAM.

What does KEAM stands for?

KEAM stands for “Kerala Engineering Architecture Medical”. It is the Governing body that is responsible for admission to various professional degree courses in the state of Kerala.

When to register for KEAM exam 2023?.

The KEAM application process began on March 17 and the KEAM 2023 registration last date is 10th March 2023. A student must complete the form as soon as possible because it rarely gets opened again. So, do not think that forms will be available after NEET results. As the forms are made available in Kerala prior to the release of the NEET results.

Who conducts Kerala NEET Counselling?

The Commissioner of Entrance Examination (CEE) is the main authority responsible for conducting Kerala NEET Counselling.
