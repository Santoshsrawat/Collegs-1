Top Private Medical Colleges In Haryana

Team getmyuniversity 08 May 2023 10906 views

When we discuss the top private medical colleges in Haryana, there are a total of six private medical colleges that we typically consider. Each of these colleges has its own unique features and characteristics.

There is only one college that is completely reserved for students living in Haryana and it is Maharaja Agrasen Medical College. This medical college has 100% reserved seats for students who have domicile in Haryana state.

And, Maharaja Agrasen Medical College has a government-aided fee structure that provides benefits exclusively to students who are domiciled in Haryana. All medical colleges in Haryana except for AL-Falah have a 50% quota reserved seats for the Haryana Domicile student in medical programs.

When the Get My University team visited Haryana to gain insight into the medical colleges, we received mixed feedback about the situated colleges. Some colleges are well-established, with faculty members in place, and have strong academics, while others have space for improvement and development. We also witnessed some gaps in the academic and infrastructure of some colleges. 

Before making your decision about the medical colleges in Haryana, make sure to think about several important factors. It is crucial to take the time to weigh these factors and gather as much information as possible about the colleges.

And these factors are-

  • Academic 
  • Patient in flow 
  • Infrastructure 

With the help of this composition, we will provide you with insights into the medical colleges in Haryana and the factors associated with them. 

Top Private Medical Colleges In Haryana Are Given Below:-

 

1. Maharaja Agrasen Medical College, Hisar

Named after the divine king “Maharaja Agrasen” a revered king who worked tirelessly for the welfare of his people. Now his legacy is continued by this one of the Top private medical colleges in Haryana that provides quality education and selfless medical services to the people. 

The medical college operates under a semi-government setup, and only students from the state can be admitted to the college. Moreover, there are no additional bond conditions imposed on the students. That is the biggest advantage of this medical college, unlike other government medical colleges, there is no bond condition for students.

Maharaja Agrasen Medical College's fee structure is regulated by the government, resulting in a very low fee of only 1.80 lahks per annum. The popularity of this medical college can be measured by the extent to which students prefer it over government colleges.

Overview

On April 18th, 1988, the Maharaja Agrasen Medical Education & Scientific Research Society was founded in Agroha, Haryana. Its purpose was to offer medical education and affordable healthcare services to the people of Haryana and of other states. This medical college is affiliated with  Pandit Bhagwat Dayal Sharma University of Health Sciences, Rohtak, and is recognized by the Medical Council of India (MCI).

MAMC has built and maintained its infrastructure excellently, ensuring that it is suitable for students who choose to pursue their medical education there.

There is the availability of -

  • Academic Facilities
  • Hospital
  • Residential Complex
  • Cafeteria and mess

Hospital

With 550 beds, the hospital can accommodate a significant number of patients, and it receives an impressive 2500 OPD (Out-Patient Department) visits each day. This provides ample opportunities for students to gain practical experience.

Courses

The recent intake for MBBS has increased from 50 to 100, and the number of seats available for M.Sc. nursing is  50 seats. When we talk about P.G. then it is 52 seats in various medical courses.

Now this medical college has started PG Courses in new 11 Departments and 5 DNB Departments. 

 Hostel

The medical college has separate hostels for girls and boys. The girls' hostel has a total of 42 rooms, while the boys' hostel has 52 rooms

Library

The Institute boasts a library that houses 9,926 books on a range of medical science topics. Additionally, the library subscribes to approximately 150 national and international medical journals. 

Fee

The total amount that has been adjusted for admission to this medical college is Rs. 2,49,340/- for MBBS. Whereas, for PG Fee are as per follows- 

  • For the first year- It is 1,68,250/- 
  • For the second year, it is 1,62,250/- 
  • For last year it is Rs. 1,82,250/-

The foundation of this medical college was laid down to bring international medical education within the reach of the students of Haryana, moreover, this medical college is committed to maintaining excellence in healthcare and in medical education. 

 

2. SGT Medical College

Apart from Maharaja Agrasen Medical College, SGT Medical College is considered one of the Top private medical colleges in Haryana.

The tuition fees of SGT Medical College may be relatively high, but the academic standards are robust and the faculty is supportive, which results in a high pass rate. Additionally, as it is a university campus, students are exposed to a vibrant atmosphere inside this medical college.

The college's location in close proximity to the city area is a significant reason why it is considered one of the top private medical colleges in the region, and its growth has been consistent over the years. 

It is possible for students with lower scores to get admission to this medical college. Furthermore, some students might find the cost of attending this medical college higher than other colleges in the region, but we can assure you the fee is justified by the quality of education.

Overall, SGT Medical College is a great option for those looking to pursue a career in the medical field.

Overview

SGT Medical College is one of the top private medical colleges in Haryana. It is located in Gurugram. The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various medical fields, including MBBS, MD, MS, and many more.

The SGT University is part of the Dashmesh Educational Charitable Trust, founded in 1999.  This university has a variety of branches of study. 

The Faculty of Medical and Health Sciences was established in 2010 and has since been steadily growing and producing highly skilled healthcare professionals. This medical university's facilities are modern and well-equipped, providing students with access to the latest equipment and resources.

Courses

The annual intake of MBBS in this medical college is 150, whereas seats for PG are 73 in 20 departments. 

Hospital 

SGT Medical College has a total of 810 beds, out of which 720 are teaching beds, 61 are critical care beds, and 29 are special beds. This means that students have ample opportunities to gain hands-on experience in various medical settings and specializations.

Hostel 

Shree Guru Gobind Singh Tricentenary University is spread over 70 acres. The campus of the SGT Medical College is surrounded by greenery, and its infrastructure is designed beautifully with well-furnished and spacious academic buildings. The college also provides sports facilities and comfortable residential facilities for its staff and well-furnished hostels for students.

The college provides transportation services with a fleet of 37 buses that cover various routes across NCR and nearby regions for the convenience of both students and staff. Hostel residents can also avail of bus facilities for city visits, and the transportation charges are based on actual usage.

Fee

According to the information provided, the total cost of the MBBS program at SGT Medical College is 89.93 lakh. This includes a tuition fee of 18 lakh rupees, with the first year's fee being Rs.21,54,00 and the second year's fee being Rs. 27 lakhs.

 

3. Adesh Medical College, Kurukshetra 

Adesh Medical College is a private medical college located in Shahabad Markanda, Haryana, India. It was established in 2017 and is affiliated with Pandit Bhagwat Dayal Sharma University of Health Sciences, Rohtak. The college offers undergraduate (MBBS) and postgraduate (MD/MS) courses in various medical disciplines. The total size of the area in which this college is located at 29 acres.

We have witnessed growth in the Adesh Medical College, either it is by academic and Patient inflow has also seen growth. 

Currently, there is an opportunity for growth in both faculty and healthcare at Adesh Medical College, which could potentially lead to it becoming a highly esteemed medical college. 

Despite this potential, Adesh medical college is still the preferred choice for many students due to its lower fee structure, compared to the aforementioned college, SGT. The cut-off at Adesh Medical Collge remains high,

Overview

This medical college is run under the aegis of the Adesh Welfare Society, which established itself in 2012.  Adesh Medical College is affiliated with 

The campus is spread over 100 acres and available with the state of art facilities and enough facilities to structure your career. 

Hospital

Adesh Medical College has a total of 750 beds distributed across different departments for clinical training and patient care. And, the daily OPD in this medical college is 500 to 600 on average.  

In the recent time we have seen patient flow has been improved.

Courses 

Both, UG and PG courses are provided here at this medical college, the number of MBBS seats is 150, and PG setas are 44 seats in the 14 departments.

Location

The location of this medical college is near Ambala city, which is a city in the northern Indian state of Haryana. Specifically, the college is located just 10 km away from the Ambala Junction railway station, which makes it easily accessible by train. 

Campus Highlights

  • 6 labs
  • Central Library
  • Skill lab
  • Laundry facility

Fee

The total cost of pursuing an MBBS in Haryana at this college is Rs. 78,65,000. The fee for the first year of the program is Rs. 14,80,000, and the tuition fee for the entire course is Rs. 12,00,000. For the second year of the program, the fee is Rs. 13,70,000.

 

4. NC Medical College, Panipat, Haryana

The NC Medical College is affiliated with the Pt. B.D. Sharma University of Medical Sciences, Panipat and is recognized by NMC and this was established in 2016. This medical college is situated at a distance of 20 kilometers from the city of Panipat.

Upon conducting an in-depth review, it was discovered that there is a pressing need for improvements in the medical college to provide students with more medical exposure and to enhance the infrastructure for a better academic experience.

And, there is no development in the adjoining area of this campus.

Since the college is located at a considerable distance from the city, you would need to adjust and make do with the facilities available within the campus as there are no amenities outside of it. But it can be managed if you learn to settle with the medical college.

Courses

Both PG and UG courses are available at NC Medical College. The number of seats available for MBBS is 150 and seats for PG is 27 in 10 departments.

Hospital

A hospital is available with 650 teaching beds, and 50 High Dependency Beds. And the hospital is located on the campus of NC medical college. Moreover, the outpatient department receives 300 to 400 patients daily.

Location

The college is situated on NH-71A (Rohtak-Panipat Road), approximately 14 km away from the city of Gohana Mod (Panipat). The college has good connectivity with roads and railways, making it easily accessible from various parts of the state and country. The Indira Gandhi International Airport in Delhi and the airport in Chandigarh offer domestic and international flights, providing convenient travel options for visiting the college campus.

UG Passing %

When we talk about the UG passing % it is 138 out of 145. 

Infrastructure 

The hostel is available for both boys and girls in the residential complex of the college.

Also -

  • Basic Infrastructure
  • AC classroom
  • Guest House 
  • Cafeteria
  • Central Library
  • Common room

Fee

The total cost of the MBBS program in Haryana at NC Medical College is 76.90 lakhs. The fee for the first year is 15,04,400 rupees, with a tuition fee of 12 lakhs.

 

5. AL-Falah Medical College

Al-Falah Medical College is a private medical college located in Faridabad, Haryana, India. It was established in 2019 and is affiliated with the Pt. B.D. Sharma University of Health Sciences, Rohtak. The college offers undergraduate courses in Medicine and Surgery (MBBS) and is recognized by the Medical Council of India (MCI).

This medical university is an endeavor of the charitable trust of the Al-Falah.

Overview 

Al-Falah is considered by the students because it is located nearly to the city of Delhi, and it is spread over 78 acres of campus.

It is underdeveloping college it will take time to grow this college.  Based on student feedback, it seems that there are frequent power outages at this medical college. This can be a significant inconvenience and can disrupt students' studies, 

The faculty at the college is generally good, particularly in the Department of Physiology and Biology. However, the Anatomy department is lacking in certain areas and requires attention from both the management and the department in order to improve and elevate the standards of the medical college.

Although the out-patient ratio of this college is good, the in-patient department is currently weak and in need of further development. Improvement in these areas is necessary for the college to grow.

According to feedback received from students, the rooms in this medical college are not as per the requirements. The management of this medical college needs to focus and invest more attention in order to improve and uplift its standards.

Courses

The number of MBBS seats available in this medical college is 150, PG has only 1 department which is a para clinic.

Hospital

Al-Falah Charitable Trust established a dispensary in 1997 to cater to the medical needs of students, staff, and the local community. 

This initiative paved the way for the growth of Al-Falah Hospital, which expanded into a 378 bedded super-specialty hospital in 2014. The hospital is located within the expansive 72-acre campus of Al-Falah University and provides medical services to the students, staff, and local population.

There are 4 functional operating rooms inside this medical college and doctors with 24/7 availability. There are 300 averages in the OPD of the alfalfa medical college. 

Central Library 

The library has a seating room that is 1253 square feet long and the total size of the library is 10880 sq. feet. And, the total number of books available in the library is 3200.

Location

The nearest railway station is the Delhi railway station which is 25 km. away from the campus.

Hostel

The hostel is available for both boys and girls that are available for 135 boys and 114 girls. 

Fee

The total package of MBBS in Al- Falah Medical College is 78 lakh and the first-year tuition fee is 18,45,000 and the tuition fee is 12.75 lakh.

6. World College of Medical Science and Research Jhajjar, Haryana

World Medical College, Jhajjar, and Hospital are affiliated with Pandit Bhagwat Dayal Sharma University of Health Sciences, Rohtak.  It was established in the year 2008 and provides UG and PG both in medical. 

During our visit to the World College of Medical Sciences and Research, we observed that there was a weak emphasis on clinical exposure for the students. We found that the college had to rely on other medical institutions located a bit far away to provide its students with clinical exposure. 

This dependence on other colleges for medical exposure suggests that the college may not be adequately equipped to provide its students with the necessary clinical training to become successful medical professionals.

It appears that there are some gaps or areas for improvement in the World College of Medical Sciences and Research, which may take some time to address. While it is important to recognize and address these gaps,

Courses

Both, PG and UG are available in this medical college and the seat for the MBBS is 150. 

Library 

The World College of Medical Sciences and Research has a centralized library located in the teaching block, covering an area of 2408.12 square meters. The library provides separate reading rooms for students, as well as for faculty and staff members. It is equipped with a collection of 11,290 books, including both national and foreign journals.

Academic

The academic setting at the World College of Medical Sciences and Research is conducive to the optimal development of medical students. This means that the learning environment provides an atmosphere that is ideal for students to excel academically and personally.
