NEET UG 2023 Result

Team getmyuniversity 13 Jun 2023 1314 views

According to NEET Result 2023 Live Updates, the National Testing Agency will shortly release the NEET UG 2023 results of the National Eligibility Completion Entrance Test – Undergraduate or NEET UG 2023.

More than 20.87 lakh medical aspirants registered for the NEET exam in 2023, breaking the record that was set in the previous year. This number makes it clear that as interest in the medical area has grown, so has the level of competition.

However, it would be best to maintain your sense of self-confidence and trust in yourself. Your patience is being tested during this period. Keep yourself cool until the NEET UG 2023 result is released.

To check NEET UG 2023 Result, students are advised to check the official website at neet.nta.nic.in. Medical students will get NEET UG 2023 scorecard after submitting the roll number and birthdate.

The NEET entrance exam was given across India on May 7, 2023. In contrast, the exam was held in Manipur on June 6, 2023. On June 13th 2023, the official answer key was made public. And after the results are announced, the counselling process will start within two to three weeks.

Steps to Check NEET UG Result 2023

Here are the steps that you will have to perform when NEET UG 2023 Result is out, namely:-

STEP 1: Visit the official website at www.neet.nta.nic.in

STEP 2 : On the Homepage of the National Testing Agency, you will see the heading named as “Student”.  From the given head click on “NATIONAL ELIGIBILITY CUM ENTRANCE TEST”.

STEP 3 : A new page shall be opened in front of you where you will be required to fill the required details in order to login.

STEP 4 : Enter the credentials allotted to you for NEET Exam and the following details :

  • Roll Number
  • Date of Birth
  • Security PIN

STEP 5 : After filling the details, submit it and then you will see your NEET UG ScoreCard 2023 displayed on your screen.

STEP 6 : Download and take a printout of the NEET UG 2023 Result as it will be required in future.

NEET UG 2023 Result Validity

The NTA NEET Result 2023 scores are only good for the current academic year, or one year, while applying to Indian colleges. However, the MCC has declared that for applicants seeking admission to international medical institutes, the NEET Result 2023's validity has been increased to three years. The decision to extend the validity was made in consideration of the potential length of the admissions procedure to international institutions.

Our FAQ's

How can I download the NEET UG 2023 Result?

From the official website, you can easily download the NEET UG 2023 Result. Read the blog and learn all the steps that will help you get your NEET UG ScoreCard.

How is NEET Cut off determined?

On the basis of the difficulty of the NEET entrance exam, the number of medical aspirants appearing as well as the availability of MBBS seats in medical colleges, NEET UG CUT OFF is released.

Who releases the NEET Result 2023?

The National Testing Agency is the authorized body that releases the NEET Result 2023 on the official website i.e. neet.nta.nic.in.

What is the result date of NEET 2023?

It is likely to say that the NEET 2023 result shall be declared on 13th June 2023. However, as per our belief, you can expect it within this week itself.

What is meant by Tie Breaking Rule?

Where two medical student score exactly the same marks in the NEET exam, then the following tie-breaking rules become applicable, namely:- 1 If a student has scored well in Biology then on the basis of it, he or she is allotted a rank followed by candidates security marking in chemistry and then lastly in Physics. 2 Where still, the tie continues to exist, then in that case a student who has got less number of negative marks will be given preference over other students who has more negative marks. 3 And if still it continues, then the student securing less negative marks in Biology subject is allotted a better rank followed by chemistry and physics.
