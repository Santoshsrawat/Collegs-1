Guru Govind Singh Medical College Faridkot

Guru Gobind Singh Medical College, commonly known as GGSMC, is a prestigious medical institution located in Faridkot, Punjab, India. Established in 1973, it is one of the renowned government medical colleges in Punjab. The college is affiliated with Baba Farid University of Health Sciences and offers undergraduate and postgraduate medical programs.

GGSMC offers an undergraduate program leading to the Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS) degree. The college also provides postgraduate programs in various specialties, including MD, MS, and diploma courses, allowing students to pursue advanced specialization in their chosen fields.

The college is known for its faculty members, who employ innovative teaching methodologies and encourage interactive learning to foster the holistic development of students. GGSMC has a well-equipped teaching hospital, known as Guru Gobind Singh Medical College and Hospital, on its campus. Students gain valuable clinical exposure, hands-on experience, and a chance to observe and participate in various medical procedures under the supervision of skilled professionals.

The college campus boasts a modern infrastructure, including well-equipped classrooms, laboratories, libraries, and sports facilities.

GGSMC promotes research and encourages students and faculty members to engage in research activities. The institution provides resources and support for research projects, fostering a culture of inquiry and innovation. 

In summary, Guru Gobind Singh Medical College in Faridkot is a distinguished medical institution that offers quality education, clinical exposure, and research opportunities to medical students. With its experienced faculty, well-equipped hospital, and emphasis on research and innovation, GGSMC provides a comprehensive learning experience for aspiring healthcare professionals.

Guru Gobind Singh Medical College Location Details

Guru Gobind Singh Medical College (GGSMC) is located in Faridkot, Punjab, India. Faridkot enjoys good connectivity through various modes of transportation. The nearest airport is Sri Guru Ram Dass Jee International Airport in Amritsar, which is approximately 100 kilometers away from Faridkot. The airport operates domestic and international flights, providing convenient travel options for students and faculty.

The city is well-connected by rail and road networks. Faridkot Railway Station is the main railway station serving the city, connecting it to different parts of the country through regular train services. National highways and state highways pass through Faridkot, providing easy access to neighboring cities and states.
