Admission Process

Maharishi Markandeshwar Medical College & Hospital Solan

The Department of medical education is the sole body in Himachal Pradesh that conducts counseling for MBBS admission in Himachal colleges. 

There are 6 Govt/1 AIIMS & 1 Private medical college in Himachal Pradesh- which offer 763 M.B.B.S Seats. The Counseling process conducts in 3 rounds, the first 2 rounds are offline and the last round conducts in offline mode. 

The seat allotment for AIIMS is done through a centralized counseling process conducted by the Medical Counseling Committee (MCC).

Himachal Pradesh is an Open State, where students from other states can participate in the counseling for management quota seats in Private and NRI seats in Government.

STEPS IN THE COUNSELING PROCESS Of HIMACHAL PRADESH:

  1. Eligibility criteria

Only the candidates, who have qualified for the NEET UG 2023 will be eligible to apply for MBBS Admission in Himachal Pradesh, with a centralized counseling process. 

To be eligible for admission to medical courses in India, a candidate needs to fulfill certain criteria that demonstrate their academic, and medical capability.

  • Candidates must have completed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Biotechnology.
  • English as the main subject with a minimum of 50% marks (40% for SC/ST/OBC candidates).

2. IF there are any students whose parents have no ethnicity of Himachal Pradesh and if they are government servants or working within the state of Himachal Pradesh, then their kids are eligible under the following conditions :

  • To meet the eligibility criteria for certain benefits or schemes in the state of Himachal Pradesh, an employee must have worked continuously for a minimum period of two years within the state. 

Additionally, the employee's child must have completed their 10+1 and 10+2 education from recognized schools or colleges located within the state and affiliated with ICSE, CBSE, HPBOSE, or equivalent boards and universities established by Indian law. 

  • Candidates are perfectly eligible for applying for MBBS admission in Himachal Pradesh if they fulfill the requirement mentioned above.

Eligibility Condition For Management Quota :

  • Private unaided Medical/Dental Colleges in Himachal Pradesh will fill their Management Quota Seats with candidates who have qualified in NEET-UG-2022.
  • A common merit list will be published, which will not consider the candidate's residence status or schooling condition in Himachal Pradesh.

Entrance examination

The candidate needs to appear for the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET) conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA). The NEET score is used for admission to MBBS Admission in Himachal Pradesh. 

Registration

After the NEET results are declared, the candidate needs to register on the official website of the Himachal Pradesh University, Shimla.

Choice filling 

The candidate needs to select the colleges and courses in which they wish to take admission. The selection must be made based on the availability of seats, previous year's cut-off, and the candidate's NEET score.

Seat allotment 

The seats are allotted based on the candidate's NEET score, choice of colleges and courses, and availability of seats. Meeting the MBBS admission requirements in Himachal Pradesh is sufficient to be considered for a seat allotment.

Document verification 

The candidate needs to carry the original and photocopies of all the necessary documents for verification at the time of admission. Document verification is a very important aspect of the MBBS admission in Himachal Pradesh to verify the very details of candidates. 

Admission fee payment

After the documents are verified, the candidate needs to pay the admission fee to confirm their admission.
