T D Medical College Alleppey Allappuzha

Established in 1963, TD Medical College is affiliated with the KUHS and offers undergraduate (MBBS) and postgraduate (MD/MS) programs in various medical specialties. The MBBS course has an intake capacity of approximately 150 students per year. Admissions to the college are based on entrance examinations conducted at the state level.

The campus of T D Medical College is equipped with modern infrastructure and facilities to support the academic and research needs of the students. Boasting modern amenities like laboratories, halls, and classes, the college also provides sports facilities to the students for rejuvenation. 

The college also provides separate hostels for male and female students, ensuring a comfortable and conducive living environment.

One unique aspect of T D Medical College is its location in Alappuzha, which is renowned for its backwaters and scenic beauty. The college offers a serene and picturesque environment for learning, creating a pleasant atmosphere for students to pursue their medical education.

The college is a top choice amongst budding medicos for its panel of expert faculty members as they are experts in their respective fields and provide comprehensive theoretical knowledge and practical training. They also actively engage in research activities, contributing to advancements in medical science.

T D Medical College Hospital, associated with the college, serves as the teaching hospital and is a major healthcare facility in the region. The hospital provides a wide range of medical services to the community and offers excellent clinical exposure and practical training opportunities for the students. This exposure helps them gain hands-on experience in treating diverse medical conditions.

Recognized by MCI, the T D Medical College in Alappuzha is a reputed institution that combines academic excellence with a beautiful location. It provides a conducive learning environment, experienced faculty, and ample clinical exposure, making it an excellent choice for aspiring medical professionals seeking a well-rounded education.

T D Medical College Alappuzha Location Details

The college is situated in the Vandanam area of Alappuzha, is well-connected by road, and is easily accessible from various parts of the district.

Admission Process

T D Medical College Alleppey Allappuzha

Kerala is one of India's best states for MBBS courses, as it has a rich diversity, culture, and natural vegetation to assist you in your quality medical education.  Also, Kerala has a plethora of well-reputed medical colleges in the country which offer Medical Education at the lowest possible costs.  

To make sure that you have a smooth and hassle-free MBBS admission in Kerala, it is important that you have a thorough understanding of all the important details regarding admission. But, before taking admission in this state, it is advised that you have ample knowledge about the registration process for MBBS counseling in Kerala. 

The Process for MBBS Kerala Counseling includes a counseling process for 3182 MBBS and 2077 BDS seats that will be allotted in the government and private medical colleges in Kerala. 

The process of counseling for MBBS in Kerala is conducted by the Commissioner of Entrance Examination Kerala. NEET counseling is usually conducted online through Centralised Admission Process (CAP) in three stages, including a mop-up round. 

Requirement of the admission process 

Eligibility Criteria: The student is required to clear or passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Biotechnology and English as mandatory subjects and must have achieved a minimum of 50% marks in combined of all subjects (40% marks for SC/ST/OBC candidates). If a candidate is filling all the criteria then they are qualified for NEET-UG.

Application Process: The application process for MBBS admission in Kerala is done online. Candidates have to go to the official website of the Commissioner for Entrance Examinations (CEE) and have to fill up the application form. The fee for the application forms for General category candidates is Rs. 1000 and for SC/ST candidates, it is Rs. 500.

Counseling Process: The counseling process for MBBS admission in Kerala is conducted online. Candidates who have qualified for NEET-UG and have applied for admission in Kerala will be called for counseling based on their rank in the NEET-UG exam. The counseling process will involve choice filling, seat allotment, and document verification.

Seat Reservation: Seat reservation is as per the Government of Kerala norms. 15% of the seats are reserved for the All India Quota (AIQ) and the remaining 15% of seats are filled through the state quota. Out of the state quota seats, 20% of seats are reserved for SC candidates, 15% for ST candidates, and 30% for SEBC candidates.

Documents Required -

Candidates must carry the following documents for the counseling process :

  • NEET-UG admit card and scorecard
  • 10th and 12th mark sheets and certificate
  • Transfer certificate (TC)
  • Migration certificate (if applicable)
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)
  • Non-Creamy Layer certificate (if applicable)
  • Domicile certificate (if applicable)
  • Medical fitness certificate

Fee Structure: The fee structure for MBBS admission in Kerala varies from college to college. However, the approximate fee for a government medical college is around Rs. 25,000 per annum, and for a private medical college, it ranges from Rs. 5-10 lakhs per annum.

Please note that Kerala is the only state which closes its registration before the NEET exam and many students miss this exam due to a lack of information. It is important to monitor and keep yourself updated about the important dates and notifications released by the CEE Kerala, so you don’t miss out on any important information.
