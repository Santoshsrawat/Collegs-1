World College of Medical Sciences & Researc Jhajjar Haryana

In 2016, the World College of Medical Sciences & Research was established with the aim of serving medical excellence and training doctors. The medical college is privately owned and is affiliated to Pandit Bhagwat Dayal Sharma University of Health Sciences, Rohtak.

The National Medical Council has recognised all of WCMSRH Jhajjar's undergraduate and graduate programmes (NMC). The popular MBBS course lasts for 5.5 years, including a year of internship, and there are 150 mbbs seats. The college is working to improve its faculty so that medical education is not compromised.

The Global Medical College Hospital includes 650 teaching beds, 94 non-teaching beds, and offers a wide range of multispecialty services. The patient inflow is discovered to be low right now, which requires for improvement.

Students can access a basic variety of services at World Medical College in Jhajjar, Haryana, including sports, a library, a blood bank, a canteen, and a hostel. The college's infrastructure should be improved by adding even more cutting-edge facilities.

World College of Medical Sciences & Research is located in the Jhajjar district of Haryana and provides great access to both railways and airport. As it will just take 48 minutes from Jhajjar Railway Station to reach the college campus and 1.19 hours from Indira Gandhi International Airport.

In terms of infrastructure, patient intake, and academics, WCMSRH Jhajjar has a lot of potential for development. The college can expand further and offer a variety of benefits to medical students to improve their medical expertise, which will undoubtedly result in more positive outcomes.

Admission Process

World College of Medical Sciences & Researc Jhajjar Haryana

Haryana MBBS Admission 2023 is about to start and it is important for aspirants to gather details about MBBS admission in Haryana such as :

  • Counseling Body - The Directorate of Medical Education (DMER), Haryana handles the counseling procedure for Haryana NEET 2023 admission.
  • Eligibility - To be eligible for admission into an MBBS program in Haryana, the candidate must be at least 17 years of age. Additionally, if a candidate wants to claim admission to a state government quota seat, then the candidate must be a resident of Haryana. Students need to have cleared their 12th standard or an equivalent exam from a recognized educational institution with Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Biotechnology, and English as the main subjects.

General candidates should have at least 50% overall marks. If a candidate belongs to a reserved category, then he/she must obtain a total score of at least 40% in all the subjects and if they belong to the PWD category, they must secure at least 45%.

  • Is Haryana an Open State? - Yes. 85% of state quota seats will be granted to candidates belonging to Haryana and the rest 15% of seats will be offered to medical aspirants under all India Quota Seats.

However,  The official website dmer.haryana.gov.in will soon release the full schedule for admission to Haryana NEET. Individuals who meet the eligibility requirements will have the opportunity to register for Haryana NEET UG 2023.

Here are some Quick Details to get a clear idea about the status of MBBS in Haryana in 2023 :

  1. Admission will be based on the scores obtained by candidates in the NEET UG exam.
  2. The National Testing Agency (NTA) will be conducting the NEET UG exam.
  3. There are approximately 1835 MBBS and 950 BDS seats available through the tentative 85% state quota.

The admission process for MBBS in Haryana typically involves the following steps:

Registration

To initiate the counseling process for MBBS admission in Haryana, candidates should first visit the websites www.uhsr.ac.in and www.uhs ug admissions. in.

There are many governments, government-aided, private unaided medical dental colleges, and private MBBS colleges in Haryana.

Candidates should register themselves as per the dates mentioned in the admission schedule.

Eligible candidates must then register for Haryana NEET counseling through the official website dmer.haryana.gov.in.

Also, some details while registering -

  • For online MBBS admission in Haryana, candidates first need to complete the registration process by accurately filling out the online application form with their personal information and required details. 
  • Once the candidate submits the application form, a unique user ID and password will be generated, which they can use for subsequent logins in the future.
  • Candidates should carefully fill in the details in the online form, if any information is filled incorrectly then their admission will be canceled. 
  • After the registration candidate pays the fee within the stipulated time. 

Seat Allotment upon NEET score :

Seat Allotment: Based on the candidate's NEET score, preferences, and availability of seats, the counseling authority will allot seats to candidates.

  1. To be eligible for MBBS admission in Haryana, candidates must first qualify for the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET) with minimum qualifying marks.
  2. If two candidates have the same score in their NEET Exam, there are certain criteria that will be used to determine which candidate will be offered the seat.
  • Candidates obtaining higher marks in Biology (Botany and Zoology) in the test.
  • If still there is a tie, then a candidate has higher marks in Chemistry on the test
  • Still, if there is a tie, then a candidate older in age will be put higher in merit

Choice Filling

After registration, candidates must fill in their choices of colleges and courses in order of preference. 

Reporting at allotted MBBS college 

Candidates who are allotted seats must report to the respective MBBS college within the given timeframe and complete the admission formalities.

  1. Seat allotment results will be posted online and not sent individually.
  2. Candidates must bring original certificates, fees receipt, and application form to the allotted college for verification. 
  3. Failure to report within the given time will result in seat cancellation.

Document Verification

Candidates must then appear for document verification at the designated center with their original documents and a set of photocopies.

Conclusion
