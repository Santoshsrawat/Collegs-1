Birat Medical College & Teaching Hospital

Established in the recent years, Birat Medical College& Teaching Hospital is affiliated to the Kathmandu University. The college has one of the finest healthcare facilities and holds good reputation for Center of Excellence. The Birat Medical College& Teaching Hospital is spread in the area of 33 acres of land and has a comfortable environment for study. It is a preferred choice of college for medical aspirants who wish to pursue medicine from an outstanding institute. Annually, 70-75 national & international students approach to the college for seeking admission. The college offers graduate & post graduate courses in different field of medicine. The college has set performance benchmarks in the field of medical education and aims to provide high quality education with updated course curriculum under senior heath care faculty and expert advice.
