GET MY UNIVERSITY APPROACH

Medical Admission is a complex process and needs a great amount of pre-planning and a professional approach that can match the RIGHT FIT FOR the RIGHT STUDENTS.

Team Get My University assists medical aspirants in the following ways: 

  • Explaining the Quality of the College which includes analysis of Clinical Facts (Patient Inflow, Hospital Infrastructure, Departments, Location and connectivity Academics, Student Amenities like a hostel, food, extracurriculars, etc.)
  • Proper budget planning by explaining hidden elements like charges, annual increments in fees, Rural Service bonds, Bank guarantees, and Penalties. 
  • Give complete clarity about the Admission Process of multiple states thereby preventing wrong choices with multiple registrations, and security fees thereby making a huge waste of both money and precious time of Medical aspirants.
  • Systematic analysis of past year Cutoffs and scientific trend analysis about future cutoffs of respected colleges. 
  • Planning the admission process to prevent any confusion such as multiple forms filling, registration errors, domicile applicability, parallel counseling rounds, clash of dates, etc. 

It is not JUST about getting an institute it is MOST about getting the best institute and a poor candidate profile.
