Top Medical colleges in Uttarakhand that we have arranged on the basis of their rating, such as -

  1. HIMS Dehradun (Himalayan Institute of Medical Science
  2. Shri Guru Ram Rai Institute of Medical and Health Sciences

If we compare Uttarakhand to the rest of the country’s state then we will find that the Tuition fee for MBBS and the cost of living in Uttarakhand is a little expensive, but we can not overlook the state's commitment to providing a top-notch education to MBBS students, just because of its expenses.

  • Patient inflow: The patient inflow in Uttarakhand MBBS colleges is generally good, as the state has a significant population and is home to many hospitals and medical centers. Top medical colleges in Uttarakhand are affiliated with various hospitals and clinics, providing students with ample opportunities to interact with patients and gain practical experience.

Detailed Explanation of Top Mentioned Colleges In Uttarakhand

1. Himalayan Institute of Medical Science (HIMS)

Himalayan Institute of Medical Science was established in 1995, and this is a trust-run medical college this college has been affiliated with Swami Rama Himalayan University since its establishment and provides undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various medical fields, including MBBS, MD, MS, and MSc. 

HIMS has 150 seats for students that want to pursue MBBS, along with courses under specialization and super specialization such as obstetrics and gynecology, ENT, Neurosurgery, etc. 

The fee for the following MBBS courses in HIMS is around 95 lacs. Along with it, the college is recognized by the National Medical Council (NMC). HIMS has a 750-bedded hospital, which provides students with ample opportunities to gain practical experience and hands-on training in a clinical setting.

MBBS Course Details

The MBBS course duration is 5 and half years which includes 12 months of internship. Admission is granted through NEET UG Exam and according to score. Himalayan Institute of Medical Science accepted students through Uttarakhand State Counselling.

Internship Details of HIMS

The internship at HIMS is a one-year program that includes both theoretical and practical components. During the internship, students are required to rotate through various departments, including medicine, surgery, obstetrics etc. 

2. Shri Guru Ram Rai Institute of Medical and Health Sciences

Shri Guru Ram Rai Institute of Medical and Health Sciences is a medical college located in Dehradun, Uttarakhand, India. The college was established in 2006 and is affiliated with Hemwati Nandan Bahuguna Uttarakhand Medical Education University. 

The college offers undergraduate medical courses such as MBBS, B.Sc. Nursing, and postgraduate courses such as MD/MS, M.Sc. Nursing, and others. The college is also equipped with modern facilities and infrastructure to provide quality education to its students.

Shri Guru Ram Rai Institute has 150 MBBS seats along with specialized courses such as - ENT, Gynecology, Orthopedics, and many more. 

The Fee for pursuing MBBS will cost you around 75 lakhs, also the college is recognized by the National Medical Council (NMC). This college has one of the best infrastructure, a library, and outstanding faculty to make it a top medical college in Uttarakhand.
