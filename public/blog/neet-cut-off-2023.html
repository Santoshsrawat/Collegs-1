About NEET Cut off 2023

The National Testing Agency issued the NEET UG 2023 cutoff on June 13, 2023, for all categories of students, including UR/EWS & PH, OBC & PH/SC & PH, ST & PH, and SC/ST/OBC.

The minimal cut-off percentile for the NEET 2023 exam is 50th percentile for candidates in the General category, 45th percentile for candidates in the General PwD category, and 40th percentile for candidates in the SC/ST/OBC category.  
Visit the official website at  www.neet.nta.ac.in to find out the NEET Cut off for 2023.

Factors that determine NEET 2023 Cut off Marks

NEET Cut off 2023 score determination elements for MBBS/BDS admission include a number of variables, namely:-

•    Firstly, the number of candidates attempting the NEET exam
•    Second is the difficulty level of NEET exam
•    And lastly is the number of MBBS seat available 

NEET Cut off 2023 -Tie Breaking Process

There are numerous instances where the NEET rank of two or more candidates ends up being equal. The tie-breaking procedure has been introduced to facilitate an outcome. Therefore, the techniques listed below can be used to break ties in NEET results.

•    Higher marks in Biology

For the NEET merit list, candidates with higher marks or percentiles in biology (Botany and Zoology) would be given preference.

•    Higher marks in Chemistry

If a tie still stays the same, the candidate with better Chemistry scores/percentile will be taken into consideration.

•    Incorrect Answers

In the event that a tie still exists, students who answered less correct answers than incorrect ones will receive a higher ranking.

•    NEET Admission Cut off 2023

At last, students can check the round wise NEET last rank on the basis of which MBBS admission shall take place.
NEET Result 2023.

Students can access and download the NEET NTA result as it has been announced and is available on the official website at neet.nta.ac.in. The NEET roll number, date of birth, and security pin must be mentioned by students in order to download the NEET Result 2023.
