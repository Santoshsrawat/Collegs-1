Andhra Pradesh UG NEET Counselling Admission Process

STEP 1 : Register on the official website i.e. http://ntruhs.ap.nic.in/  and then login using ID and password.

STEP 2 : Fill the Andhra Pradesh NEET Counselling 2023 application form and don’t forget to take printout of online application by paying the required fees.

STEP 3 : Upload the required set of documents such as NEET admit card, rank card, 12th marksheet, signature etc.

STEP 4 : After uploading of all the documents, secret key will be given that a student will need to download the document verification slip.

STEP 5 : Begin the process of choice filling which is of great significance and must be done carefully as no additions can be made. However, only deletion and alteration is allowed.

STEP 6: The last step is Reporting. After the Andhra Pradesh NEET first round counselling results, student should report to the respective authority before going to the college.

NOTE :- Bank Guarantee of one year is applicable in all the medical colleges of Andhra Pradesh. However, some colleges are lenient and allow student to give post- dated cheque instead of Bank Guarantee.

Application and Processing fee including verification fee: Rs.10,620/- (Rs.9000/-+ Rs.1620/-(GST)) plus Bank charges extra can be paid through Debit card / Credit card / Net Banking / UPI.

Documents required for AP NEET Counselling 2023

Here are some of the essential documents that will be required at the time of registration process for Andhra Pradesh, namely:-

  1. NEET 2023 Admit Card
  2. NEET UG 2023 Rank Card
  3. SSC or equivalent examination marksheet & passing certificate with birth date shown
  4. Aadhaar Card
  5. Transfer Certificate
  6. Passing certificates from Class 6 to Class 12th
  7. Permanent caste certificate
  8. Minority certificate
  9. Residence certificate of the candidate
  10. Income certificate
  11. Local status certificate
  12. Latest passport size photograph
  13. Specimen signature of candidate

Documents required for NRI quota seats in Private Non-minority/ Minority Medical/ Dental Colleges

  1. Declaration (Annexure-II) on White Paper signed by both the candidate and NRI along
  2. with evidential documents such as Green Card/Citizenship Card/Copy of NRI’s Bank
  3. Statement for last 6 months /Pass-port issued by the respective Country/ Latest Electricity
  4. Bill/Gas Bill /Water Bill in the name of NRI (Visa / Driving license will not be accepted)

Eligibility Criteria for Andhra Pradesh UG NEET Counselling 2023

Below mentioned are the eligibility criteria which is essential to be known by every medical aspirant applying for Andhra Pradesh NEET UG Counselling.

  • Medical aspirants must have qualified NEET entrance exam from an institute recognized by NMC.
  • Student should have completed 10+2 with PCB and English with a minimum of 50% marks (40% marks for BC/SC/ST category candidates and 45% for OC PH i.e. Physically handicapped).
  • The age of medical aspirant must be above 17 years as of 31-12-2022.
  • Nationality of candidate must be Indian or PIO/OCI cardholders.
  • Every candidate has to fulfill the local and non-local status in AP state.
  • Candidate with valid AP state residence will be awarded the state quota seats.
  • Non-domicile candidates are eligible for only B1 seats under B category seats in Private Medical & Dental Colleges.
