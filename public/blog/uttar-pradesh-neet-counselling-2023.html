Uttar Pradesh NEET UG Counselling 2023 Admission Process

Step 1: Registration

Every medical aspirant is first required to register on the official website, https://upneet.gov.in and complete the necessary payments. After then, students can log in and review the seating chart to make their selections.

Points to remember while registering for UP NEET Counselling 2023

  1. The medical students must register only once on the platform.
  2. There should be no additional registration for participation in upcoming rounds.
  3. A registration fee of Rs. 2,000 must be paid, and it can be performed via the Internet using a credit card, debit card, or NET banking.

Step 2: Make a Security Deposit

After completing the registration process for Uttar Pradesh NEET counseling 2023 successfully, the next step is to pay the security deposit. It varies depending on the kind of college preferred and is refundable. The student may pay by Demand Draft produced in the name of the “Director General of Medical Education and Training”, Uttar Pradesh. 

The table below shows the level of security according to the type of college.

UG NEET Counselling for College Security Money Payable (INR)
Government Medical College 30,000
Government Dental College 30,000
Government and Private Dental Colleges 1,00,000
Government and Private Medical Colleges 2,00,000

 

Step 3 : Verification of Documents

The third step, document verification, requires the medical student to present themselves to the relevant official in order to start the document verification procedure. This stage is essential since failing to complete it within the allotted time may result in the loss of a seat. Therefore, the student must complete it promptly in order to move on to the next step of UP NEET Counselling 2023 and ensure the seat is secure.

Step 4: Choice filling and seat locking

Students can start filling out the choices once the results of the mock test are announced. Since there can be no additions once the selections have been made, it is a crucial step that calls for wise decision-making. Rearranging or deleting is permitted, though.

Step 5: Reporting

After the declaration of the final result, make the payment and then the student will be required to report to the Directorate of Medical Education and Training (DMET) and finally report to the college allotted.

Document Verification Check List for UP NEET Counselling 2023

The requirement set of documents that will be required at the time of the verification process of UP NEET Counselling are as follows:-

  • NEET admit card 2023
  • NEET rank card 2023

  • Printout of Online NEET Registration Slip

Important Points To Consider in UP NEET Counselling 2023

 

  • There is no free exit after allotment in any round of counseling of UP
  • In order to claim free exit, students will have to submit allotment letter of seat allotted in some other State
  • Security deposit is not refundable if not taken admission after allotment of seat
  • Document verification will be done physically before the allotment of seat
  • Security deposit will be forfeited if not reported to the college in Round 1, to reappear in Round 2 fresh security deposit will have to be submitted
  • Choice locking is mandatory in every round without which student will not be considered at the time of allotment
  • Security deposit will be refunded to eligible candidates (Not allotted any seat or allotted seat and taken admission) after the completion of all the rounds of counselling process

 

Eligibility Criteria for Uttar Pradesh NEET Counselling 2023


The following prerequisites must be met in order to register for UP NEET counselling process

  • A medical student must get a minimum of 50% overall (or 40% for students from the reserved categories) in physics, chemistry, and biology in 12th grade.
  • Students must be at least 17 years old as of December 31, 2023, however, applicants from the reserved category have a 5-year age relaxation.
  • Student must be an Indian citizen to claim seat under Government, Private, Management.
  • Foreign Nationals are also eligible to apply for seats under NRI/Other Quota
  • Every medical student must have qualified NEET entrance exam to participate in Uttar Pradesh NEET Counselling 2023

Type of Seats In Uttar Pradesh Medical Colleges

  1. Government – refers to seats offered in Uttar Pradesh’s government medical or dental college.
  2. Private – seats available in private medical colleges of Uttar Pradesh.
  3. Muslim Minority – these seats and reserved specifically for Muslim Minority students.
  4. Jain Minority – seats which are reserved in Jain Minority Private Medical/Dental Colleges for Jain Minority students

Our FAQ's

Can I skip Round 1 after form filling for UP NEET Counselling 2023?

No, it's not possible. Because without it, medical students would not be permitted to take part in next UP NEET Counselling rounds.

Are there any hidden charges in UP’s medical college?

Yes, there is a possibility that a medical student will be charged additional fees that they are unaware of. However, with the help and direction of the team Get My University, a student will not come across this problem.

How can I apply for UP NEET Counselling 2023?

Read the above blog and know all the documents that will be required in order to apply for UG NEET Counselling 2023.

How is UP NEET Counselling 2023 conducted?

Uttar Pradesh NEET Counselling 2023 process is conducted through online mode. However, mop-up round is conducted offline.

What is 85% state quota in NEET?

Students will be given access to 85% of the medical seats at the state's medical colleges under the State Quota. Students who reside in the State or a Union Territory may attempt to get admitted under the state quota

What is the UP NEET Registration last date for 2023?

For filling Uttar Pradesh NEET Counselling form, the last date is 6th April 2023.

From where to download UP NEET online form 2023?

To download the Uttar Pradesh NEET online form 2023, visit the official website https://upneet.gov.in and fill the form at the earliest.

Is it necessary to pay the security cost and the counselling fee?

Yes, in UP NEET Counselling (AIQ), both the security cost as well as counselling fees is required to pay in order to appear for Uttar Pradesh NEET Counselling 2023.

Who is eligible for NEET UP state quota?

A medical aspirant who has a minimum score of 50% marks in Physics, Chemistry, Biology (PCB) are eligible to apply for NEET UP State Quota Seat.
