Top Medical Colleges in Rajasthan 

Team getmyuniversity 06 Apr 2023 10704 views

Pursuing MBBS from Top Medical College in Rajasthan can be an exceptional choice for those who aspire to become skilled and knowledgeable doctors. The curriculum of MBBS in Rajasthan provides students with a comprehensive understanding of medical science, as well as practical training and experience in various clinical settings. 

When it comes to pursuing admission in Rajasthan for MBBS a thought must come to mind. Are Rajasthan MBBS colleges good enough for admission? Well, we will take a look, and evaluate the recent performance of Rajasthan MBBS Colleges in 2023. This simple guide by Get My University gives a perspective and answers to simple thoughts that come to the aspirant mind about Top Medical Colleges in Rajasthan such as-

  • Fee 
  • Living conditions
  • Curriculum
  • Clinical Conditions
  • Can an average student become a good doctor?

While Rajasthan can be an excellent state to pursue MBBS, it is important to note that the cost of pursuing medical education in the state may be slightly higher compared to other states. Many of the Top medical colleges in Rajasthan come with the price. 

For students who prefer the Northern region of India and are more comfortable with its environment and climate, pursuing a medical program in Rajasthan can be a choice. 

Rajasthan can offer excellent opportunities for students with a very low NEET Score in their entrance exams who are ready to pay a higher fee for a good medical college.

It is essential to acknowledge that students should not overlook the opportunity to pursue MBBS from Rajasthan solely due to the higher costs. Rajasthan ensures that students receive a comprehensive understanding of the medical field. There are numerous medical colleges in Rajasthan namely including-

1. NIMS 

2. MGM Jaipur

3. S S Tantia

The state offers a plethora of medical colleges that accept students with varying grades, ranging from below 150 to above 500. While some of the top medical colleges may have a higher cut-off, students who are willing to pay a higher amount more than 1 crore in some colleges, can still pursue their MBBS dream in Rajasthan.

Some Facts You Should Look At Before Opting For The Best Medical College in Rajasthan

  • Cost of the study- The cost of studying MBBS in Rajasthan can vary depending on the college or university chosen by the student. Typically, studying MBBS from a Top Medical College in Rajasthan can be slightly more expensive than in other states in India, primarily due to the state's high-quality infrastructure and modern facilities. 

On average, the tuition fees for MBBS in Rajasthan can range from INR 8-20 lakhs per year, depending on the institution. And, mostly 75% of the college fee is more than 1 crore.

Additionally, other expenses such as accommodation, food, books, and equipment may also add to the overall cost of admission for MBBS in Rajasthan. Students should also focus on other expenses such as transportation, personal expenses, and healthcare costs while estimating the overall cost of studying in Rajasthan.

  • Lack of Infrastructure in some colleges- Some institutions may lack modern equipment and facilities, which can limit a student's overall clinical experience. It is important to note that while some Top medical colleges in Rajasthan may lack modern infrastructure, not all colleges suffer from this issue. 

However, it is crucial for students to conduct thorough research and seek information about the colleges they are interested in to make an informed decision. That we are going to do it for you with the help of the research that we have put into our article.

  • Level of academics- The syllabus for admission for MBBS in Rajasthan is designed in accordance with the guidelines set by the Medical Council of India (MCI) to ensure that students receive a quality education.

Despite efforts made by colleges, there is still a significant gap in providing comprehensive medical exposure and curriculum to students in Rajasthan, which may hinder their full and optimal development as competent healthcare professionals.

  • Clinical exposure- The level of clinical exposure in Rajasthan falls below the desired standards, which may hinder the development of strong bonds with the local community and limit opportunities for growth as a compassionate and effective healthcare provider.
  • Hope For Low Scorer- A low academic score may not be a hindrance to pursuing study from Top MBBS colleges in Rajasthan, as there are prestigious colleges that offer admission to students who are willing to pay the required fees. 

However, it is important to note that admission to a top-tier college does not guarantee the best education, and students should carefully evaluate the institution's curriculum and resources before enrolling to ensure that they receive the best education possible.

List of Some of the Top Medical Colleges in Rajasthan

  • MGM Jaipur

Mahatma Gandhi Medical College and Hospital (MGM) Jaipur is a well-known medical institution located in the city of Jaipur in Rajasthan, India. 

MGM Jaipur comes second among the Top Medical Colleges in Rajasthan, this college has an incredible reputation in Rajasthan. 

The college is affiliated with the Rajasthan University of Health Sciences and offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in various medical disciplines such as Medicine, Surgery, Pediatrics, Obstetrics & Gynecology, Orthopedics, Anesthesia, and Radiology, among others. 

The college also has a hospital attached to it, which provides clinical exposure to students and serves the local community. MGM Jaipur is recognized by the Medical Council of India (MCI) and has a reputation for providing quality medical education and training to its students. 

Additionally, the faculty at MGM Jaipur is well-qualified and experienced in their respective fields, and the college has a track record of producing successful medical professionals. 

  • Geetanjali Udaipur

Geetanjali Medical College is a privately owned institution situated in Udaipur, Rajasthan. The college is affiliated with Geetanjali Hospital, which is a large tertiary care hospital with 1150 beds. 

The college's earlier batches until 2011 were affiliated with Rajasthan University of Health Sciences, which conducted the examinations. However, from 2012 onwards, the college is affiliated with Geetanjali University, which was established in 2011 and is spread across 25 acres of land.

Geetanjali is one of the prominent hospitals and universities in Rajasthan, it was established in 2008. 

Geetanjali University has the great feature of being one of the Top Medical Colleges in Rajasthan-

  • The campus is spread over 50 acres of lush green land.
  • Auditorium with a seating capacity of 750.
  • Also has several food courts, cafeterias, and canteens, that are ready to serve various regions' student food’s taste.
  • Mini supermarket inside the campus.
  • Smart and technical enabled lecture and excellent classroom. 

However, the total cost of completing an MBBS degree can be significant, estimated to be around 1 crore, which might be beyond the limit of certain students.

  • NIMS Jaipur

Rajasthan has several top medical colleges that offer quality education and training in the field of medicine. Among them, NIMS Jaipur is one of the top medical colleges in Rajasthan.

NIMS University Jaipur is a privately owned institution located in the city of Jaipur, Rajasthan, India. 

NIMS University Jaipur benefits from its location amidst the picturesque Aravalli Range, which provides a stunning backdrop and a serene environment for students to study and learn.

The university offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various fields such as medicine, engineering, and management. The university is equipped with modern infrastructure, including state-of-the-art labs, classrooms, and a well-equipped hospital for clinical training. 

NIMS was Established under the Nims University Act, 2008, enacted by the Government of Rajasthan.

It is the largest self-financed university in Rajasthan and is structured as a composite model university. 

Some Prominent Features of this institute: 

  • Quality Teaching and oriented research
  • Its state-of-the-art infrastructure is located along the Jaipur-Delhi Highway.
  • 450+ courses in the medical field, the opportunity to explore more
  • Nation orient academic excellence to have good doctors
  • High Moral Ethics that give students a boost to achieve their dreams and aspirations.

 But the Fee for this institution is higher and is more than 80 lakh for the MBBS course. If the Fee is not an issue for you then you can go to this institute. Along with it, fees really justify the service and level of education they provide.

  • JNU Jaipur 

JNU in Jaipur is one of the famous and well-acknowledged universities of Jaipur and it has more than 600 + faculty. 

The college offers an intensive program of both theoretical and practical coursework, providing hands-on training to produce 150 skilled clinical doctors annually. These graduates are equipped to offer effective and sustainable healthcare services to the community.

This college is host to excellence in academics and has proficiency in a good amount of curriculum. 

The fee for this institute is more than 80 lakh (Approx).

  • Pacific Medical College Udaipur

The university is situated in the beautiful surroundings of the Aravallis, with a beautiful and lush green environment, and boasts a modern and advanced infrastructure. 

Pacific Medical College's main focus is on providing education in various healthcare fields, including Nursing, Physiotherapy, Pharmaceuticals, and Healthcare. The university's flagship program, PMU, is dedicated to offering high-quality education and training in these areas.

Nestled in the serene and verdant hills of Bhilo Ka Bedla Pratappura in Udaipur (Raj), a 'B' class city located on National Highway 27, the university is spread over an extensive 32.14-acre campus that is exquisitely landscaped. 

Lush greenery is enough to provide a serene environment for students to learn and grow.

  • Pacific Institute of Medical Science Udaipur

The college has a modern campus with state-of-the-art infrastructure, including a well-stocked library, advanced laboratories, and a hospital that provides clinical training to the students. The faculty consists of experienced and qualified professors who are dedicated to providing quality education and training to the students.

Admission to the MBBS program is based on the NEET entrance exam conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA). The candidates who meet the eligibility criteria and score the minimum qualifying marks in the entrance exam can apply for admission to the college.

There are certain grounds this college needs to improve such as its infrastructure and academic excellence to give the best to its students.

  • Ananta Udaipur

Ananta Medical University is a highly respected medical institution located in the city of Udaipur, Rajasthan, India.

Ananta Medical University is widely regarded as one of the top medical universities, offering all the resources and facilities necessary to produce exceptional medical professionals of the future. If you're looking for a comprehensive and well-rounded medical education, this university provides everything you require to study from Best Rajasthan NEET MBBS college. 

This institute boasts a truly impressive state-of-the-art infrastructure and provides an exceptional standard of education that is complemented by unparalleled practical training. The quality of education and practical experience on offer here is unmatched, making it an ideal choice for anyone looking to receive the best possible medical training.

In the present scenario, Ananta Hospital has 730 Bedded tertiary care hospitals attached to its Medical College. 

Some important Points:

  • Walk in patient ratio is impressive
  • College has the dedicated infrastructure for various facilities
  • Good amount of students 
  • Very friendly and learning environment

  • SS Tantia Sriganganagar

SS Tantia College of Pharmacy is a college located in Sriganganagar, Rajasthan, India. The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in Pharmacy, including Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm), Diploma in Pharmacy (D.Pharm), and Master of Pharmacy (M.Pharm) in various specializations such as Pharmacology, Pharmaceutical Chemistry, Pharmaceutics, and Pharmaceutical Quality Assurance.

This esteemed institute offers good medical facilities to patients with exceptional clinical and diagnostic support. With state-of-the-art equipment and a team of skilled medical professionals, this institute is dedicated to delivering top-quality healthcare services to its patients. 

When you opt to study at one of the Top Medical Colleges in Rajasthan, that SS college will give you big clinical exposure. 

Whether it's advanced diagnostic tests or cutting-edge treatment options, patients can rest assured that he/she is in safe hands. 

And, for study, you can look for better opportunities with this institute. 

Conclusion

While Rajasthan boasts a plethora of top-notch educational institutions, many of them come with a steep price tag that may be a challenge for some students to afford. It is crucial to address the need for a revamped fee structure that can make these colleges accessible to deserving students regardless of their financial backgrounds.

If we prioritize allocating seats to students with exceptional academic grades, we can raise the standard of education across the board. By admitting high-performing students, educational institutions can ensure a more competitive and intellectually stimulating environment that benefits all students.
