Introduction

The NEET MBBS Counselling for Medical and Dental Programmes in Bihar is conducted by the Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board, also known as BCECE. Three rounds make up this counselling process: Rounds 1 and 2, as well as the Mopup or spot round, which is conducted online and offline, respectively. Here you can discover every single detail of Bihar NEET counselling 2023.

Students who pass the NEET exam must register on the official website www.bcece admission.nic.in and pay the registration cost of Rs. 1200 for General/BC/EBC and Rs. 600 for SC/ST/DQ, as set by the administration, in order to participate in the counselling procedure.

Bihar UG NEET Counselling Admission Process

Step 1 : Registration

The first step in receiving NEET counselling in Bihar is to register on the official website,

https://bceceboard.bihar.gov.in/. An active phone number and email address are required for Bihar NEET 2023 registration

Step 2 : Fill the Bihar UG NEET Counselling Application form

After creating id and password, student is required to fill the Bihar NEET Counselling 2023 form and take a print out of the same and pay the fees accordingly.

The authorities will publish the seat vacancy details for each round of counseling. A merit list will also be published based on the NEET scores and other eligibility criteria. 

Step 3 : Choice filling

Students can start filling the MBBS colleges of their choice once the results of the mock test are announced. Since there can be no additions once the selections have been made, it is a crucial step that calls for wise decision-making. Rearranging or deleting is permitted, though.

Step 4 : Reporting

After the declaration of final result, make the payment after which student shall be required to report to the respective authority and then at last to the college allotted.

Note:-

  • Security Deposit is applicable from R1 onwards.

  • Candidate has to pay Rs. 1200/-(One Thousand Two Hundred Only) for UNRESERVED/BC/EBC candidates and Rs. 600/-(Six Hundred) only for SC/ST/DQ candidates as non refundable registration / counselling fee through online mode only e.g. by Credit card/Debit Card/ Net Banking / UPI. But in case of Pvt. Colleges, all candidates have to pay Rs.1200/- only as non-refundable Registration & Counselling Fee.

Bihar MBBS Eligibility Criteria 2023

Applying for Bihar NEET counselling 2023 admission there are some essential conditions that must be fulfilled. The conditions are as follows:-

  • Candidate must be a citizen of India                         
  • Medical aspirant must be above 17 years of age     

For Domicile Students :-

  • Parent of Domicile student must be a permanent resident of India
  • Candidate whose parents are refugees in Bihar
  • Candidates whose parents do not live in the state permanently but who are employed by the Bihar government.
  • The parents of students who are in a transferable job in Bihar and Jharkhand state.
  • The candidate's parents should be working in the Public Sector Undertaking (PSU) of the Government of India.
  • Candidates whose parent/s are working with the United Nations and are recruited in Bihar.
  • Candidates for the general category must have passed the qualifying test for class 12 with a combined score of 50% in Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and English. The requirement for SC/ST/OBC candidates, on the other hand, is a minimum qualifying exam score of 40%.
  • Domicile candidate have access to all the available seats

*Non-domicile students can apply for B Category seats in Private Medical Colleges

Salient Features of Bihar NEET Counseling 2023

  1. In Round 1, students can exit the counseling process without any charges.
  2. The application form can be filled in Round 1 and Mop-up rounds.
  3. In Round 2, there is no fresh registration.
  4. Physical document verification is conducted after the seat allotment.
  5. It is mandatory for students to lock their choices in every round to be considered for the seat allotment process.
  6. Students who wish to participate in the Mop-up round must carry a demand draft of Rs 12 lakhs for MBBS and Rs 50,000 for BDS.
  7. 33% of the seats are reserved for female candidates.
Note :- If NRI seats remain vacant during the Mop-up round, they will be converted to Private seats.

Type Of MBBS Seats in Bihar

Government seat: This refers to a seat that is offered in a government medical/dental college in Bihar. However, only domicile candidates are eligible to apply for these seats.

  1. Private seat: This refers to a seat that is offered in a private medical/dental college in Bihar. Both domicile and non-domicile candidates can apply for these seats.
  2. Muslim Minority seat: These seats are reserved in Muslim Minority Private Medical/Dental Colleges in Bihar for Muslim Minority students. Only Muslim Minority students are eligible to apply for these seats.
  3. Sikh Minority seat: These seats are reserved in Sikh Minority Private Medical Colleges in Bihar for Sikh Minority students. Only Sikh Minority students are eligible to apply for these seats.

Our FAQ's

Who is responsible for conducting Bihar NEET Counselling?

Bihar MBBS Counselling 2023 is conducted by Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board, BCECEB.

Who are eligible to participate in Bihar NEET 2023 admission?

Every medical aspirant who has cleared NEET entrance exam is eligible to participate in Bihar NEET 2023 counselling. To know the complete process in detail, you can consult Team Get My University.

Can I fill out the Bihar NEET 2023 counselling application form offline?

No, Bihar NEET 2023 Counselling application form cannot be filled offline and so every medical aspirant shall fill the application from in online mode.

What is the official website for Bihar UG NEET Counselling 2023?

The counselling will be conducted online, and candidates who wish to participate must register on the official website of BCECEB ( https://bceceboard.bihar.gov.in/ ).

What is the safe score for Bihar MBBS NEET 2023?

The safe score for Bihar MBBS NEET Counselling 2023 ranges between 180-200.

In which mode Bihar NEET Counselling 2023 will be held?

Online registration is required for the Bihar NEET Counselling 2023. Basically, the first two rounds take place online, while the third round i.e. the mop up round takes place offline.

Is Bihar NEET 2023 Counselling started?

The NEET Counselling 2023 will begin after the declaration of NEET 2023 result in online mode.
