Direct Admission in Symbiosis Institute of Technology (SIT) Pune for B.Tech: Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for Direct Admission in Symbiosis Institute of Technology (SIT) Pune for B.Tech, candidates are required to fulfill the below mentioned conditions, namely:-

1.    Educational Qualification

A minimum of 45% marks in Physics, Mathematics, and one of the following subjects—Chemistry, Biotechnology, Biology, or Technical Vocational—was required for completion of the 10+2 or equivalent examination for applicants (45% for SC/ST candidates).

*Applicable on all Indian students and International students (NRI/PIO/Foreign Nationals

2.    SITEEE Exam

All those students who wish to pursue B.Tech offered by Symbiosis Institute of Technology (SIBM) are required to appear for mandatory Computer Based Entrance Test (CBT) named as SITE Engineering Entrance Exam (SITEEE). 

Direct Admission in Symbiosis Institute of Technology (SIT) Pune for B.Tech: Admission Process

The steps to be followed for Direct Admission in Symbiosis Institute of Technology (SIT) Pune for B.Tech are as follows-

1.    Registration

Students are required to appear for SITEEE/JEE (Main)/ Any State Govt. Engg. Entrance Exam candidates and select the branch. 

2.    Uploading of Documents

After successfully clearing the SITEEE Exam, entrance exam scorecard and category certificate is required to be submitted. Following the upload of their documents to the registration page, candidates must press the submit button. Candidates who don't comply will not be placed on the merit list (branch allocation).

3.    Payment of Fees

Check the B.Tech. tuition costs at the Symbiosis Institute of Technology (SIT) in Pune and make the necessary payment.

4.    Verification of Documents

Entrance scorecards will be verified only for JEE (Main) / Any State Govt. Engg. Entrance Exam fees paid candidates, to check the scores mentioned during registration. Category certificates will be verified for applicable candidates.

5.    Hostel Allotment

Last step after complete direct admission process of Symbiosis Institute of Technology (SIT) Pune for B.Tech  is to get registered for hostel allotment.
