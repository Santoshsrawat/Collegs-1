Introduction

Medical students are often puzzled about which career will be secure and offer a competitive package after 12th. Students can choose from a variety of medical programme, with Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS) being one of the most well-liked options. There are, however, some other programs also that offer good career opportunities. For more medical updates, stay connected and keep following Get My University.

In order to learn which medical programs would guarantee you a living a good life with good salary package, read the blog based on best medical courses after 12th in india carefully.

Medical Courses List After 12th

The following mentioned are the list of top best medical courses after 12th in India that a medical aspirant can opt:-

1.    Bachelor of Medicine & Bachelor of Surgery
2.    Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT)
3.    Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS)
4.    Bachelor of Homoeopathic Medicine & Surgery (BHMS)
5.    Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine & Surgery (BAMS)
6.    Bachelor of Pharmacy (BPharm)
7.    Bachelor of Speech and Language Pathology
8.    Bachelor of Veterinary Science
9.    Bachelor of Unani Medicine & Surgery (BUMS)
10.    Bachelor of Veterinary Sciences & Animal Husbandry (BVSc & AH)


What are the best courses after 12th in medical field?

1.    Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS)

MBBS is the best course of study for those aspiring to a career in medicine and who want to one day serve as doctors in society. A doctor with an internationally recognised degree like an MBBS can work abroad and explore various cultures.

Eligibility  12th Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology as main subjects )
Entrance Exam NEET Exam
Duration  5.5 Years Including 1 year of Internship
Course Fee 4-15 Lakhs Per Month

   
  2.    Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT)

Over the years, the demand and need of physiotherapists has increased significantly. Thus, students with the main subjects Physics, Chemistry and Biology can go for Bachelor of Physiotherapy and complete within 4 years itself.

Eligibility 12th Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology Main Subjects)
Entrance Exam No Entrance Exam
Duration  4 Years (6 Months Internship)
Course Fee 40k – 80k Per Year


3.    Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS)

The next desirable career after MBBS is BDS, which can be pursued at a reasonable cost and has a similar demand as MBBS. Therefore, BDS requires less time than MBBS if we compare the two, it can be one of the best medical courses after 12th in India.

Eligibility 12th Science
Entrance Exam NEET Exam
Duration 5 Years
Course Fee 1-6    Lakhs Per Year

 

4.    Bachelor of Homoeopathic Medicine & Surgery (BHMS)

Choose Bachelor of Homoeopathic Medicine & Surgery if you want a well-paying career and have at least a 40% average in your 12th grade. This education degree is appropriate if you want to pursue a profession in homeopathy.

Eligibility 12th Science
Entrance Exam NEET Exam
Duration 5.5 Years
Course Fee 80k – 1.5 Lakhs Per Annum

 

5.    Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine & Surgery (BAMS)

The Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine & Surgery programme gives students a thorough understanding of Ayurveda and gives them the knowledge and abilities to promote and prevent illness. It is a well-known medical programme and the only Ayurvedic medical degree that is acknowledged by the medical community.

Eligibility 12th Science
Entrance Exam NEET Exam
Duration 5.5 Years
Course Fee 25k – 3.2 Lakh Per Month

  

6.    Bachelor of Pharmacy (BPharm)    

Bachelor of Pharmacy includes study of pharmaceutical science subjects such as drug safety, discovery, medical chemistry, industrial pharmacy. Medical aspirants who have the desire to know how medical drug affect the human body then is the suitable course. 

Eligibility 12th Science with minimum 50% aggregate
Entrance Exam State or University Level Entrance Exam
Duration 4 Years
Course Fee  20k – 80k Per Month

 

7.    Bachelor of Medical Laboratory & Technology (BMLT)

Bachelor of Medical Laboratory & Technology is one of the best medical courses after 12th in India, it is a medical programme that requires a medical aspirant to focus on practical and laboratories related knowledge. Pursuing BMLT programme shall allow a medical student to get expertise in using laboratory equipment and laboratory diagnostic.

Eligibility  12th Science with minimum 50% aggregate 
Entrance Exam State or University Level Entrance Exam
Duration 3 Years 
Course Fee INR 20,000 – INR 2 Lakhs

 

8.    Bachelor of Naturopathy & Yogic Sciences (BNYS)

For medical aspirants who have to build their career in Ayurvedic or homoeopathy, this is the right medical programme to pursue which has a duration of 3 years.

Eligibility 12th Science with minimum 50% aggregate 
Entrance Exam State or University Level Entrance Exam
Duration 3 Years 
Course Fee INR 20,000 – INR 2 Lakhs

 

9.    Bachelor of Unani Medicine & Surgery (BUMS)

Bachelor of Unani Medicine & Surgery (BUMS) is a medical course that offers a legal degree after which a medical aspirant can become a medical advisor. 

Eligibility 12th Science with minimum 50% aggregate marks
Entrance Exam State or University Level Entrance Exam
Duration  5. 5 Years
Course Fee 6.5 Lakhs

 

10.    Bachelor of Veterinary Sciences & Animal Husbandry (BVSc & AH)

Medical aspirants who are looking for the best medical courses after 12th in India, then pursuing Bachelor of Veterinary Sciences & Animal Husbandry would be a suitable choice. The duration of BVSc & AH is same as that of MBBS.

Eligibility 12th Science with minimum 50% aggregate marks
Entrance Exam State or University Level Entrance Exam
Duration 5.5 Years
Course Fee INR 15,000 – INR 1 Lakhs

Our FAQ's

4. Why MBBS in Indian is tough?

Due to its extensive coursework and practical training, MBBS in India is a challenging medical program. Additionally, passing the entrance exam known as the NEET exam is also quite difficult.

2. Which medical course has highest scope?

MBBS has the greatest potential and is in high demand among Indian students, in our opinion. A medical aspirant who pursues MBBS might expect to receive a good salary package and have access to a range of potential options.

1. Which medical course is best?

MBBS i.e. Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery is known to be one of the most popular medical courses in India. It has a duration of approx. 5 to 6 years.

3. Which doctor course is best and easy?

All medical programs are excellent and well-known, but they are not easy to crack. Since every course has its own challenges, it takes a lot of work and effort to succeed. It is a profession that saves lives and needs a lot of expertise, thus over the long term, it pays well.

5. Medical Courses after 12th without NEET?

NEET entrance exam is the most important exam which is required to clear for pursuing almost every medical programme. But an exception always exists and so some medical courses like phlebotomist, medical lab technologists, nutritionists, and physician assistants can be pursued.
