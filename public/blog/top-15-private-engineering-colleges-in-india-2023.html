Top 15 Private Engineering Colleges in India 2023

Team getmyuniversity 07 Sep 2023 1150 views

Do you know that with over 2 million engineers graduating from various engineering institutes each year, India has the third-largest pool of engineers in the entire world?
It is because of Top 15 Private Engineering Colleges in India that provides fantastic prospects for placement through on-campus hiring. 

Additionally, the demand for competent and talented engineers has increased due to India's rapid expansion in technological advancement, opening up excellent employment chances for individuals with an engineering degree.

Therefore, this article on the "Top 15 Private Engineering Colleges in India" is essential reading for you if you want to be a successful engineer in the future. In our constant endeavor to help students become successful engineers, Team Get My university has taken into account every significant criteria and have identified the best Private Engineering Colleges in India that are the greatest and most sophisticated in every way.

College 1: BITS Pilani (Birla Institute of Technology and Science)

Highly Flexible Culture - Due to the special features it provides to its students, BITS Pilani is recognized as one of the Best Private Engineering Colleges in India. As, BITS students benefit from flexibility and can choose between dual degrees, transfers, and programs of courses.  Additionally, it allows students to select their preferred lab, class, and lecture times.

BITS Pilani was founded in 1964 as the first Indian university to offer a degree in engineering entirely through the medium of distance education.

Accreditation - BITS, Pilani has been accredited by the National Assessment & Accreditation Council (NAAC) with 'A' grade with a CGPA of 3.45 on a four point scale after visiting its Pilani, K.K Birla Goa & Hyderabad campuses in 2016.

College 2: VIT Vellore (Vellore Institute of Technology)

International Relations - VIT has collaborations with more than 300 foreign universities and a significant global environmental impact. The opportunity to study for two years at VIT and two years at a partner overseas university is offered by VIT. Additionally, one may participate in international research collaborations or study abroad for a semester.

VIT University has been ranked among the top 100 universities in India by the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) for several consecutive years.

Accreditation  - VIT Vellore has been ranked 9 and 12 under the 'University' and 'Engineering' categories, respectively, by the NIRF 2022 Rankings. Also, during VIT Vellore placements in 2022,  it is one of the top 15 private engeneering colleges in India the highest package offered was INR 1.2 crore per annum. VIT Vellore is famous for its infrastructure as well.

 College 4: M.S Ramaiah Institute of Technology

Remarkable Placement – M.S Ramaiah Institute of Technology is one of the top 15 private engineering colleges in India and is known to the No. 1 engineering college in Bangalore and thus offers exceptional placement offers to engineers. As per the record, the highest package offered has been 50 LPA and average package goes around 6-7 LPA. Many reputed companies visit the campus for recruitment, offering job opportunities to eligible students.

An independent private engineering school named M. S. Ramaiah Institute of Technology (MSRIT) is situated in Bangalore, Karnataka, India. It is associated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), which was founded in 1962. The institute bears Sri M. S. Ramaiah's name, a well-known industrialist and philanthropist who founded it.

Accreditation - As of the 2023 session, Ramaiah Institute of Technology is ranked 67 among 1249 engineering institutions and 17 among 91 institutions of architecture by the NIRF. RIT received an A+ rating from NAAC with a grade of 3.28.

 College 5: RV College of Engineering

Best Academics - RV College of Engineering is popular for its strong academic programs and excellent faculty. The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in a variety of engineering disciplines, including computer science, mechanical engineering, and electrical and electronics engineering.

One of the first self-financing engineering colleges in the nation was R.V. College of Engineering (RVCE), founded in 1963. Rashtreeya Sikshana Samithi Trust (RSST), a nonprofit trust, manages the institution. The trust oversees approximately 25 institutions, with RVCE serving as its flagship institution. Today, RVCE is acknowledged as one of India's top technical institutions.

Accreditation - In the 2023 NIRF Ranking released by the Ministry of Education, Government of India, VIT is ranked as the 8th best university category, the 11th best research institution, and the 11th best engineering institution in India. The National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) awarded VIT the highest rating of A++ for accreditation in the fourth cycle of 2021.
