Understanding the Admission Process

Admission in SRM University is possible either through clearing entrance exam i.e. SRMJEEE or direct admission through management quota. 

1.    SRMJEEE Exam

SRM University administers a national entrance exam for admission to B.Tech courses offered at SRMIST campuses. Applicants seeking admission to SRM University should apply for and pass the SRMJEEE entrance exam with a respectable score in order to secure a seat in that programme.

SRMJEEE  is held based on the 10+2 syllabus and has MCQ-type questions asked from Physics, Chemistry, Maths/Biology, Aptitude and English. It is conducted thrice in a year and has duration of 2.30 hours which is held in English medium. SRMJEEE exam fee is Rs.1200 and is non-refundable. The application form for each phase can be filled out if a candidate wants to take the SRMJEEE exam more than once.

2.    Direct Admission through Management Quota

Obtaining admission in SRM University through management quota is challenging since it takes specialised knowledge. Thus, for expert assistance, contact Team Get My University and you won't have any trouble getting admission with us. For both the process, fulfilling the eligibility criteria is essential. When a student clears the SRMJEEE and earns a high ranking, they are likely to get admitted without much difficulty to SRM University. But even if they don't, students can still be admitted directly to SRM University through the management quota.

Eligibility Criteria for Direct Admission in SRM University

a)    Nationality and Age

Resident Indian or Non-Resident Indian (NRI), holder of PIO or OCI card issued by Government of India are eligible to apply for SRMJEEE (UG).
Note: NRIs, holders of PIO or OCI card issued by Government of India who has not taken the SRMJEEE (UG) must apply under International student category only.
Should have attained the age of 16 years and 6 months on the 31st July of the calendar year in which the 12th Board examination is to be held.

b)    Qualifying Examination

All B.Tech programmes :Minimum 60% aggregate in Physics, Mathematics, and Chemistry /Biotechnology/ Biology/ Computer Science/ Information Technology/ Informatics Practices/ Engineering Graphics.

Passed in Higher secondary examination (10+2 pattern) or appearing in Higher Secondary examination in the current academic year with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects along with any one of the subjects, Chemistry/ Biotechnology/ Biology/ Computer Science/ Information Technology/ Informatics Practices/ Engineering Graphics as major subjects in regular stream from any state board within India, CBSE, ISCE, Matriculation, or NIOS

c)    SRMJEEE (UG)

Candidates who have attempted Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, English & Aptitude in SRMJEEE(UG) are eligible for all the B.Tech Degree Programs

d)    Direct Admission 

To encourage and support students of exemplary talent, SRM Institute of Science and Technology (formerly known as SRM University) offers direct admission and scholarships to first rank students of all the central and state boards in India, top 10,000 rankers in IIT JEE, top rankers in each district of Tamil Nadu and exemplary sports persons at National and International level.

SRM University Admission Process

Step 1 – Apply online at www.srmuniv.ac.in
Step 2 – Appear for SRMJEEE entrance examination.

Note – No entrance examination is required for international and NRI students to get direct admission in SRM University through management quota.

 

Step 3 – Admission is on the basis of merit through transparent counselling process based on entrance exam ranking and marks secured in qualifying exam.

SRM University Scholarships

Availability of Scholarships and Banks Loans

There are financial aid options including scholarships, grants, and assistantships that do not need repayment to the university. Grants are typically given based on financial need, whereas scholarships and assistantships are given based on academic merit. For all undergraduate programmes in engineering, scholarships and grants are available. Engineering postgraduate and doctorate students may apply for assistantships in research and teaching.

Availability of B.Tech Tuition Fee Waivers

A tuition fee waiver between 25% to 100% is given to students on academic merit, achievement in sports and economic background.

Availability of Educational Bank Loans

Several nationalised and private banks, recognizing the reputation of SRM, visit the campus during Admission Counselling to sanction on-the-spot student educational bank loan.

Availability of Founder’s Scholarship

This esteemed award includes a monthly stipend of INR 1000 along with transportation, board and accommodation in the university residence hall and full tuition waiver. This honour is granted to students who have excelled in athletics, SRMEE, JEE (Mains), JEE (Advanced), school academics, candidates from underrepresented groups, and candidates with disabilities.

Availability of Semester Abroad Scholarships

These are awarded to deserving students taking a semester abroad at prestigious SRM partner universities. Over 2 crore is given each year in fee waivers at SRM for students studying abroad as well as matching funds at a few select universities like MIT, Carnegie Mellon, and Cornell.

Our FAQ's

Which SRM branch is good?

All branches of SRM are brilliant. However, Chennai Campus of SRM is the main campus where a student must have a rank within 10000 for CSE branch.

Is SRM KTR better than Manipal?

Every university has advantages of its own. SRM KTR, however, has a higher placement rate than Manipal. As a result, you will receive a fantastic wage package and the ideal placement.

Can I apply for direct admission in multiple programs at SRM University?

Yes, candidates can apply for direct admission at SRM University in multiple programs. However, it is important to carefully select the programs based on your interests and eligibility. Each program may have different eligibility criteria and document requirements, so be sure to thoroughly read the instructions before submitting your applications.

Is direct admission in SRM University possible?

Direct admission at SRM University is available for a wide range of programs. However, the availability of seats through direct admission may vary depending on the program and the category of admission. It is advisable to check the official website of SRM University or contact Team Get My University.

Are there any scholarships available for students seeking direct admission in SRM University?

Yes, SRM University offers scholarships to deserving students. The scholarships are awarded based on academic performance and other eligibility criteria set by the university. To avail of these scholarships, candidates must meet the specified requirements and submit the necessary documents as per the university's guidelines.

What is the selection process for direct admission in SRM University?

The selection process for direct admission in SRM University through management quota is primarily based on the academic performance of the candidates. The university evaluates the marks obtained in the qualifying examinations, as well as other criteria such as age restrictions and specific eligibility requirements. Shortlisted candidates may be called for counseling sessions or interviews, depending on the program and the category of admission.

Is SRM University government or private?

SRM Institute of Science and Technology (SRMIST) formerly SRM University is a private deemed university, located in Kattankulathur, Chengalpattu (near Chennai), Tamil Nadu, India.
