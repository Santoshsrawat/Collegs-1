Karnataka NEET UG 2023 Counselling Registration begins at kea.kar.nic.in

On July 14, 2023, the Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA) opened registration for the Karnataka NEET UG 2023 Counselling. Candidates can submit their applications for UG counselling through the Karnataka neet counselling website i.e. kea.kar.nic.in

Karnataka NEET UG 2023 Registration Process

Step 1 : Visit the link given on KEA Website for UGNEET 2023 registration

Step 2 : Select the option given as “ UGCET – 2023 registered candidates”

Step 3 : Enter the UG NEET 2023 Roll Number

Step 4 : Complete the registration details

Step 5 : UGCET 2023 online application details will be displayed

Step 6 : Do not forget to verify the details in each Tab.

Step 7 : Wherever necessary makes the required changes and make sure all the information filled is correct.

Step 8 : Before selecting the declaration take the draft, take the print and verify the entries as once made will not be changed further.

Step 9 : Select the declaration and complete the proces

Step 10 : Pay the fees and take the printout for future use.

Karnataka UG NEET 2023 Document Verification

The Karnataka Examinations Authority, 18th Cross, Malleshwaram, Bangalore, will be the location for the verification of documents and admission to medical, dental, ayurvedic, unani, and homeopathic programmes for the year 2023.

Documents to carry for Karnataka UG NEET Document Verification Process

•    KEA UG NEET 2023 application printout
•    Verification Slip
•    UG NEET 2023 Score Card
•    Valid Identity Card (Driving License/ Voter ID/ Passport/ PAN/ Aadhaar Card)

Candidates must appear at the document verification based only on their All India Rank and present all original documents as specified in the UGNEET-2023 Information Bulletin. No one else may represent the candidates; they must personally appear as scheduled for document verification.

Important Information About Karnataka NEET UG 2023

1.    Application forms for the UGNEET 2023 can only be registered online at the website http://kea.kar.nic.in Applications CANNOT be sent by mail in offline or printed form.

2.    Karnataka UG NEET 2023 Registration fee of Rs. 500 (for Karnataka SC/ST/Cat-1/PWD/GM/2A/2B/3A/3B candidates), Rs. 2500 (for non-Karnataka candidates), and Rs. 5,500 (for NRI Ward/NRI/OCI/PIO/Foreign Nationals candidates) should be paid through the provided payment gateway using a Credit Card or a Debit Card issued by banks in India, through Internet Banking, or by downloading bank challan.

3.    No request for change of details in online application will be entertained

4.    A candidate may choose to sit for other exams including JEE, MCC, KPCF, and KEA, as well as seats in the medical or engineering fields, among others. If the candidate who was also allotted a seat through KEA wishes to confirm the seat chosen from other boards, the candidate who was also allotted a seat must give up the seat before the deadline set for changing options for the second round of seat allocation; otherwise, it may amount to a seat being blocked, and KEA may take action or initiate proceedings against such candidates.

5.    If a candidate chooses to give up their seat, they must do so solely in writing to KEA. The candidate should not assume that by simply failing to report at the designated or chosen college, he or she has turned over the seat to KEA.
