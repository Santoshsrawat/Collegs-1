Top 10 Deemed Medical Colleges in India

Team getmyuniversity 17 May 2023 16875 views

Before delving into the discussion about the Top 10 Deemed Medical Colleges in India. You should know What deemed colleges are and What Deemed Medical Colleges mean? The Ministry of Education grants "Deemed" to universities in India to give them complete autonomy, and certified that they have earned a distinguished status because they excel in every aspect of providing quality education.

All decisions within these colleges are made internally, without any involvement of third parties. The colleges have full autonomy and authority to make their own decisions regarding various aspects, such as curriculum, policies, and exams.

The NMC guidelines apply to every Best Deemed Medical College in India, but they mainly make their own decisions by themselves. You might find, fee structures in Deemed Medical Colleges are generally on the higher side because these institutions are self-financed.

As students seeking admission to one of the Top 10 Deemed Medical Colleges in India, you will have the benefits of the -

  • Exceptional Infrastructure
  • Excellent and knowledgeable Teaching faculty
  • Outstanding exam results
  • Self-growth environment

There are 51 Deemed universities in India in 2023, and the total number of seats in these colleges is 8550. But right now, we are telling you the Top 10 Deemed Medical Colleges in India. We have arranged these colleges based on the parameters such as -

  • How good are academics?
  • Is infrastructure meeting the medical requirement?
  • Do students have access to a diverse range of clinical experience?
  • How is the Patient in-Flow
  • What is the student's feedback?
  • College Recognition and Overall Reputation.
  • Passing Percentage in these colleges.

The Get My University Team has prepared the list of deemed medical colleges to provide students with comprehensive insights into the very details any student require to make the best decision about their medical career and future.

Top Deemed Medical Colleges in India Cut-off

Most Deemed medical colleges offer quality education under great infrastructure, so students and parents prefer to be admitted there. Therefore, it is natural to have high cut-off marks in Deemed Medical Colleges compare to the other state medical colleges.

1. KMC, Manipal, Karnataka 

First on our, list of Deemed Medical Colleges in India is KMC Manipal it is renowned as a prestigious deemed medical university in India. It holds a prominent position not only among deemed universities but also among the Top 10 Deemed Medical Colleges in India.

Overview

Established in 1953, Kasturba Medical College has grown to become a highly attractive and prestigious destination for aspiring medical students. Over the years, it has gained a strong reputation as a renowned medical institution, and students from more than 30 countries graduate from this medical college every year. It is MCI recognized Deemed University.

Hospital

After the establishment of this medical college, it was initially affiliated with the Medical Relief Society for the clinical facilities for students. Since 1961, Kasturba Hospital has grown from 150 beds to 2032 beds with excellent medical infrastructure. 

With a daily outpatient department that receives 3,702 patients and a monthly record of 4,073 major surgeries and 6,616 minor surgeries, KMC demonstrates a significant patient flow. This offers students an exceptional opportunity to gain practical experience and exposure to a wide range of medical cases. Being enrolled in one of the Top 10 Deemed Medical Colleges in India provides students with the opportunity to explore the vast world of medicine with access to a wide range of medical equipment and diagnostic facilities.

Courses

There are various medical courses available in KMC, Manipal such as -

  • UG
  • PG
  • Super-specialty

When we talk about the UG course then KMC, Manipal is one of the best MBBS colleges in India, according to the NIRF ranking. There are a total of 250 seats are available in KMC, Manipal for the MBBS. 

Whereas, for P.G. the numbers of seats are 253 including super specialty courses for aspiring medical students that are looking to study in one of the Top 10 Deemed Medical Colleges in India. 

Infrastructure

KMC, Manipal boasts excellent infrastructure with a comprehensive range of facilities within its expansive 600+ acre campus. Students have access to a wide array of resources and amenities that cater to their academic, research, and extracurricular needs. 

  • Labs are well equipped 
  • Hostel facilities are available for both boys and girls on campus.
  • Classrooms are modern and smart
  • Well-stocked and quiet library

Moreover, KMC, Manipal is an eminent institution that attains an impressive overall passing percentage of 94%. This signifies the commitment to academic excellence and the high standards maintained by the college.

Fees - Rs. 17,70,000

 

2. KMC, Mangalore

KMC, Mangalore is another Best Deemed university in India that has a global reputation and recognition worldwide. It is recognized by the Medical Council of India.

Overview

KMC, Mangalore was established in 1955 with the objective of meeting the healthcare demands of the community and providing exceptional medical education. Over the years, the college has successfully fulfilled these objectives, earning itself a position among the top 10 deemed medical colleges in India. 

Infrastructure 

The infrastructure at KMC, Mangalore is designed to provide students with all the essential facilities necessary for focused and effective study. Also, the college campus is equipped with well-designed classrooms, modern laboratories, and a well-stocked library.

It is 31 acres campus that has individual departments as well as 2 hospitals under KMC that are supported by excellent infrastructure. Moreover, The Department of Neurology at KMC, Mangalore is equipped with EEG (Electroencephalography) and ENMG (Electroneuromyography) labs.

Hospital

KMC, Mangalore has 2 hospitals attached to itself, known as Government Wenlock Hospital and Lady Goschen Hospital. Both, these hospitals were built to provide extensive clinical exposure to UG students and to give them the medical training, they require to gain practical experience.

  • A 600-bed multidisciplinary specialty center renowned for its medical expertise, nursing care, and diagnostic quality. KMC, Mangalore is indeed one of the distinguished Top 10 deemed medical colleges in India that provide students with significant medical exposure. The college offers ample opportunities for practical learning and hands-on experience.

Moreover,  KMC Hospital, located at Ambedkar Circle, is a super specialty Corporate Hospital set with multi-specialty services available under one roof. As, a medical student you will have the chance to work among a team of some of the best doctors in India, and qualified paramedical staff. This medical college is a 250-bed inpatient facility.

Courses

There are a number of courses that are available in this medical college that are -

  • In UG there is MBBS for 4 years and 6 months
  • In PG - Master of Medicine, Doctor of Medicine, and Msc.

Passing Percentage 

The Passing percentage in Mangalore, Karnataka is 90%.

Fees - Rs. 17,70,000.

 

3. Sri Ramachandra Medical College

This medical college establishment was led by the Sri Ramachandra Educational and Health Trust in the year 1985 and is known as the best among the Top 10 Deemed medical colleges in India. 

SRMC is dedicated to serving society as a center of excellence with an emphasis on medical education, research, and healthcare.

SRMC, also known as Sri Ramachandra Medical College and Research Institute, is located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. It is a renowned Deemed institution offering the best medical education and affordable healthcare services. This medical college has been assigned the top position in the NIRF ranking of the best medical college many times.

The rankings of SRMC (Sri Ramachandra Medical College) reflect its strong reputation in the medical world and its unwavering commitment to being recognized as one of the best-deemed medical colleges in India. SRMC benefits from this advantageous location, making it easier for students, faculty, and patients to reach the institute. The city has a well-developed transportation system. 

Infrastructure

The hostel facilities offer a total of 3608 rooms for both men and women combined. There are many single rooms inside the campus to accommodate undergraduate and postgraduate students in medicine and another discipline. 

The University, covering an extensive 150-acre area, is nestled within a lush green campus. The campus functions as a  self-contained township, accommodating colleges and facilities such as -

  • A medical center
  • Housing for staff and students, 

Moreover, conference facilities, playgrounds, multi-cuisine restaurants, and even a post office.

Courses

Sri Ramachandra Medical College is renowned for offering a wide range of courses in the medical field. Such as -

  • U.G and P.G of Pharmacy
  • Dental College
  • Clinical Research
  • Public Health

Moreover, Sri Ramachandra Medical College offers a diverse range of courses, including super specialty courses and postgraduate programs in various medical disciplines. All of the courses available in this medical college are taught by the experienced and knowledgable which makes it one of the Best Deemed Medical Colleges in India.

When we talk about the number of seats available for the MBBS at Sri Ramachandra Medical College is 250.

Fees - Rs. 25,00,000.

4. JSS Medical College

JSS Medical College is a famous college that is also known as Jagadguru Sri Shivarathreeshwara Medical College, and it was established in 1984. This medical college is located in Mysore, Karnataka, and is recognized by the Medical Council of India offering both UG and PG medical courses. 

Overview

The outstanding contributions of this medical college in providing exceptional healthcare services and affordable medical education have earned it a well-deserved reputation as one of the top 10 deemed medical colleges in India. JSS Medical College was initially affiliated with the University of Mysore from 1984 to 1995. Later, from 1995 to 2008, it was affiliated with the Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences, Bangalore. 

The college's affiliations with the University of Mysore and later with the Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences demonstrate its dedication to maintaining high academic standards and providing quality education. 

There is a reason for this medical college of being on the list of Best Deemed Medical Colleges in India as it has achieved an impressive ranking of 93rd in the Clinical and Health category of the World University Rankings 2022 by THE (Times Higher Education Rankings). 

Infrastructure 

This medical college is situated on a beautiful, sprawling campus of 43 acres, surrounded by lush greenery. The college has been constructed with great attention to aesthetic design, resulting in an impressive facility.

Courses 

JSS provides various courses available in this medical college and they are -

  • MBBS and the number of seats available in the JSS Medical College for MBBS is 200.
  • Postgraduate Degree (M.D. and M.S.) in 22 different medical fields.
  • 4 Speciality courses

Hospital

The primary care health facility started in 1986 with a 60 bedded hospital, as a requirement for the teaching hospital, then later it upgraded Hospital to a 1200 bedded hospital. 

To cater to the increasing healthcare need of the people of the Mysore and surrounding population it laid the foundation of the new hospital with 1800 beds, also an emergency facility that is big and has 260 beds. 

The hospital is located in an area of 12.5 acres and provides service in the 37 super specialties and has 55 special clinics. 

  • Outpatient is of 16000
  • 3500 inpatients

Fees  - Rs. 18,50,000.

 

5. Amrita Medical College

Amrita Medical College is home to some reputed faculty and renowned health facility centers in Andhra Pradesh. Established in the year 1998, now it is one of the leading deemed colleges in India. 

Overview

Situated in Kochi, this medical college, commonly referred to as Amrita Vishwa VidyaPeetham, is committed to delivering quality education and maintaining excellent infrastructure. It is affiliated with Saveetha University in Kochi and holds recognition from the NMC (National Medical Commission). Amrita Medical College Andhra Pradesh is definitely the best among the Top 10 Deemed Medical Colleges in India.

The medical college campus is designed with spacious and roomy interiors, comprising three blocks spread across 400 acres of campus. The campus of Amrita Medical College is well-planned, encompassing various infrastructure facilities such as academic buildings, an administration wing, a central library, hostels, a guest house, staff quarters, and playgrounds. 

Undoubtedly, Amrita Medical College has garnered its well-deserved reputation as one of the Premier Deemed Medical Colleges in India due to its extensive range of top-notch facilities.

Hospital

For the past decade, Amrita Medical College has been providing comprehensive healthcare services to the people of Andhra Pradesh as a tertiary care health facility, demonstrating its commitment to nurturing and improving the well-being of the community. Amrita Medical has medical colleges located in Kochi and Faridabad.  

Amrita School of Medicine, founded in 2002, has gained recognition and praise for its exceptional medical services. As a result, it has emerged as one of the best deemed medical colleges in India.

The present School tower of Amrita School of Medicine boasts 12 stories, covering a total floor area of 120,000 sq. ft. Furthermore, the hospital associated with the school encompasses an additional floor area of 1,100,000 sq. ft.

Facilities -

The availability of exceptional facilities at Amrita Medical College has undoubtedly positioned it as one of the Top Deemed Medical Colleges in India.

All facilities in this medical college have been put accordingly to the latest medical requirements, along with this college prioritizes the well-being and needs of the students, providing them with an optimal educational experience.

8. DR. D. Y. Patil Medical College, Hospital and Research Centre

DR. D. Y. Patil Medical College was established in the year 1996, at Pimpri. Pune. This medical college has the approval of the Ministry of Education and has been designated as Top Deemed Medical College in India. 

This medical college receives the approval of the Medical Council of India (MCI) and the government of India. Moreover, this is the third private medical college in India recognized by MCI with 250 seats.

The educational institution offers undergraduate (UG), postgraduate (PG), and super-specialty courses. Among the available options, there are a total of 20 different courses specifically offered for postgraduate (PG) students.

Overview

According to the recent ranking by the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF), Dr. D.Y Patil Medical College in Pune has been ranked 17th among the best medical colleges in India. Additionally, it holds the status of being the top-ranked medical college in the state of Maharashtra.

Infrastructure

The infrastructure of this medical college is truly magnificent. The college building of D.Y Patil Medical College in Pune has been meticulously designed and constructed, showcasing remarkable attention to detail. 

The faculty at D.Y Patil Medical College, Pune, has demonstrated exceptional dedication and effort in providing quality education. The institute boasts world-class facilities, including spacious air-conditioned lecture theatres, well-equipped museums, practical laboratories, and a well-stocked library.

Hospital

Dr. D.Y Patil Medical College is one of the biggest super specialty Hospitals with ultra-modern state-of-the-art infrastructure, consisting of more than 2011 beds that are dedicated to various specialties. It is NABH accredited hospital and has NABL accredited molecular testing laboratory. 

Courses

In its initial stage, the Institute began with the MBBS (undergraduate) program, offering 100 seats to students. However, over time, the Institute has significantly expanded its offerings. 

Currently, it provides 250 seats for the MBBS program, demonstrating substantial growth. Furthermore, the Institute now offers 218 seats for MD and MS (postgraduate) programs, and 22 seats for M.Ch./D.M. (super-specialty) programs, and Ph.D. programs in all medical specialties. Additionally, there are fellowship programs available in 31 subjects and certificate courses offered in 19 subjects. 

Fees - Rs. 20,60,000.

10. Bharti Vidyapeeth Medical College, Pune

The Bharti Vidyapeeth Medical College is one of the best-Deemed medical colleges in India, this medical college was established in 1946 to give students, a perfect college to pursue MBBS in Maharashtra. 

Overview

With more than 324 programs across 8 campuses across India, Bharti Vidyapeeth Medical College is the largest medical college in the country. As of 2005, there were 100 enrollments per year at the Bharti Vidyapeeth Medical College. But in the present time, the total number of MBBS seats in Bharti Vidyapeeth Medical College is 150.

But, we will talk about the Bharti Vidyapeeth Medical College, Sangli. This is a UG/PG/ Tertiary care center taking care of the population belonging to Western Maharashtra.

Hospital 

The hospital at Bharti Vidyapeeth Medical College, Sangli, boasts a total of 780 beds, including 51 beds in the adult ICU, 10 beds in the PICU (Pediatric Intensive Care Unit), and 12 beds in the NICU (Neonatal Intensive Care Unit). These ICUs are equipped with state-of-the-art equipment and are staffed by dedicated and skilled healthcare professionals.

Infrastructure

The college is in a multi-story building spanning an expansive area of 2.5 lakh square feet. The building is well-ventilated and spacious, providing a conducive learning environment. The multitude of facilities and features offered at Bharti Vidyapeeth Medical College, Sangli, undoubtedly contribute to its recognition as one of the best-deemed medical colleges in India. 

Fees - Rs. 23,46,845.
