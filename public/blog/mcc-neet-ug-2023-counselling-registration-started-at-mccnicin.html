MCC NEET UG 2023 Counselling Registration Started at mcc.nic.in

Team getmyuniversity 21 Jul 2023 1217 views

Medical Counselling Committee (MCC) NEET UG 2023 Counselling: Registration for NEET UG 2023 Counselling has begun as of today, July 20, 2023. For the 15% AIQ seats in medical institutions that offer admission to MBBS, BDS, and other UG medical courses, applicants who passed NEET 2023 and verified the minimal cut-off scores must apply online at mcc.nic.in.

MCC NEET UG Counselling Dates (Round1)

Particulars  Dates
Release of Seat Matrix 20th July 2023 to 21st July 2023
NEET UG 2023 counselling registration  20th July 2023, to 25th July 2023 (up to12:00 NOON) only as per Server Time
Payment facility will be available up to 08:00 PM of 25th July 2023 as per Server Time
Round 1 NEET 2023 counselling 22nd July 2023 to 26th July 2023 (up to 11:55 P.M of 26th July 2023) only as per Server Time
Choice Locking will be available from  03:00 P.M of 26th July 2023 up to 11:55 P.M of 26th July 2023 as per Server Time
Processing of Seat Allotment   27th July 2023 to 28th July 2023
Round 1 Provisional Seat Allotment Result  29th July, 2023
Uploading of documents by the candidates on MCC portal   30th July 2023
Reporting/ Joining 31st July 2023 to 4th Aug 2023
Verification of Joined candidates Data by institutes Sharing of data by MCC  5th Aug 2023 to 6th Aug 2023

 

MCC NEET UG Counselling Registration Process    

The processes listed below should be followed in order to submit an application on the official website for candidates who have completed the National Eligibility Cumulative Entrance Test (NEET) UG 2023:

Step 1 – Visit the official website, mcc.nic.in.

Step 2 – Select the 'New Registration NEET UG 2023 Counselling' option on the homepage.

Step 3 – Give the required information, such as your NEET application number and UG roll number, in the newly generated tab.

Step 4 – Re-enter your NEET UG 2023 roll number.

Step 5 – Complete the NEET UG 2023 counselling form and submit the payment.

Each round of therapy at MCC is conducted separately. There are four rounds, according to last year: Round 1, Round 2, Mop Up Round, and Stray Vacancy Round. Candidates will be allowed to apply once the round starts.
