1. JEE MAIN (Engineering Exam)

JEE Main is applicable for admission to NITs, IIITs, and CFTIs participating through the Central Seat Allocation Board with the proviso that the applicant must have obtained at least 75% of the possible points in the 12th-grade exam or be in the top 20 percentile in the 12th-grade examination held by the relevant Boards. The cutoff score for SC/ST candidates in the 12th-grade test is 65%.

The following subject combinations are necessary for admission to B.E./B.Tech. & B. Arch./B. Planning Courses at NITs, IIITs, and other CFTIs:-

Course Criteria based on 12th/equivalent exam
B.E/B.TECH Clear the qualifying exam with physics, maths and one of chemistry, biotechnology, biology, or a technical vocational subject as required topics.
B.ARCH Clear qualifying exam with mathematics, physics, chemistry
B.PLANNING Clear qualifying exam with mathematics

2. NEET (Medical Exam)

The National Eligibility Cum Entrance Test (UG) is applicable for admission to MBBS/BDS Courses in India at Medical/Dental Colleges run with the approval of the Medical Council of India/Dental Council of India under the Union Ministry of Health and Family Welfare.

The Directorate General of Health Services, Government of India, will use the NTA's All India Rank to conduct counseling for 15% All India Quota Seats and to provide the results to state/other Counselling Authorities. The NTA's responsibility will be limited to conducting the entrance examination and declaring the results.

3. CMAT (Management Exam)

The Common Management Admission Test (CMAT) is an entrance exam administered at the national level for management programmes. The CMAT is a three-hour computer-based online test that is given in a single sitting and is designed to assess a candidate's proficiency in a variety of areas, including quantitative technique, logical reasoning, language comprehension, and general awareness.

The CMAT score is accepted by all AICTE-Approved Institutions/University Departments/Constituent Colleges/Affiliated Colleges

4. GPAT (Pharmacy Exam)

An entrance test for M.Pharm programmes at the national level is known as the GRADUATE PHARMACY APTITUDE TEST (GPAT). This exam makes it easier for universities to choose qualified pharmacy graduates for admission to the Master of Pharmacy (M.Pharm) program. 

The GPAT is a three-hour online computer test that is administered in a single session. All institutions, university departments, constituent colleges, and affiliated colleges that have received AICTE approval will accept the GPAT score. On the basis of the GPAT result, some scholarships and other forms of financial aid are also awarded in the field of pharmacy.

5. NET (College/University Teaching & Fellowship Exam)

The National Eligibility Test (NET), administered on behalf of UGC, is used to determine whether Indian citizens are eligible for junior research fellowships and assistant professor eligibility at Indian universities and colleges.

The candidate's performance in the combined total of both NET papers will determine whether they are awarded JRF and/or eligibility for assistant professorship. However, individuals who only meet the requirements for assistant professorships are not to be given consideration for the JRF prize. Candidates who pass the test to determine their eligibility as assistant professors are subject to the hiring policies of the relevant universities, colleges, or state governments, as applicable.

Our FAQ's

Is JEE 2024 syllabus reduced?

There is no official update released on the reduction of JEE MAIN Syllabus 2024 and so the JEE MAIN pattern is similar to the previous year. There are a total of 64 chapters in JEE Mains syllabus - 16 in Math, 20 in Physics and 28 in Chemistry.

What is the 75% criteria for JEE 2024?

Candidates must pass the Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) to be considered for admission to the Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs). A minimum combined score of 75% on the Class 12 board exam or ranking in the top 20% of the relevant board are requirements for JEE eligibility.

Is NEET 2024 application form released?

In the first week of March 2024, the application for the NEET Exam 2024 is anticipated to be released. The NEET 2024 Registration form can be downloaded by candidates from the NTA's official website. The full application procedure will be completed online.

How to apply for NEET 2024?

Students who wish to apply for NEET 2024 must do so by visiting the official website at neet.nta.nic.in and fill the application form 2024.

What is the official website of NTA JEE 2024?

The official website of NTA JEE 2024 is jeemain.nta.nic.in.

Is JEE 2024 syllabus released?

Not yet, JEE Syllabus 2024 has not been released yet. We shall keep you updated when it will be released. NTA will release the JEE MAIN Syllabus 2024 PDF on the official website at jeemain.nta.nic.in.

What is the date of JEE MAIN 2024?

The JEE Main 2024 test will be administered by the National Testing Agency (NTA) in two sessions in January and April 2024. The JEE Main rankings are used to determine admission to IITs, NITs, and CFTIs. Candidates for the JEE can show up for both sessions.

Is JEE 2024 online or offline?

JEE MAIN 2024 will be conducted in online computer-based test mode. There will be 2 papers in JEE Main – Paper I for B.E/ B.Tech and Paper II for B.Arch & B.Plan.

Who is eligible for JEE 2024?

To be eligible for JEE 2024, there is no age limit. However, it is important for every candidate to pass 10+2 or equivalent examinations from a recognized board. Only those candidates will be eligible for JEE Main 2024 who passed the 10+2 or equivalent exam in 2022, 2023, or who are sitting for it in 2024.

Who conducted the NEET 2024 exam?

The National Testing Agency (NTA) is the authority responsible for conducting the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET 2024) for India’s medical, dental, AYUSH and nursing courses.
