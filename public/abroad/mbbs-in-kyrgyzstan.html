Countries like Russia, Bangladesh, and Nepal have emerged as preferred destinations for students seeking a quality medical education. If we talk about the preferred destination then Kyrgyzstan is another country that has attracted MBBS aspirants in recent years. 

Kyrgyzstan is on the wishlist of many Indian medical aspirants because of the quality of its education and affordable fee structure.  

Students choose to study MBBS in Kyrgyzstan because it offers low fees for the MBBS. Besides, studying in Kyrgyzstan gives students the opportunity to experience a new country and culture. The total cost for MBBS is below Rs. 25 lakhs including the fee and hostel also Kyrgyzstan is one of the few foreign countries that provide MBBS at a very economical fee structure. The fee structure depends from college to college. 

It is important to note that there are over 20 colleges in Kyrgyzstan that offer MBBS courses, but not all of them are recognized by the National Medical Commission (NMC) of India. Therefore, before considering any college to pursue medical education, Indian students should make sure to verify its approval status on the NMC website.

The total duration for the MBBS program is 6 years and there is a total of 5 MCI-recognized medical colleges in Kyrgyzstan. Any top medical college in Kyrgyzstan would guarantee students a remarkable future.  An MBBS degree from Kyrgyzstan is recognized globally and holds the same value as a degree in medicine from Russia. 

However, it is important for you to study at the top-ranked college for MBBS in Kyrgyzstan. The State Medical Academy in Kyrgyzstan is not only the oldest but also the most prestigious and renowned university in the country.

Indian students will feel at home in Kyrgyzstan because its climate is the same cold as northern cities in India. There are many prominent MBBS colleges in Kyrgyzstan available in big numbers, one can opt to do MBBS.

Currently, more than 10,000 Indian students are enrolled in medical universities for MBBS. But, we have seen a decline in the number of aspirants who graduated from Kyrgyzstan clearing the NEXT or FMGE exam. This is also an issue that needs to be addressed. 

Yet, Kyrgyzstan has a top-notch medical education system make student enable with the necessary skills and knowledge required to start practicing medicine in any country around the world. The medical colleges in the nation are set up to help you to become a prominent medical professional. 

Kyrgyzstan has innovative teaching methods to equip students with the skills and knowledge necessary to excel in medicine. 

About Kyrgyzstan 

Kyrgyzstan, located in Central Asia, is surrounded by several developed and amicable countries. The country is home to stunning mountain ranges, beautiful lakes, and ancient Silk Road cities. Kyrgyzstan has a rich and long history of tradition. The people of this country are very warm and friendly. In earlier days, Kyrgyzstan was an integral part of the former USSR, later it split from Russia in 1991. 

The main religion of Kyrgyzstan is Islam, there are around 83% of the population follows the Islam religion. The people of Kyrgyzstan follow Russian culture. Since Russians and Indians are friendly with each other and have a strong relationship, Kyrgyzstan also keeps the same empathy towards India and its students.

Why

MBBS in Kyrgyzstan is an excellent option for Indian students for many reasons such as:

  • The Fee structure in Kyrgyzstan for MBBS is affordable, the fee structure lies under INR 25 lakhs.
  • The Medical degree of Kyrgyzstan is recognized by world health leading organizations and also by MCI.
  • Kyrgyzstan currency som is equal to 0.94 compared to the Indian 1 rupee.
  • Kyrgyzstan is very safe in terms of student safety, there is a foreign student association to help foreign students. There haven't been any violent incidents involving students. 
  • The ratio of teacher to student is 1: 15 in the country. 
  • The medical education curriculum in Kyrgyzstan provides a pragmatic learning experience by combining theoretical studies with practical applications. This balance between theory and practice allows for a comprehensive and well-rounded education.
  • Every college has an Indian diaspora, so you will not face any problems settling in.
  • Some colleges in Kyrgyzstan offer part-time jobs to their students, enabling them to earn while they complete their studies. This can be particularly beneficial for students who may require financial support to cover their living expenses while studying abroad.

Admission Process

Eligibility criteria for MBBS in Kyrgyzstan 

  • The minimum age of the student must be 17 years and the maximum age should not exceed 25 years of age.
  • You should have scored at least 55% in the 12th class exam, with science steam.
  • Qualifying for NEET is mandatory. 
  • The entrance exam is not a requirement to do MBBS in Kyrgyzstan.
  • Test of any foreign language is not mandatory for admission. 

Admission process for MBBS in Kyrgyzstan 

  1. Apply on the Kyrgyzstan medical university portal. At, the first aspirant has to fill out the admission form carefully, without any mistakes.
  2. Then, in the second step aspirant need to submit their scanned copies of passports for MBBS/MD in Kyrgyzstan. All required documents must be submitted through the college's online portal.
  3. After submitting the documents, students can expect to receive the admission letter from the university within 10 working days.
  4. Once the admission letter is received, students can apply for their student visa to study MBBS in Kyrgyzstan.
  5. After receiving the visa and invitation letter from the university, students must contact the Kyrgyzstan Embassy to complete the necessary formalities.
  6. Finally, once all the formalities are complete, students can make travel arrangements and catch a flight to Kyrgyzstan to begin their MBBS program.

Aspirants seeking MBBS admission in Kyrgyzstan should keep ready all the following documents -:

  • Copy of passport
  • Copy of marksheet
  • Copy of invitation letter
  • Birth certificate
  • Medical certificate
  • 10 passport size photos
  • HIV/AIDS test report
  • Medical test

Our FAQ's

1. Is Kyrgyzstan good for MBBS?

Kyrgyzstan is home to some of the top medical universities recognized by the NMC and many students choose Kyrgyzstan because of its affordability, and assurance of security. 2. How many years for MBBS in Kyrgyzstan including internship?

The total duration of MBBS is 6 years, including a 1-year internship.
